8.11 The Surprising Sufficiency of Projective Algebraic Geometry

射影代数几何的惊人充分性

Weil's abstract definition of a variety (affine algebraic sets glued together along isomorphic/ˌaɪ.səˈmɔː.fɪk/ 同构的 open sets) was referred to briefly in (0.4), and is quite easy to handle in terms of sheaves. Given this, the idea of working only with varieties embedded in a fixed ambient/ˈæm.bi.ənt/ 环绕的，周围的 space $\mathbb{P}^N_k$ seems at first sight unduly restrictive/rɪˈstrɪk.tɪv/ 限制性的. I want to describe briefly the modern point of view on this question.

Weil对簇的抽象定义（沿同构开集粘合仿射代数集）在 (0.4) 中已简要提及，用层的语言来处理相当容易。在此基础上，仅研究嵌入在固定环绕空间 $\mathbb{P}^N_k$ 中的簇这一想法，乍看之下似乎过于限制。我想简要描述一下对这个问题的现代观点。

(a) Polarisation and positivity

(a) 极化与正性

Firstly, varieties are usually considered up to isomorphism, so saying a variety $X$ is projective means that $X$ can be embedded in some $\mathbb{P}^N$, that is, is isomorphic to a closed subvariety $X \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ as in (5.1--7). Quasiprojective means isomorphic to a locally closed subvariety of $\mathbb{P}^N$, so an open dense subset of a projective variety; projectivity/prəˌdʒek.ˈtɪv.ɪ.ti/ 射影性 includes the property of completeness, that $X$ cannot be embedded as a dense open set of any bigger variety.

首先，簇通常考虑到同构为止，因此说一个簇 $X$ 是射影的意味着 $X$ 可以嵌入某个 $\mathbb{P}^N$，即同构于 (5.1--7) 中那样的闭子簇 $X \subset \mathbb{P}^N$。拟射影意味着同构于 $\mathbb{P}^N$ 的一个局部闭子簇，即射影簇的一个稠密开子集；射影性包含完备性——即 $X$ 不能作为任何更大簇的稠密开集嵌入。

The choice of an actual embedding $X \hookrightarrow \mathbb{P}^N$ (or of a very ample line bundle $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$ whose sections will be the homogeneous coordinates of $\mathbb{P}^N$) is often called a polarisation/ˌpəʊ.lər.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ 极化, and we write $(X, \mathcal{O}_X(1))$ to indicate that the choice has been made. In addition to completeness, a projective variety $X \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ satisfies a key condition of 'positive degree': if $V \subset X$ is a $k$-dimensional subvariety then $V$ intersects a general linear subspace $\mathbb{P}^{N-k}$ in a positive finite number of points. Conversely, the Kleiman criterion/kraɪˈtɪə.ri.ən/ 准则 says that some multiple of a line bundle on a complete variety $X$ can be used to provide a projective embedding of $X$ if its degree on every curve $C \subset X$ is consistently greater than zero (that is, $\ge \varepsilon \cdot (\text{any reasonable measure of } C)$). This kind of positivity relates closely to the choice of a Kähler metric on a complex manifold (a Riemannian metric with the right kind of compatibility with the complex structure). So we understand projectivity as a kind of 'positive definiteness'.

选择一个实际的嵌入 $X \hookrightarrow \mathbb{P}^N$（或选择一个极丰线丛 $\mathcal{O}_X(1)$，其截面将成为 $\mathbb{P}^N$ 的齐次坐标）常被称为极化，我们用 $(X, \mathcal{O}_X(1))$ 表示已做出了这一选择。除了完备性之外，射影簇 $X \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ 还满足一个关键的"正次数"条件：如果 $V \subset X$ 是 $k$ 维子簇，则 $V$ 与一般的线性子空间 $\mathbb{P}^{N-k}$ 交于正的有限个点。反过来，Kleiman准则说，如果完备簇 $X$ 上一个线丛的某个倍数在 $X$ 的每条曲线 $C \subset X$ 上的次数一致大于零（即 $\ge \varepsilon \cdot (C \text{ 的任何合理度量})$），那么它可以给出 $X$ 的射影嵌入。这种正性与复流形上Kähler度量（一种与复结构有恰当相容性的Riemann度量）的选择密切相关。因此我们将射影性理解为一种"正定性"。

(b) Sufficiency

(b) 充分性

The surprising thing is the many problems of algebraic geometry having answers within the framework of projective varieties. The construction of Chow varieties mentioned in (8.2) is one such example; another is Mumford's work of the 1960s, in which he constructed Picard varieties and many moduli spaces as quasiprojective/ˌkweɪ.zi.prəˈdʒek.tɪv/ 拟射影的 varieties (schemes). Mori theory (responsible for important conceptual advances in classification of varieties related to rationality, see [Kollár]) is the most recent example; here the ideas and techniques are inescapably/ˌɪn.ɪˈskeɪ.pə.bli/ 不可避免地 projective in nature.

令人惊讶的是，代数几何的许多问题都能在射影簇的框架内找到答案。(8.2) 中提到的Chow簇的构造就是一个例子；另一个是Mumford在1960年代的工作，他将Picard簇和许多模空间构造为拟射影簇（概形）。Mori理论（它在与有理性相关的簇分类方面做出了重要的概念推进，参见 [Kollár]）是最近的例子；在这里，思想和技术本质上不可避免地是射影性的。

(c) Insufficiency of abstract varieties

(c) 抽象簇的不充分性

Curves and nonsingular surfaces are automatically quasiprojective; but abstract varieties that are not quasiprojective do exist (singular surfaces, or nonsingular varieties of dimension $\ge 3$). However, if you feel the need for these constructions, you will almost certainly also want Moishezon varieties (M. Artin's algebraic spaces), objects of algebraic geometry more general than abstract varieties, obtained by a somewhat more liberal/ˈlɪb.ər.əl/ 自由的，宽泛的 interpretation of 'glueing local pieces'.

曲线和非奇异曲面自动是拟射影的；但确实存在不是拟射影的抽象簇（奇异曲面，或维数 $\ge 3$ 的非奇异簇）。然而，如果你感到需要这类构造，你几乎肯定也会需要Moishezon簇（M. Artin的代数空间），这是比抽象簇更一般的代数几何对象，通过对"粘合局部片段"稍微更自由的诠释而得到。

Theorems on abstract varieties are often proved by a reduction to the quasiprojective case, so whether the quasiprojective proof or the detail of the reduction process is more useful, interesting, essential or likely-to-lead-to-cheap-publishable-work will depend on the particular problem and the individual student's interests and employment situation. It has recently been proved that a nonsingular abstract variety or Moishezon variety that is not quasiprojective necessarily contains a rational curve; however, the proof (due to J. Kollár) is Mori theoretic, so hardcore projective algebraic geometry.

关于抽象簇的定理常常通过归约到拟射影情形来证明，因此是拟射影的证明还是归约过程的细节更有用、更有趣、更本质或更容易发表，将取决于具体问题以及每个学生的兴趣和就业状况。最近已证明，一个不是拟射影的非奇异抽象簇或Moishezon簇必然包含一条有理曲线；然而，这个证明（归功于J. Kollár）属于Mori理论，也就是说是硬核的射影代数几何。
