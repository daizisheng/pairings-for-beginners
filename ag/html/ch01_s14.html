1.14 Worked example

详解例题

Let $P_1, \ldots, P_4$ be 4 points of $\mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{R}}$ such that no 3 are collinear; then the pencil of conics $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ through $P_1, \ldots, P_4$ has 3 degenerate elements, namely the line pairs $L_{12} + L_{34}$, $L_{13} + L_{24}$, $L_{14} + L_{23}$, where $L_{ij}$ is the line through $P_i, P_j$.

设 $P_1, \ldots, P_4$ 是 $\mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{R}}$ 中没有3点共线的4个点；则过 $P_1, \ldots, P_4$ 的二次曲线线性系 $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ 有3个退化元素，即直线对 $L_{12} + L_{34}$、$L_{13} + L_{24}$、$L_{14} + L_{23}$，其中 $L_{ij}$ 是过 $P_i, P_j$ 的直线。

Next, suppose that I start from the pencil of conics generated by $Q_1 = Y^2 + rY + sX + t$ and $Q_2 = Y - X^2$, and try to find the points $P_1, \ldots, P_4$ of intersection.

接下来，假设从由 $Q_1 = Y^2 + rY + sX + t$ 和 $Q_2 = Y - X^2$ 生成的二次曲线线性系出发，试图求出交点 $P_1, \ldots, P_4$。

This can be done as follows: (1) find the 3 ratios $(\lambda : \mu)$ for which $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ are degenerate conics. Using what has been said above, this just means that I have to find the 3 roots of the cubic/ˈkjuː.bɪk/ 三次的

$$F(\lambda, \mu) = \det\left|\lambda \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 0 & s/2 \\ 0 & 1 & r/2 \\ s/2 & r/2 & t \end{pmatrix} + \mu \begin{pmatrix} -1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 1/2 \\ 0 & 1/2 & 0 \end{pmatrix}\right|$$ $$= -\frac{1}{4}(s^2\lambda^3 + (4t - r^2)\lambda^2\mu - 2r\lambda\mu^2 - \mu^3).$$

(2) Separate out 2 of the degenerate conics into pairs of lines (this involves solving 2 quadratic equations). (3) The 4 points $P_i$ are the points of intersection of the lines.

具体做法是：(1) 找出使 $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ 退化的3个比值 $(\lambda : \mu)$，即求上述三次式的3个根： $$F(\lambda, \mu) = -\frac{1}{4}(s^2\lambda^3 + (4t - r^2)\lambda^2\mu - 2r\lambda\mu^2 - \mu^3).$$ (2) 将其中2条退化二次曲线分解为直线对（这需要解2个二次方程）。(3) 4个点 $P_i$ 就是这些直线的交点。

This procedure/prəˈsiː.dʒər/ 步骤，过程 gives a geometric interpretation of the reduction of the general quartic/ˈkwɔː.tɪk/ 四次的 in Galois theory (see for example [van der Waerden, Algebra, Ch. 8, §64]): let $k$ be a field, and $f(X) = X^4 + rX^2 + sX + t \in k[X]$ a quartic polynomial. Then the two parabolas/pəˈræb.əl.əz/ 抛物线 $C_1$ and $C_2$ meet in the 4 points $P_i = (a_i, a_i^2)$ for $i = 1, \ldots, 4$, where the $a_i$ are the 4 roots of $f$.

这一过程给出了 Galois 理论中一般四次方程化简的几何解释（例见 [van der Waerden, Algebra, Ch. 8, §64]）：设 $k$ 是一个域，$f(X) = X^4 + rX^2 + sX + t \in k[X]$ 是一个四次多项式。则两条抛物线 $C_1$ 和 $C_2$ 交于4个点 $P_i = (a_i, a_i^2)$（$i = 1, \ldots, 4$），其中 $a_i$ 是 $f$ 的4个根。

Then the line $L_{ij} = P_iP_j$ is given by

$$L_{ij}: (Y = (a_i + a_j)X - a_ia_j),$$

and the reducible/rɪˈdjuː.sɪ.bl/ 可约的 conic $L_{12} + L_{34}$ is given by

$$Y^2 + (a_1a_2 + a_3a_4)Y + (a_1 + a_2)(a_3 + a_4)X^2 + sX + t = 0,$$

that is, by $Q_1 - (a_1 + a_2)(a_3 + a_4)Q_2 = 0$. Hence the 3 values of $\mu/\lambda$ for which the conic $\lambda Q_1 + \mu Q_2$ breaks up as a line pair are

$$-(a_1 + a_2)(a_3 + a_4), \quad -(a_1 + a_3)(a_2 + a_4), \quad -(a_1 + a_4)(a_2 + a_3).$$
直线 $L_{ij} = P_iP_j$ 由 $L_{ij}: (Y = (a_i + a_j)X - a_ia_j)$ 给出，而可约二次曲线 $L_{12} + L_{34}$ 为 $$Y^2 + (a_1a_2 + a_3a_4)Y + (a_1 + a_2)(a_3 + a_4)X^2 + sX + t = 0,$$ 即 $Q_1 - (a_1 + a_2)(a_3 + a_4)Q_2 = 0$。因此使 $\lambda Q_1 + \mu Q_2$ 分裂为直线对的3个 $\mu/\lambda$ 值分别是 $$-(a_1 + a_2)(a_3 + a_4), \quad -(a_1 + a_3)(a_2 + a_4), \quad -(a_1 + a_4)(a_2 + a_3).$$

The cubic equation whose roots are these 3 quantities is called the auxiliary/ɔːɡˈzɪl.jər.i/ 辅助的 cubic associated with the quartic; it can be calculated using the theory of elementary/ˌel.ɪˈmen.tər.i/ 初等的 symmetric functions; this is a fairly laborious/ləˈbɔː.ri.əs/ 费力的 procedure. On the other hand, the geometric method sketched above gives an elegant/ˈel.ɪ.ɡənt/ 优美的 derivation of the auxiliary cubic which only involves evaluating a $3 \times 3$ determinant.

The above treatment is taken from [M. Berger, 16.4.10 and 16.4.11.1].

以这3个量为根的三次方程称为与该四次方程关联的辅助三次方程（auxiliary cubic）；它可以用初等对称函数的理论来计算，但过程相当繁琐。另一方面，上面勾画的几何方法给出了辅助三次方程的一个优美推导，只需要计算一个 $3 \times 3$ 行列式。 以上内容取自 [M. Berger, 16.4.10 和 16.4.11.1]。
