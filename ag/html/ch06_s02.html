6.2 Remarks

6.2 注记

(a) The derivatives/dɪˈrɪv.ə.tɪvz/ 导数 $\partial f / \partial X_i(P)$ appearing above are formal algebraic operations (that is, $\partial / \partial X_i$ takes $X_i^n$ into $nX_i^{n-1}$); no calculus is involved.

(a) 上面出现的导数 $\partial f / \partial X_i(P)$ 是形式的代数运算（即 $\partial / \partial X_i$ 把 $X_i^n$ 映为 $nX_i^{n-1}$）；这里不涉及任何微积分。

(b) Suppose $k = \mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$, and that $\partial f / \partial X_i(P) \neq 0$; for clarity let me take $i = 1$. Then the map $p\colon \mathbb{A}^n \to \mathbb{A}^n$ defined by $(X_1, \ldots, X_n) \mapsto (f, X_2, \ldots, X_n)$ has nonvanishing/nɒnˈvæn.ɪ.ʃɪŋ/ 不消失的 Jacobian/dʒəˈkoʊ.bi.ən/ 雅可比（行列式） determinant at $P$, so that by the inverse function theorem, there exists a neighbourhood $P \in U \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ such that $p|_U \colon U \to p(U) \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ is a diffeomorphism/ˌdɪf.i.əˈmɔː.fɪ.zəm/ 微分同胚 of the neighbourhood $U$ with an open set $p(U)$ of $\mathbb{A}^n$ (in the usual topology of $\mathbb{R}^n$ or $\mathbb{C}^n$); that is, $p|_U$ is bijective/baɪˈdʒek.tɪv/ 双射的, and both $p$ and $p^{-1}$ are differentiable functions of real or complex variables. In other words, $(f, X_2, \ldots, X_n)$ form a new differentiable coordinate system on $\mathbb{A}^n$ near $P$; this implies that a neighbourhood of $P$ in $V : (f = 0)$ is diffeomorphic to an open set in $\mathbb{A}^{n-1}$ with coordinates $(X_2, \ldots, X_n)$. Thus near a nonsingular point $P$, $V$ is a manifold/ˈmæn.ɪ.fəʊld/ 流形 with $(X_2, \ldots, X_n)$ as local parameters.

(b) 设 $k = \mathbb{R}$ 或 $\mathbb{C}$，且 $\partial f / \partial X_i(P) \neq 0$；为清楚起见取 $i=1$。则映射 $p\colon \mathbb{A}^n \to \mathbb{A}^n$，定义为 $(X_1, \ldots, X_n) \mapsto (f, X_2, \ldots, X_n)$，在 $P$ 处的雅可比行列式不为零。因此，由反函数定理，存在邻域 $P \in U \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 使得 $p|_U \colon U \to p(U) \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 是邻域 $U$ 与 $\mathbb{A}^n$ 的开集 $p(U)$ 之间的微分同胚（在 $\mathbb{R}^n$ 或 $\mathbb{C}^n$ 的通常拓扑下）；即 $p|_U$ 是双射，且 $p$ 和 $p^{-1}$ 都是实变量或复变量的可微函数。换言之，$(f, X_2, \ldots, X_n)$ 在 $P$ 附近构成 $\mathbb{A}^n$ 上的一个新的可微坐标系；这意味着 $P$ 在 $V : (f=0)$ 中的邻域微分同胚于以 $(X_2, \ldots, X_n)$ 为坐标的 $\mathbb{A}^{n-1}$ 的开集。因此在非奇异点 $P$ 附近，$V$ 是一个以 $(X_2, \ldots, X_n)$ 为局部参数的流形。
