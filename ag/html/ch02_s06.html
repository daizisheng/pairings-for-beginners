2.6 Proposition: cubics through 8 general points

2.6 命题：过8个一般点的三次曲线

Proposition 2.6   Let $k$ be an infinite field, and $P_1, \ldots, P_8 \in \mathbb{P}^2_k$ distinct points; suppose that no 4 of $P_1, \ldots, P_8$ are collinear/kəˈlɪn.i.ər/ 共线的, and no 7 of them lie on a nondegenerate conic; then

$$\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) = 2.$$
命题 2.6   设 $k$ 是无穷域，$P_1, \ldots, P_8 \in \mathbb{P}^2_k$ 是不同的点；假设 $P_1, \ldots, P_8$ 中没有 4 个共线，也没有 7 个在同一条非退化圆锥曲线上；则 $$\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) = 2.$$

Proof   For brevity, let me say that a set of points are conconic/kɒnˈkɒn.ɪk/ 共圆锥曲线的 if they all lie on a nondegenerate conic. The proof of (2.6) breaks up into several cases.

证明   为简洁起见，如果一组点都在同一条非退化圆锥曲线上，就说它们"共圆锥曲线"。(2.6) 的证明分成几种情形。

Main case   No 3 points are collinear, no 6 conconic. This is the 'general position' case.

Suppose for a contradiction that $\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) \geq 3$, and let $P_9, P_{10}$ be distinct points on the line $L = P_1 P_2$. Then

$$\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_{10}) \geq \dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) - 2 \geq 1,$$

so that there exists $0 \neq F \in S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_{10})$. By Corollary 2.5, $F = H \cdot Q$, with $Q \in S_2(P_3, \ldots, P_8)$. Now I have a contradiction to the case assumption: if $Q$ is nondegenerate then the 6 points $P_3, \ldots, P_8$ are conconic, whereas if $Q$ is a line pair or a double line, then at least 3 of them are collinear.

主要情形   没有 3 点共线，没有 6 点共圆锥曲线。这是"一般位置"的情形。
假设 $\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) \geq 3$ 以求矛盾，取直线 $L = P_1P_2$ 上的两个不同点 $P_9, P_{10}$。则 $$\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_{10}) \geq \dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) - 2 \geq 1，$$ 所以存在 $0 \neq F \in S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_{10})$。由推论 2.5，$F = H \cdot Q$，其中 $Q \in S_2(P_3, \ldots, P_8)$。这就产生了矛盾：如果 $Q$ 是非退化的，那么 6 个点 $P_3, \ldots, P_8$ 共圆锥曲线；如果 $Q$ 是一对直线或二重直线，则至少有 3 个点共线。

First degenerate/dɪˈdʒen.ər.ɪt/ 退化的 case   Suppose $P_1, P_2, P_3 \in L$ are collinear, and let $L : (H = 0)$. Let $P_9$ be a 4th point on the line $L$. Then by Corollary 2.5,

$$S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9) = H \cdot S_2(P_4, \ldots, P_8).$$

Also, since no 4 of $P_4, \ldots, P_8$ are collinear, by Corollary 1.11,

$$\dim S_2(P_4, \ldots, P_8) = 1, \quad \text{and then} \quad \dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9) = 1,$$

which implies $\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) \leq 2$.

第一退化情形   假设 $P_1, P_2, P_3 \in L$ 共线，$L : (H = 0)$。取 $L$ 上的第 4 个点 $P_9$。由推论 2.5， $$S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9) = H \cdot S_2(P_4, \ldots, P_8).$$ 又因为 $P_4, \ldots, P_8$ 中没有 4 个共线，由推论 1.11， $$\dim S_2(P_4, \ldots, P_8) = 1，\quad \text{从而} \quad \dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9) = 1，$$ 这就意味着 $\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) \leq 2$。

Second degenerate case   Suppose $P_1, \ldots, P_6 \in C$ are conconic, with $C : (Q = 0)$ a nondegenerate conic. Then choose $P_9 \in Q$ distinct from $P_1, \ldots, P_6$. By Corollary 2.5 again,

$$S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9) = Q \cdot S_1(P_7, P_8);$$

the line $L = P_7 P_8$ is unique, so that $S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9)$ is the 1-dimensional space spanned by $QL$, and hence $\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) \leq 2$.   Q.E.D.

第二退化情形   假设 $P_1, \ldots, P_6 \in C$ 共圆锥曲线，$C : (Q = 0)$ 是非退化圆锥曲线。取 $P_9 \in Q$ 且与 $P_1, \ldots, P_6$ 不同。再次由推论 2.5， $$S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9) = Q \cdot S_1(P_7, P_8)；$$ 直线 $L = P_7P_8$ 是唯一的，所以 $S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_9)$ 是由 $QL$ 张成的 1 维空间，因此 $\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) \leq 2$。   Q.E.D.
