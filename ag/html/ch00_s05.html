0.5 "Purely algebraically defined"

0.5 "纯代数定义的"

So much for the drawbacks of the algebraic approach to geometry. Having said this, almost all the algebraic varieties of importance in the world today are quasiprojective/ˌkweɪ.zaɪ.prəˈdʒek.tɪv/ 拟射影的, and we can have quite a lot of fun with varieties without worrying overmuch about the finer points of definition.

以上就是代数方法研究几何的各种不便之处。话虽如此，当今世界上几乎所有重要的代数簇都是拟射影的，我们完全可以愉快地和簇打交道，不用太过纠结于定义的细微之处。

The main advantages of algebraic geometry are that it is purely algebraically defined, and that it applies to any field, not just $\mathbb{R}$ or $\mathbb{C}$; we can do geometry over fields of characteristic/ˌkær.ək.təˈrɪs.tɪk/ 特征（域的特征） $p$. Don't say 'characteristic $p$ – big deal, that's just the finite fields'; to start with, very substantial/səbˈstæn.ʃəl/ 大量的，实质性的 parts of group theory are based on geometry over finite fields, as are large parts of combinatorics/ˌkɒm.bɪ.nəˈtɒr.ɪks/ 组合学 used in computer science. Next, there are lots of interesting fields of characteristic $p$ other than finite ones. Moreover, at a deep level, the finite fields are present and working inside $\mathbb{Q}$ and $\mathbb{C}$. Most of the deep results on arithmetic of varieties over $\mathbb{Q}$ use a considerable amount of geometry over $\mathbb{C}$ or over the finite fields and their algebraic closures.

代数几何的主要优势：它是纯代数定义的，因此适用于任何域，不仅仅是 $\mathbb{R}$ 或 $\mathbb{C}$——我们可以在特征 $p$ 的域上做几何。

别说"特征 $p$——那不就是有限域嘛，没什么了不起的"。首先，群论的很大一部分建立在有限域上的几何之上，计算机科学中使用的大量组合学也是如此。其次，除了有限域之外，还有很多有趣的特征 $p$ 的域。而且，在深层次上，有限域就存在并且在 $\mathbb{Q}$ 和 $\mathbb{C}$ 内部运作着。关于 $\mathbb{Q}$ 上簇的算术的大多数深刻结果，都需要用到大量 $\mathbb{C}$ 上的几何或有限域及其代数闭包上的几何。

This concludes the introduction; see the informal discussion in (2.15) and the final §8 for more general culture.

引言到此结束。更多一般性的讨论见 (2.15) 的非正式讨论和最后一章 §8。
