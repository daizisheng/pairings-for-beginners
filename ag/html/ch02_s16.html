2.16 Commercial break

2.16 广告时间

Complex curves (= compact Riemann surfaces) appear across a whole spectrum/ˈspek.trəm/ 范围，光谱 of math problems, from Diophantine/ˌdaɪ.əˈfæn.tiːn/ 丢番图的 arithmetic through complex function theory and low dimensional topology to differential equations of math physics. So go out and buy a complex curve today.

复曲线（= 紧黎曼面）出现在数学问题的各个领域，从丢番图算术到复函数论，从低维拓扑到数学物理中的微分方程。所以快去买一条复曲线吧。

To a quite extraordinary/ɪkˈstrɔːr.dɪ.nər.i/ 非凡的，惊人的 degree, the properties of a curve are determined by its genus, and more particularly by the trichotomy/traɪˈkɒt.ə.mi/ 三分法 $g = 0$, $g = 1$ or $g \geq 2$. Some of the more striking aspects of this are described in the table on the following page, and I give a brief discussion; this ought to be in the background culture of every mathematician.

曲线的性质在很大程度上由其亏格决定，更具体地说由三分法 $g = 0$、$g = 1$ 或 $g \geq 2$ 决定，这一点是相当惊人的。其中一些引人注目的方面在下面的表格中描述，我来简要讨论一下；这些应该是每个数学家都了解的背景知识。

To give a partial answer to the Diophantine question mentioned in (1.1–2) and again in (2.1), it is known that a curve can be parametrised/ˈpær.ə.mɪ.traɪzd/ 参数化的 by rational functions if and only if $g = 0$; if I'm working over a fixed field, a curve of genus 0 may have no $k$-valued points at all (for example, the conic in (1.2)), but if it has one point, it can be parametrised over $k$, so that its $k$-valued points are in bijection with $\mathbb{P}^1_k$.

作为(1.1–2)和(2.1)中提到的丢番图问题的部分回答：一条曲线可以用有理函数参数化当且仅当 $g = 0$；如果我们在固定的域上工作，亏格0的曲线可能根本没有 $k$-值点（例如(1.2)中的圆锥曲线），但如果有一个点，它就可以在 $k$ 上参数化，使得其 $k$-值点与 $\mathbb{P}^1_k$ 一一对应。

Any curve of genus 1 is isomorphic/ˌaɪ.soʊˈmɔːr.fɪk/ 同构的 to a cubic as in this section, and a group law is defined on the $k$-valued points (provided of course that there exists at least one – there's no such thing as the empty group); if $k$ is a number field (for example, $k = \mathbb{Q}$), the $k$-valued points form an Abelian group which is finitely generated/ˌfaɪ.naɪt.li ˈdʒen.ər.eɪ.tɪd/ 有限生成的 (the Mordell–Weil Theorem).

任何亏格1的曲线都同构于本节中讨论的三次曲线，在其 $k$-值点上可以定义群律（当然前提是至少有一个 $k$-值点——不存在空群这回事）；如果 $k$ 是数域（例如 $k = \mathbb{Q}$），$k$-值点构成一个有限生成的Abel群（Mordell–Weil定理）。

Whereas a curve of genus $g \geq 2$ is now known to have only a finite set of $k$-valued points; this is a famous theorem proved by Faltings in 1983, and for which he received the Fields medal in 1986. Thus for example, for any $n \geq 4$, the Fermat curve $x^n + y^n = 1$ has at most a finite number of rational points.

而亏格 $g \geq 2$ 的曲线现在已知只有有限多个 $k$-值点；这是Faltings在1983年证明的著名定理，他因此获得了1986年的菲尔兹奖。因此例如，对任意 $n \geq 4$，费马曲线 $x^n + y^n = 1$ 最多有有限个有理点。

Over $\mathbb{C}$, a curve $C$ of genus 1 is topologically a torus, and has a group law, so that it is analytically of the form $C \cong \mathbb{C}/(\mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z} \cdot \tau)$:

在 $\mathbb{C}$ 上，亏格1的曲线 $C$ 拓扑上是一个环面，并有群律，因此解析地可以写成 $C \cong \mathbb{C}/(\mathbb{Z} \oplus \mathbb{Z} \cdot \tau)$：

The isomorphism between this quotient and a plane curve $C_3 \subset \mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{C}}$ is given by a holomorphic/ˌhɒl.əˈmɔːr.fɪk/ 全纯的 map $\varphi \colon \mathbb{C} \to C_3$, that is, a kind of 'parametrisation' of $C_3$; but $\varphi$ cannot be in terms of rational functions (by (2.2)), and is infinity-to-one; this is the theory of doubly periodic functions of a complex variable, which was one of the mainstays of 19th century analysis (Weierstrass $\wp$-function, Riemann theta function).

这个商与平面曲线 $C_3 \subset \mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{C}}$ 之间的同构由全纯映射 $\varphi: \mathbb{C} \to C_3$ 给出，也就是 $C_3$ 的一种"参数化"；但 $\varphi$ 不可能用有理函数表达（由(2.2)），而且是无穷对一的；这就是复变函数中双周期函数的理论，是19世纪分析学的支柱之一（Weierstrass的 $\wp$ 函数，Riemann的theta函数）。

Another important thing to notice is that different periods $\tau$ will usually lead to different curves; they're all homeomorphic/ˌhoʊ.mi.əˈmɔːr.fɪk/ 同胚的 to the standard torus $S^1 \times S^1$, but as algebraic curves, or complex analytic curves, they're not isomorphic. The period $\tau$ is a modulus, that is, a complex parameter which governs variation of the complex structure $C$ on the fixed topological object $S^1 \times S^1$.

另一个重要的事情是，不同的周期 $\tau$ 通常会导致不同的曲线；它们全都同胚于标准环面 $S^1 \times S^1$，但作为代数曲线或复解析曲线，它们是不同构的。周期 $\tau$ 就是一个模（modulus），也就是一个复参数，它控制着固定拓扑对象 $S^1 \times S^1$ 上复结构 $C$ 的变化。

The student interested in more on curves should look at [D. Mumford, Curves and their Jacobians], the first part of which is fairly colloquial/kəˈloʊ.kwi.əl/ 通俗的，口语化的, or [Clemens].

对曲线有更多兴趣的同学可以参看 [D. Mumford, Curves and their Jacobians]，其第一部分写得相当通俗易懂，或者参看 [Clemens]。
