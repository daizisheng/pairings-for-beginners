4.3 Polynomial Maps

多项式映射

Let $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ and $W \subset \mathbb{A}^m$ be algebraic sets; write $X_1, \dots, X_n$ and $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$ for the coordinates on $\mathbb{A}^n$ and $\mathbb{A}^m$ respectively.

设 $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 和 $W \subset \mathbb{A}^m$ 是代数集；分别用 $X_1, \dots, X_n$ 和 $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$ 表示 $\mathbb{A}^n$ 和 $\mathbb{A}^m$ 上的坐标。

Definition A map $f \colon V \to W$ is a polynomial map if there exist $m$ polynomials $F_1, \dots, F_m \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ such that

$$f(P) = \bigl(F_1(P), \dots, F_m(P)\bigr) \in \mathbb{A}^m_k \quad \text{for all } P \in V.$$

This is an obvious generalisation/ˌdʒen.ər.əl.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ 推广 of the above notion of a polynomial function.

定义 映射 $f \colon V \to W$ 称为多项式映射，如果存在 $m$ 个多项式 $F_1, \dots, F_m \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 使得 $$f(P) = \bigl(F_1(P), \dots, F_m(P)\bigr) \in \mathbb{A}^m_k \quad \text{对所有 } P \in V.$$ 这是前面多项式函数概念的一个明显推广。

Claim A map $f \colon V \to W$ is a polynomial map if and only if for all $j$, the composite map $f_j = Y_j \circ f \in k[V]$ ($j$th coordinate function).

断言 映射 $f \colon V \to W$ 是多项式映射，当且仅当对所有 $j$，复合映射 $f_j = Y_j \circ f \in k[V]$（第 $j$ 个坐标函数）。

This is clear: if $f$ is given by $F_1, \dots, F_m$, then the composite is just $P \mapsto F_j(P)$, which is a polynomial function. Conversely, if $f_j \in k[V]$ for each $j$, then for any choice of $F_j \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ such that $f_j = F_j \bmod I(V)$, I get a description of $f$ as the polynomial map given by $(F_1, \dots, F_m)$.

In view of this claim, the map $f$ can be written $f = (f_1, \dots, f_m)$.

这很明显：如果 $f$ 由 $F_1, \dots, F_m$ 给出，那么复合映射就是 $P \mapsto F_j(P)$，这是一个多项式函数。反过来，如果对每个 $j$ 都有 $f_j \in k[V]$，那么对于 $F_j \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 的任意选取（只要满足 $f_j = F_j \bmod I(V)$），我就得到了 $f$ 作为由 $(F_1, \dots, F_m)$ 给出的多项式映射的描述。 根据这个断言，映射 $f$ 可以写成 $f = (f_1, \dots, f_m)$。

The composite of polynomial maps is defined in the obvious way: if $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$, $W \subset \mathbb{A}^m$ and $U \subset \mathbb{A}^\ell$ are algebraic sets, and $f \colon V \to W$, $g \colon W \to U$ are polynomial maps, then $g \circ f \colon V \to U$ is again a polynomial map; for if $f$ is given by $F_1, \dots, F_m \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$, and $g$ by $G_1, \dots, G_\ell \in k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m]$, then $g \circ f$ is given by

$$G_1(F_1, \dots, F_m), \dots, G_\ell(F_1, \dots, F_m) \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n].$$
多项式映射的复合以显然的方式定义：如果 $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$、$W \subset \mathbb{A}^m$ 和 $U \subset \mathbb{A}^\ell$ 是代数集，而 $f \colon V \to W$、$g \colon W \to U$ 是多项式映射，那么 $g \circ f \colon V \to U$ 仍然是多项式映射；因为如果 $f$ 由 $F_1, \dots, F_m \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 给出，$g$ 由 $G_1, \dots, G_\ell \in k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m]$ 给出，那么 $g \circ f$ 由 $$G_1(F_1, \dots, F_m), \dots, G_\ell(F_1, \dots, F_m) \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$$ 给出。

Definition A polynomial map $f \colon V \to W$ between algebraic sets is an isomorphism if there exists a polynomial map $g \colon W \to V$ such that $f \circ g = g \circ f = \mathrm{id}$.

定义 代数集之间的多项式映射 $f \colon V \to W$ 称为同构，如果存在多项式映射 $g \colon W \to V$ 使得 $f \circ g = g \circ f = \mathrm{id}$。

Several examples of polynomial maps have already been given: the parametrisations/ˌpær.ə.mɪ.trɪˈzeɪ.ʃənz/ 参数化 $\mathbb{R}^1 \to C \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ by $t \mapsto (t^2, t^3)$ or $(t^2 - 1, t^3 - t)$ given in (2.1), and the map $k \to C \subset \mathbb{A}^3_k$ by $t \mapsto (t^3, t^4, t^5)$ discussed in (3.11, b) are clearly of this kind. Also, while discussing Noether normalisation, I had an algebraic set $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n_k$, and considered the general projection $p \colon V \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$ defined by $m$ 'fairly general' linear forms $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$; since the $Y_i$ are linear forms in the coordinates $X_i$ of $\mathbb{A}^n_k$, this projection is a polynomial map.

前面已经给出了若干多项式映射的例子：(2.1) 中的参数化 $\mathbb{R}^1 \to C \subset \mathbb{R}^2$，$t \mapsto (t^2, t^3)$ 或 $(t^2 - 1, t^3 - t)$，以及 (3.11, b) 中讨论的映射 $k \to C \subset \mathbb{A}^3_k$，$t \mapsto (t^3, t^4, t^5)$，显然都是这种类型。此外，在讨论 Noether 正规化时，我有一个代数集 $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n_k$，并考虑了由 $m$ 个"相当一般的"线性形式 $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$ 定义的一般投影 $p \colon V \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$；由于 $Y_i$ 是 $\mathbb{A}^n_k$ 的坐标 $X_i$ 的线性形式，这个投影就是一个多项式映射。

On the other hand the parametrisation of the circle given in (1.1) is given by rational functions (there's a term $\lambda^2 + 1$ in the denominator/dɪˈnɒm.ɪ.neɪ.tər/ 分母); and the inverse map $(X, Y) \dashrightarrow t = Y/X$ from either of the singular cubics $C \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ back to $\mathbb{R}^1$ is also disqualified/dɪsˈkwɒl.ɪ.faɪd/ 被取消资格的 (or at least, doesn't qualify as written) for the same reason.

另一方面，(1.1) 中圆的参数化是由有理函数给出的（分母中有 $\lambda^2 + 1$ 这一项）；而从任一奇异三次曲线 $C \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ 回到 $\mathbb{R}^1$ 的逆映射 $(X, Y) \dashrightarrow t = Y/X$ 也因同样的原因不合格（或者至少按所写的形式不合格）。
