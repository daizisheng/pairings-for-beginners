0.2 Specific calculations versus general theory

0.2 具体计算与一般理论

There are two possible approaches to studying varieties:

Particular   Given specific polynomials $f_i$, we can often understand the variety $V$ by explicit tricks with the $f_i$; this is fun if the dimension $n$ and the degrees of the $f_i$ are small, or the $f_i$ are specially nice, but things get progressively/prəˈɡres.ɪv.li/ 逐步地，越来越 more complicated, and there rapidly comes a time when mere ingenuity/ˌɪn.dʒəˈnjuː.ə.ti/ 独创性，巧妙 with calculations doesn't tell you much about the problem.

特殊方法：给定具体的多项式 $f_i$，我们常常可以通过对 $f_i$ 的巧妙操作来理解簇 $V$。当维数 $n$ 和 $f_i$ 的次数较小，或者 $f_i$ 特别好的时候，这种方法很有趣。但事情会越来越复杂，很快就会到达仅靠计算技巧已经无法深入的地步。

General   The study of properties of $V$ leads at once to basic notions such as regular functions on $V$, nonsingularity/ˌnɒn.sɪŋ.ɡjʊˈlær.ə.ti/ 非奇异性 and tangent planes, the dimension of a variety: the idea that curves such as the above cubics are 1-dimensional is familiar from elementary Cartesian geometry, and the pictures suggest at once what singularity should mean.

一般方法：研究 $V$ 的性质会立刻引出一些基本概念，如 $V$ 上的正则函数、非奇异性与切平面、簇的维数。像上面的三次曲线是一维的——这个想法在初等解析几何中就很熟悉了，图形也立刻暗示了"奇异性"应该是什么意思。

Now a basic problem in giving an undergraduate algebraic geometry course is that an adequate/ˈæd.ɪ.kwət/ 充分的，足够的 treatment of the 'general' approach involves so many definitions that they fill the entire course and squeeze out all substance. Therefore one has to compromise/ˈkɒm.prə.maɪz/ 折中，妥协, and my solution is to cover a small subset of the general theory, with constant reference to specific examples. These notes therefore contain only a fraction of the 'standard bookwork' which would form the compulsory/kəmˈpʌl.sər.i/ 必修的，强制的 core of a 3–year undergraduate math course devoted entirely to algebraic geometry. On the other hand, I hope that each section contains some exercises and worked examples of substance.

给本科生讲代数几何面临的一个基本难题是：要充分处理"一般"的方法涉及太多定义，它们会占满整门课程，把所有实质内容都挤掉。因此必须折中。作者的方案是：覆盖一般理论的一小部分，同时不断参照具体例子。所以这份讲义只包含了"标准教材内容"的一小部分——那些内容本可以构成一个完全致力于代数几何的三年制本科数学课程的必修核心。不过，作者希望每一节都包含一些有实质内容的习题和详解例子。
