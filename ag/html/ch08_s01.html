History and Sociology of the Modern Subject

现代学科的历史与社会学

8.1 Introduction

引言

Algebraic geometry has over the last thirty years or so enjoyed a position in math similar to that of math in the world at large, being respected/rɪˈspek.tɪd/ 受尊敬的 and feared much more than understood. At the same time, the 'service' questions I am regularly asked by British colleagues or by Warwick graduate students are generally of an elementary/ˌel.ɪˈmen.tər.i/ 初等的，基本的 kind: as a rule, they are either covered in this book or in [Atiyah and Macdonald]. What follows is a view of the recent development of the subject, attempting to explain this paradox/ˈpær.ə.dɒks/ 悖论，矛盾. I make no pretence/prɪˈtens/ 自命，伪装 at objectivity/ˌɒb.dʒekˈtɪv.ɪ.ti/ 客观性.

代数几何在过去大约三十年间，在数学界享有的地位类似于数学在整个世界中的地位——被尊敬和畏惧的程度远远超过被理解的程度。与此同时，英国同事或华威大学研究生经常向我请教的"服务性"问题，通常都是初等层面的：一般来说，它们要么在本书中已有涉及，要么在 [Atiyah and Macdonald] 中已有涉及。下面是对这门学科近期发展的一个看法，试图解释这一悖论。我不自称客观。
