2.14 Topology of a nonsingular cubic

2.14 非奇异三次曲线的拓扑

It is easy to see that a nonsingular/nɒnˈsɪŋ.ɡjʊ.lər/ 非奇异的 plane cubic $C : (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset \mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{R}}$ has one of the two shapes

That is, topologically/ˌtɒp.əˈlɒdʒ.ɪ.kəl.i/ 从拓扑上讲, $C$ is either one or two circles (including the single point at infinity, of course). To look at the same question over $\mathbb{C}$, take the alternative normal form

$$C : (y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda)) \cup \{\infty\};$$

what is the topology of $C \subset \mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{C}}$? The answer is a torus/ˈtɔːr.əs/ 环面:

很容易看出，非奇异平面三次曲线 $C: (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset \mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{R}}$ 有两种形状之一。也就是说，拓扑上 $C$ 要么是一个圆，要么是两个圆（当然包括无穷远处那个点）。要在 $\mathbb{C}$ 上考虑同一个问题，取另一种标准形式 $C: (y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda)) \cup \{\infty\}$；$C \subset \mathbb{P}^2_{\mathbb{C}}$ 的拓扑是什么？答案是一个环面：

The idea of the proof is to consider the map

$$\pi \colon C \to \mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}} \quad \text{by} \quad (X, Y, Z) \mapsto (X, Z) \quad \text{and} \quad \infty \mapsto (1, 0);$$

in affine coordinates this is $(x, y) \mapsto x$, so it's the 2-to-1 map corresponding to the graph of $y = \pm\sqrt{x(x-1)(x-\lambda)}$. Everyone knows that $\mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$ is homeomorphic/ˌhoʊ.mi.əˈmɔːr.fɪk/ 同胚的 to $S^2$, the Riemann sphere ('stereographic projection'); consider the 'function' $y(x) = \pm\sqrt{x(x-1)(x-\lambda)}$ on $\mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$. This is 2-valued outside $\{0, 1, \lambda, \infty\}$:

证明的思路是考虑映射 $\pi: C \to \mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$，定义为 $(X,Y,Z) \mapsto (X,Z)$，$\infty \mapsto (1,0)$；在仿射坐标下就是 $(x,y) \mapsto x$，所以这是对应于 $y = \pm\sqrt{x(x-1)(x-\lambda)}$ 图像的2对1映射。众所周知 $\mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$ 同胚于 $S^2$（黎曼球，通过"球极投影"）；考虑 $\mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$ 上的"函数" $y(x) = \pm\sqrt{x(x-1)(x-\lambda)}$。它在 $\{0, 1, \lambda, \infty\}$ 之外是双值的：

Now cut $\mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$ along two paths $01$ and $\lambda\infty$; the double cover falls apart as 2 pieces, so that the function $y$ is single valued on each sheet. So (the shading indicates how the two sheets match up under the glueing/ˈɡluː.ɪŋ/ 粘合).

现在沿两条路径 $01$ 和 $\lambda\infty$ 把 $\mathbb{P}^1_{\mathbb{C}}$ 切开；双覆盖就分成了两片，使得 $y$ 在每一片上都是单值的（阴影表示两片在粘合时是如何对应的）。
