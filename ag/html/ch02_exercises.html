Exercises to Chapter 2

第2章习题

2.1 Let $C : (y^2 = x^3 + x^2) \subset \mathbb{R}^2$. Show that a variable line through $(0, 0)$ meets $C$ at one further point, and hence deduce/dɪˈdjuːs/ 推导出 the parametrisation of $C$ given in (2.1). Do the same for $(y^2 = x^3)$ and $(x^3 = y^3 - y^4)$.

2.1 设 $C: (y^2 = x^3 + x^2) \subset \mathbb{R}^2$。证明过 $(0,0)$ 的动直线与 $C$ 恰好再交于一个点，从而推导出(2.1)中给出的 $C$ 的参数化。对 $(y^2 = x^3)$ 和 $(x^3 = y^3 - y^4)$ 做同样的事。

2.2 Let $\varphi \colon \mathbb{R}^1 \to \mathbb{R}^2$ be the map given by $t \mapsto (t^2, t^3)$; prove directly that any polynomial $f \in \mathbb{R}[X, Y]$ vanishing on the image $C = \varphi(\mathbb{R}^1)$ is divisible/dɪˈvɪz.ɪ.bəl/ 可整除的 by $Y^2 - X^3$. [Hint: use the method of Lemma 2.5.] Determine what property of a field $k$ will ensure that the result holds for $\varphi \colon k \to k^2$ given by the same formula.

Do the same for $t \mapsto (t^2 - 1, t^3 - t)$.

2.2 设 $\varphi: \mathbb{R}^1 \to \mathbb{R}^2$ 是由 $t \mapsto (t^2, t^3)$ 给出的映射；直接证明在像 $C = \varphi(\mathbb{R}^1)$ 上消失的任何多项式 $f \in \mathbb{R}[X,Y]$ 都可被 $Y^2 - X^3$ 整除。[提示：用引理2.5的方法。] 确定域 $k$ 需要什么性质才能保证对由同一公式给出的 $\varphi: k \to k^2$ 也有同样的结论。
对 $t \mapsto (t^2 - 1, t^3 - t)$ 做同样的事。

2.3 Let $C : (f = 0) \subset k^2$, and let $P = (a, b) \in C$; assume that $\partial f / \partial x(P) \neq 0$. Prove that the line

$$L : \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(P) \cdot (x - a) + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}(P) \cdot (y - b) = 0$$

is the tangent/ˈtæn.dʒənt/ 切线 line to $C$ at $P$, that is, the unique line $L$ of $k^2$ for which $f|_L$ has a multiple root at $P$ (this is worked out in detail in (6.1)).

2.3 设 $C: (f = 0) \subset k^2$，$P = (a,b) \in C$；假设 $\partial f/\partial x(P) \neq 0$。证明直线 $L: \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(P) \cdot (x - a) + \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}(P) \cdot (y - b) = 0$ 就是 $C$ 在 $P$ 处的切线，即 $k^2$ 中使得 $f|_L$ 在 $P$ 有重根的那条唯一直线（详见(6.1)）。

2.4 Let $C : (y^2 = x^3 + 4x)$, with the simplified group law (2.13). Show that the tangent line to $C$ at $P = (2, 4)$ passes through $(0, 0)$, and deduce that $P$ is a point of order 4 in the group law.

2.4 设 $C: (y^2 = x^3 + 4x)$，用简化的群律(2.13)。证明 $C$ 在 $P = (2,4)$ 处的切线过 $(0,0)$，从而推出 $P$ 在群律中是4阶的点。

2.5 Let $C : (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ be nonsingular; find all points of order 2 in the group law, and understand what group they form (there are two cases to consider).

Now explain geometrically how you would set about finding all points of order 4 on $C$.

2.5 设 $C: (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset \mathbb{R}^2$ 非奇异；找出群律中所有2阶的点，并弄清楚它们构成什么群（需要分两种情况讨论）。
然后从几何上解释如何找到 $C$ 上所有4阶的点。

2.6 Let $C : (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset \mathbb{R}^2$; write a computer program to sketch part of $C$, and to calculate the group law. That is, it prompts you for the coordinates of 2 points $A$ and $B$, then draws the lines and tells you the coordinates of $A + B$. (Use real variables.)

2.6 设 $C: (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset \mathbb{R}^2$；编写一个计算机程序来画出 $C$ 的一部分，并计算群律。也就是说，程序提示你输入两个点 $A$ 和 $B$ 的坐标，然后画出直线并告诉你 $A + B$ 的坐标。（用实数变量。）

2.7 Let $C : (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset k^2$; if $A = (x_1, y_1)$ and $B = (x_2, y_2)$, show how to give the coordinates of $A + B$ as rational functions of $x_1, y_1, x_2, y_2$. [Hint: if $F(X)$ is a polynomial of degree 3 and you know 2 of the roots, you can find the 3rd by looking at just one coefficient/ˌkoʊ.ɪˈfɪʃ.ənt/ 系数 of $F$. This is a question with a nonunique answer, since there are many correct expressions for the rational functions. One solution is given in (4.14).]

2.7 设 $C: (y^2 = x^3 + ax + b) \subset k^2$；若 $A = (x_1, y_1)$，$B = (x_2, y_2)$，说明如何把 $A + B$ 的坐标表示为 $x_1, y_1, x_2, y_2$ 的有理函数。[提示：如果 $F(X)$ 是3次多项式且你已知其中2个根，只需看 $F$ 的一个系数就能找到第3个根。这题的答案不唯一，因为有理函数有很多正确的表达式。一个解答见(4.14)。]

2.8 By writing down the equation of the tangent line to $C$ at $A$, find a formula for $2A$ in the group law on $C$, and verify that it is the limit of a suitable formula for $A + B$ as $B$ tends to $A$. [Hint: use Ex. 2.7, and if necessary refer to (4.14).]

2.8 写出 $C$ 在 $A$ 处的切线方程，找到群律中 $2A$ 的公式，并验证它是当 $B$ 趋近于 $A$ 时 $A + B$ 的某个合适公式的极限。[提示：用习题2.7，必要时参考(4.14)。]

2.9 Let $x, z$ be coordinates on $k^2$, and let $f \in k[x, z]$; write $f$ as

$$f = a + bx + cz + dx^2 + exz + fz^2 + \cdots .$$

Write down the conditions in terms of $a, b, c, \ldots$ that must hold in order that

(i) $P = (0, 0) \in C : (f = 0)$;

(ii) the tangent line to $C$ at $P$ is $(z = 0)$;

(iii) $P$ is an inflexion point of $C$ with $(z = 0)$ as the tangent line.

(Recall from (2.12) that $P \in C$ is an inflexion point if the tangent line $L$ is defined, and $f|_L$ has at least a 3-fold zero at $P$.)

2.9 设 $x, z$ 是 $k^2$ 上的坐标，$f \in k[x,z]$；把 $f$ 写成 $f = a + bx + cz + dx^2 + exz + fz^2 + \cdots$。写出用 $a, b, c, \ldots$ 表示的条件，使得：
(i) $P = (0,0) \in C: (f = 0)$；
(ii) $C$ 在 $P$ 处的切线是 $(z = 0)$；
(iii) $P$ 是 $C$ 的拐点，且切线为 $(z = 0)$。
（回忆(2.12)中的定义：$P \in C$ 是拐点，是指切线 $L$ 有定义，且 $f|_L$ 在 $P$ 处至少有3重零点。）

2.10 Let $C \subset \mathbb{P}^2_k$ be a plane cubic, and suppose that $P \in C$ is an inflexion point; prove that a change of coordinates in $\mathbb{P}^2_k$ can be used to bring $C$ into the normal form

$$Y^2 Z = X^3 + aX^2 Z + bXZ^2 + cZ^3.$$

[Hint: take coordinates such that $P = (0, 1, 0)$ and the inflexion tangent is $(Z = 0)$; then using the previous question, in local coordinates $(x, z)$, $Y$ will appear in a quadratic term $Y^2 Z$, and otherwise only linearly. Show then that you can get rid of the linear term in $Y$ by completing the square.]

2.10 设 $C \subset \mathbb{P}^2_k$ 是平面三次曲线，且 $P \in C$ 是拐点；证明通过 $\mathbb{P}^2_k$ 中的坐标变换可以把 $C$ 化为标准形式 $Y^2Z = X^3 + aX^2Z + bXZ^2 + cZ^3$。
[提示：选坐标使得 $P = (0,1,0)$ 且拐点切线为 $(Z = 0)$；然后利用上一题，在局部坐标 $(x,z)$ 中，$Y$ 只在二次项 $Y^2Z$ 中出现，其余只以一次幂出现。然后证明可以通过配方消去 $Y$ 的一次项。]

2.11 (Group law on a cuspidal/ˈkʌs.pɪ.dəl/ 尖点的 cubic.) Consider the curve

$$C : (z = x^3) \subset k^2;$$

$C$ is the image of the bijective/baɪˈdʒek.tɪv/ 双射的 map $\varphi \colon k \to C$ by $t \mapsto (t, t^3)$, so it inherits a group law from the additive group $k$. Prove that this is the unique group law on $C$ such that $(0, 0)$ is the neutral element and

$$P + Q + R = 0 \iff P, Q, R \text{ are collinear}$$

for $P, Q, R \in C$. [Hint: you might find useful the identity

$$\det \begin{pmatrix} 1 & a & a^3 \\ 1 & b & b^3 \\ 1 & c & c^3 \end{pmatrix} = (a - b)(b - c)(c - a)(a + b + c).]$$

In projective terms, $C$ is the curve $(Y^2 Z = X^3)$, our old friend with a cusp at the origin and an inflexion point at $(0, 1, 0)$, and the point of the question is that the usual construction gives a group law on the complement/ˈkɒm.plɪ.ment/ 补集 of the singular point.

2.11（尖点三次曲线上的群律。）考虑曲线 $C: (z = x^3) \subset k^2$；$C$ 是双射 $\varphi: k \to C$，$t \mapsto (t, t^3)$ 的像，因此它从加法群 $k$ 继承了一个群律。证明这是 $C$ 上唯一满足以下条件的群律：$(0,0)$ 是单位元，且对 $P, Q, R \in C$，$P + Q + R = 0 \iff P, Q, R$ 三点共线。
[提示：你可能会用到恒等式 $\det\begin{pmatrix}1 & a & a^3 \\ 1 & b & b^3 \\ 1 & c & c^3\end{pmatrix} = (a-b)(b-c)(c-a)(a+b+c)$。]
在射影意义下，$C$ 就是曲线 $(Y^2Z = X^3)$，我们的老朋友——原点处有尖点、$(0,1,0)$ 处有拐点——这道题的要点是，通常的构造在奇异点的补集上给出了群律。

2.12 (Due to Leonardo Pisano, known as Fibonacci, A.D. 1220.) Prove that for $u, v \in \mathbb{Z}$,

$$u^2 + v^2 \text{ and } u^2 - v^2 \text{ both squares} \implies v = 0.$$

Hints (due to Pierre de Fermat, see J.W.S. Cassels, Journal of London Math Soc. 41 (1966), p. 207):

2.12（出自Leonardo Pisano，即Fibonacci，公元1220年。）证明：对于 $u, v \in \mathbb{Z}$，若 $u^2 + v^2$ 和 $u^2 - v^2$ 都是完全平方数，则 $v = 0$。
提示（归功于Pierre de Fermat，参见 J.W.S. Cassels, Journal of London Math Soc. 41 (1966), p. 207）：

Step 1 Reduce to solving

$$x^2 = u^2 + v^2, \quad y^2 = u^2 - v^2 \qquad (*)$$

with $x, y, u, v \in \mathbb{Z}$ pairwise coprime/ˌkoʊˈpraɪm/ 互素的.

第1步　化为求解 $x^2 = u^2 + v^2$，$y^2 = u^2 - v^2$（记为 $(*)$），其中 $x, y, u, v \in \mathbb{Z}$ 两两互素。

Step 2 Considerations mod 4 show that $x, y, u$ are odd and $v$ even.

第2步　模4的讨论说明 $x, y, u$ 都是奇数，$v$ 是偶数。

Step 3 The 4 pairs of factors on the l.-h.s. of the factorisations

$$(x - u)(x + u) = v^2$$ $$(u - y)(u + y) = v^2$$ $$(x - y)(x + y) = 2v^2$$ $$(2u - x - y)(2u + x + y) = (x - y)^2 \qquad (**)$$

have no common factors other than powers of 2.

第3步　以上四个因式分解中，等式左边的每对因子除了2的幂次外没有公因子。

Step 4 Replacing $y$ by $-y$ if necessary, we can assume that $4 \nmid x - y$. Now by considering the parity/ˈpær.ɪ.ti/ 奇偶性 of factors on l.-h.s. of $(**)$, prove that

$$x - u = 2v_1^2, \quad u - y = 2u_1^2, \quad x - y = 2x_1^2$$ $$\text{and} \quad 2u - x - y = 2y_1^2$$

with $u_1, v_1, x_1, y_1 \in \mathbb{Z}$.

第4步　如有必要把 $y$ 换成 $-y$，可以假设 $4 \nmid x - y$。现在通过考虑 $(**)$ 左边因子的奇偶性，证明 $x - u = 2v_1^2$，$u - y = 2u_1^2$，$x - y = 2x_1^2$，$2u - x - y = 2y_1^2$，其中 $u_1, v_1, x_1, y_1 \in \mathbb{Z}$。

Step 5 Show that $u_1, v_1, x_1, y_1$ is another solution of $(*)$ with $v_1 < v$, and deduce a contradiction by 'infinite descent/dɪˈsent/ 下降'.

第5步　证明 $u_1, v_1, x_1, y_1$ 是 $(*)$ 的另一个解，且 $v_1 < v$，由"无穷递降法"得出矛盾。

Compare this argument with the proof of (2.2), which was easier only in that I didn't have to mess about with 2s.

把这个论证和(2.2)的证明比较一下——后者简单仅仅是因为不用和2纠缠。
