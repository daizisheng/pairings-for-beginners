5.8 Birational maps

5.8 双有理映射

Definition   Let $V$ and $W$ be (affine or projective) varieties; then a rational map $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ is birational (or is a birational equivalence) if it has a rational inverse, that is, if there exists a rational map $g \colon W \dashrightarrow V$ such that $f \circ g = \operatorname{id}_W$ and $g \circ f = \operatorname{id}_V$.

定义   设 $V$ 和 $W$ 是（仿射或射影的）簇。有理映射 $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ 被称为双有理的（或双有理等价），如果它有有理逆映射，即存在有理映射 $g \colon W \dashrightarrow V$ 使得 $f \circ g = \operatorname{id}_W$ 且 $g \circ f = \operatorname{id}_V$。

Proposition   The following three conditions on a rational map $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ are equivalent/ɪˈkwɪv.əl.ənt/ 等价的:

(i) $f$ is a birational equivalence;

(ii) $f$ is dominant/ˈdɒm.ɪ.nənt/ 支配的 (see (4.10)), and $f^* \colon k(W) \to k(V)$ is an isomorphism;

(iii) there exist open sets $V_0 \subset V$ and $W_0 \subset W$ such that $f$ restricted to $V_0$ is an isomorphism $f \colon V_0 \to W_0$.

命题   关于有理映射 $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$，下面三个条件等价：

(i) $f$ 是双有理等价；

(ii) $f$ 是支配的（见 (4.10)），且 $f^* \colon k(W) \to k(V)$ 是同构；

(iii) 存在开集 $V_0 \subset V$ 和 $W_0 \subset W$，使得 $f$ 限制到 $V_0$ 上是同构 $f \colon V_0 \to W_0$。

Proof   $f^*$ is defined in the same way as for affine varieties, and (i) $\iff$ (ii) is as in (4.11). (iii) $\implies$ (i) is clear, since an isomorphism $f \colon V_0 \to W_0$ and its inverse $g = f^{-1} \colon W_0 \to V_0$ are by definition rational maps between $V$ and $W$.

The essential implication (i) $\implies$ (iii) is tricky, although content-free (GOTO (5.9) if you want to avoid a headache): by assumption (i), there are inverse rational maps $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ and $g \colon W \dashrightarrow V$; now set $V' = \operatorname{dom} f \subset V$ and $\varphi = f|_{V'} \colon V' \to W$, and similarly $W' = \operatorname{dom} g \subset W$ and $\psi = g|_{W'} \colon W' \to V$. In the diagram

$$\psi^{-1}V' \xrightarrow{\;\psi\;} V' \xrightarrow{\;\varphi\;} W$$

all the arrows are morphisms, and $\operatorname{id}_W |_{\psi^{-1}V'} = \varphi \circ \psi$ (as morphisms) follows from $\operatorname{id}_W = f \circ g$ (as rational maps). Hence

$$\varphi(\psi(P)) = P \quad \text{for all } P \in \psi^{-1}V'.$$

Now set $V_0 = \varphi^{-1}\psi^{-1}V'$, and $W_0 = \psi^{-1}\varphi^{-1}W'$; then $\varphi \colon V_0 \to \psi^{-1}V'$ is a morphism by construction. However, $\psi^{-1}V' \subset W_0$, since $P \in \psi^{-1}V'$ implies that $\varphi(\psi(P)) = P$, so that $P \in \psi^{-1}\varphi^{-1}W' = W_0$. Therefore, $\varphi \colon V_0 \to W_0$ is a morphism, and similarly $\psi \colon W_0 \to V_0$.   Q.E.D.

证明   $f^*$ 的定义与仿射簇完全一样，(i) $\iff$ (ii) 如 (4.11)。(iii) $\implies$ (i) 显然，因为同构 $f \colon V_0 \to W_0$ 和逆映射 $g = f^{-1} \colon W_0 \to V_0$ 本身就是 $V$ 和 $W$ 之间的有理映射。

关键的蕴涵 (i) $\implies$ (iii) 虽然不含实质内容但有点绕（怕头疼就直接跳到 (5.9)）：由假设 (i)，有互逆的有理映射 $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ 和 $g \colon W \dashrightarrow V$；令 $V' = \operatorname{dom} f \subset V$，$\varphi = f|_{V'} \colon V' \to W$，类似地 $W' = \operatorname{dom} g \subset W$，$\psi = g|_{W'} \colon W' \to V$。在图

$$\psi^{-1}V' \xrightarrow{\;\psi\;} V' \xrightarrow{\;\varphi\;} W$$

中所有箭头都是态射，由 $\operatorname{id}_W = f \circ g$（作为有理映射）得到 $\operatorname{id}_W |_{\psi^{-1}V'} = \varphi \circ \psi$（作为态射）。于是

$$\varphi(\psi(P)) = P, \quad \forall\, P \in \psi^{-1}V'.$$

现在令 $V_0 = \varphi^{-1}\psi^{-1}V'$，$W_0 = \psi^{-1}\varphi^{-1}W'$；则 $\varphi \colon V_0 \to \psi^{-1}V'$ 是态射。而 $\psi^{-1}V' \subset W_0$，因为 $P \in \psi^{-1}V'$ 意味着 $\varphi(\psi(P)) = P$，从而 $P \in \psi^{-1}\varphi^{-1}W' = W_0$。因此 $\varphi \colon V_0 \to W_0$ 是态射，类似地 $\psi \colon W_0 \to V_0$ 也是。Q.E.D.
