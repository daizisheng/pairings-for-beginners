Chapter 5 — Projective and Birational Geometry

第5章 射影与双有理几何

The first part of §5 aims to generalise/ˈdʒen.ər.əl.aɪz/ 推广 the content of §§3–4 to projective varieties; this is fairly mechanical/məˈkæn.ɪ.kəl/ 机械的，常规的, with just a few essential points. The remainder of the section is concerned with birational geometry, taking up the function field $k(V)$ from the end of §4; this is material which fits equally well into the projective or affine context.

第5章的前半部分旨在把第3–4章的内容推广到射影簇上；这基本上是常规操作，只有少数几个要点需要注意。后半部分则讨论双有理几何，接续第4章末尾引入的函数域 $k(V)$；这部分内容在射影或仿射框架下都同样适用。
