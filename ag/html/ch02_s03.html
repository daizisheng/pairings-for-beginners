2.3 Lemma

2.3 引理

Lemma 2.3   Let $K$ be an algebraically closed/ˌæl.dʒɪˌbreɪ.ɪk.li kloʊzd/ 代数封闭的 field, $p, q \in K[t]$ coprime elements, and assume that 4 distinct linear combinations (that is, $\lambda p + \mu q$ for 4 distinct ratios $(\lambda : \mu) \in \mathbb{P}^1 K$) are squares in $K[t]$; then $p, q \in K$.

引理 2.3   设 $K$ 是代数封闭域，$p, q \in K[t]$ 互素，假设有 4 个不同的线性组合（即对 4 个不同的比 $(\lambda:\mu) \in \mathbb{P}^1 K$，$\lambda p + \mu q$）在 $K[t]$ 中是平方；则 $p, q \in K$。

Proof   (Fermat's method of 'infinite descent/dɪˈsent/ 下降') Both the hypotheses and conclusion of the lemma are not affected by replacing $p, q$ by

$$p' = ap + bq, \quad q' = cp + dq,$$

with $a, b, c, d \in K$ and $ad - bc \neq 0$. Hence I can assume that the 4 given squares are

$$p, \quad p - q, \quad p - \lambda q, \quad q.$$

Then $p = u^2$, $q = v^2$, and $u, v \in K[t]$ are coprime/ˌkoʊˈpraɪm/ 互素的, with

$$\max(\deg u, \deg v) < \max(\deg p, \deg q).$$

Now by contradiction/ˌkɒn.trəˈdɪk.ʃən/ 矛盾, suppose that $\max(\deg p, \deg q) > 0$ and is minimal among all $p, q$ satisfying the condition of the lemma. Then both of

$$p - q = u^2 - v^2 = (u - v)(u + v)$$

and

$$p - \lambda q = u^2 - \lambda v^2 = (u - \mu v)(u + \mu v)$$

(where $\mu = \sqrt{\lambda}$) are squares in $K[t]$, so that by coprimeness of $u, v$, I conclude that each of $u - v$, $u + v$, $u - \mu v$, $u + \mu v$ are squares. This contradicts the minimality/ˌmɪn.ɪˈmæl.ɪ.ti/ 极小性 of $\max(\deg p, \deg q)$.   Q.E.D.

证明  （费马的"无穷递降法"）引理的假设和结论都不受以下替换的影响：把 $p, q$ 换成 $$p' = ap + bq, \quad q' = cp + dq，$$ 其中 $a, b, c, d \in K$ 且 $ad - bc \neq 0$。所以我可以假设那 4 个平方分别是 $$p, \quad p - q, \quad p - \lambda q, \quad q.$$ 于是 $p = u^2$，$q = v^2$，其中 $u, v \in K[t]$ 互素，且 $$\max(\deg u, \deg v) < \max(\deg p, \deg q).$$ 现在用反证法，假设 $\max(\deg p, \deg q) > 0$ 并且在满足引理条件的所有 $p, q$ 中取到最小值。那么 $$p - q = u^2 - v^2 = (u-v)(u+v)$$ 和 $$p - \lambda q = u^2 - \lambda v^2 = (u - \mu v)(u + \mu v)$$ （其中 $\mu = \sqrt{\lambda}$）都是 $K[t]$ 中的平方，所以由 $u, v$ 互素，我得出 $u - v$、$u + v$、$u - \mu v$、$u + \mu v$ 都是平方。这与 $\max(\deg p, \deg q)$ 的极小性矛盾。   Q.E.D.
