8.9 Regular Functions and Sheaves

正则函数与层

The reader who has properly grasped the notion of rational function $f \in k(X)$ on a variety $X$ and of regularity/ˌreɡ.jəˈlær.ɪ.ti/ 正则性 of $f$ at $P \in X$ ((4.7) and (5.4)) already has a pretty good intuitive idea of the structure sheaf/ʃiːf/ 层 $\mathcal{O}_X$. For an open set $U \subset X$, the set of regular functions $U \to k$

$$\mathcal{O}_X(U) = \big\{ f \in k(X) \;\big|\; f \text{ is regular } \forall P \in U \big\} = \bigcap_{P \in U} \mathcal{O}_{X,P}$$

is a subring of the field $k(X)$. The sheaf $\mathcal{O}_X$ is just the family of rings $\mathcal{O}_X(U)$ as $U$ runs through the opens of $X$. Clearly, any element of the local ring $\mathcal{O}_{X,P}$ (see (4.7) and (5.4) for the definition) is regular in some neighbourhood/ˈneɪ.bə.hʊd/ 邻域 $U$ of $P$, so that $\mathcal{O}_{X,P} = \bigcup_{U \ni P} \mathcal{O}_X(U)$. There's no more to it than that; there's a fixed pool of rational sections $k(X)$, and sections of the sheaf over an open $U$ are just rational sections with a regularity condition at every $P \in U$.

正确理解了簇 $X$ 上的有理函数 $f \in k(X)$ 以及 $f$ 在 $P \in X$ 处的正则性（(4.7) 和 (5.4)）的读者，已经对结构层 $\mathcal{O}_X$ 有了相当好的直觉认识。对于开集 $U \subset X$，正则函数 $U \to k$ 的集合 $$\mathcal{O}_X(U) = \big\{ f \in k(X) \;\big|\; f \text{ 在所有 } P \in U \text{ 处正则} \big\} = \bigcap_{P \in U} \mathcal{O}_{X,P}$$ 是域 $k(X)$ 的一个子环。层 $\mathcal{O}_X$ 就是当 $U$ 遍历 $X$ 的开集时的环族 $\mathcal{O}_X(U)$。显然，局部环 $\mathcal{O}_{X,P}$（参见 (4.7) 和 (5.4) 的定义）中的任何元素都在 $P$ 的某个邻域 $U$ 中正则，因此 $\mathcal{O}_{X,P} = \bigcup_{U \ni P} \mathcal{O}_X(U)$。就这么简单；有一个固定的有理截面的储备池 $k(X)$，层在开集 $U$ 上的截面就是在每个 $P \in U$ 处满足正则性条件的有理截面。

This language is adequate to describe any torsion/ˈtɔː.ʃən/ 挠 free sheaf on an irreducible variety with the Zariski topology. Of course, you need the full definition of sheaves if $X$ is reducible/rɪˈdjuː.sɪ.bəl/ 可约的, or if you want to handle more complicated sheaves, or to use the complex topology.

这种语言足以描述具有Zariski拓扑的不可约簇上的任何无挠层。当然，如果 $X$ 是可约的，或者你想处理更复杂的层，或者要使用复拓扑，就需要层的完整定义。
