3.15 Proof of (3.8)

3.15 (3.8)的证明

Let $A = k[a_1, \dots, a_n]$ be a finitely generated $k$-algebra and suppose that $y_1, \dots, y_m \in A$ are as in (3.13). Write $B = k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$. Then $A$ is a finite $B$-algebra, and it is given that $A$ is a field. If I knew that $B$ is a field, it would follow at once that $m = 0$, so that $A$ is a finite $k$-algebra, that is, a finite field extension of $k$, and (3.8) would be proved. Therefore it remains only to prove the following statement:

设 $A = k[a_1, \dots, a_n]$ 是有限生成的 $k$-代数，并假设 $y_1, \dots, y_m \in A$ 如 (3.13) 所述。记 $B = k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$。则 $A$ 是有限 $B$-代数，而已知 $A$ 是域。如果我知道 $B$ 是域，那么立刻得出 $m = 0$，从而 $A$ 是有限 $k$-代数，即 $k$ 的有限域扩张，(3.8) 就证完了。因此只需证明以下命题：

Lemma   If $A$ is a field, and $B \subset A$ a subring/ˈsʌb.rɪŋ/ 子环 such that $A$ is a finite $B$-algebra, then $B$ is a field.

引理   如果 $A$ 是域，$B \subset A$ 是子环且 $A$ 是有限 $B$-代数，则 $B$ 是域。

Proof   For any $0 \neq b \in B$, the inverse/ˈɪn.vɜːs/ 逆元 $b^{-1} \in A$ exists in $A$. Now by (3.12, ii), the finiteness implies that $b^{-1}$ satisfies a monic equation over $B$, that is, there exists a relation

$$b^{-n} + a_{n-1}b^{-(n-1)} + \cdots + a_1 b^{-1} + a_0 = 0, \quad \text{with } a_i \in B;$$

then multiplying through by $b^{n-1}$,

$$b^{-1} = -(a_{n-1} + a_{n-2}b + \cdots + a_0 b^{n-1}) \in B.$$

Therefore $B$ is a field. This proves (3.8) and completes the proof of NSS.

证明   对任意 $0 \neq b \in B$，逆元 $b^{-1} \in A$ 存在。由 (3.12, ii)，有限性意味着 $b^{-1}$ 满足 $B$ 上的首一方程，即存在关系式 $$b^{-n} + a_{n-1}b^{-(n-1)} + \cdots + a_1 b^{-1} + a_0 = 0, \quad a_i \in B;$$ 两边乘以 $b^{n-1}$，得 $$b^{-1} = -(a_{n-1} + a_{n-2}b + \cdots + a_0 b^{n-1}) \in B.$$ 因此 $B$ 是域。这就证明了 (3.8)，从而完成了零点定理 (NSS) 的证明。
