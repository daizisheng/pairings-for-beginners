4.13 Standard Open Subsets

标准开子集

Let $V$ be an affine variety. For $f \in k[V]$, write $V_f$ for the open set $V_f = V \setminus V(f) = \bigl\{\, P \in V \;\big|\; f(P) \neq 0 \,\bigr\}$. The $V_f$ are called standard open sets of $V$.

设 $V$ 是一个仿射簇。对 $f \in k[V]$，记 $V_f$ 为开集 $V_f = V \setminus V(f) = \{ P \in V \mid f(P) \neq 0 \}$。$V_f$ 称为 $V$ 的标准开集。

Proposition $V_f$ is isomorphic/ˌaɪ.səˈmɔːr.fɪk/ 同构的 to an affine variety, and

$$k[V_f] = k[V][f^{-1}].$$
命题 $V_f$ 同构于一个仿射簇，并且 $$k[V_f] = k[V][f^{-1}].$$

Proof The idea is to consider the graph of the function $f^{-1}$; a similar trick was used for (b) $\Longrightarrow$ (c) in the proof of NSS (3.10).

Let $J = I(V) \subset k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$, and choose $F \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ such that $f = F \bmod I(V)$. Now define $I = (J, YF - 1) \subset k[X_1, \dots, X_n, Y]$, and let

$$V(I) = W \subset \mathbb{A}^{n+1}.$$

It is easy to check that the maps indicated in the diagram are inverse morphisms/ˈmɔːr.fɪ.zəmz/ 态射 between $W$ and $V_f$. The statement about the coordinate ring is contained in (4.8, III).   Q.E.D.

证明 思路是考虑函数 $f^{-1}$ 的图像；类似的技巧在 NSS (3.10) 的证明中已用于 (b) $\Longrightarrow$ (c)。 令 $J = I(V) \subset k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$，选取 $F \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 使得 $f = F \bmod I(V)$。现在定义 $I = (J, YF - 1) \subset k[X_1, \dots, X_n, Y]$，令 $$V(I) = W \subset \mathbb{A}^{n+1}.$$ 容易验证图中所示的映射是 $W$ 与 $V_f$ 之间的互逆态射。关于坐标环的结论包含在 (4.8, III) 中。 Q.E.D.

The standard open sets $V_f$ are important because they form a basis/ˈbeɪ.sɪs/ 基 for the Zariski topology of $V$: every open set $U \subset V$ is a union of $V_f$ (since every closed subset is of the form $V(I) = \bigcap_{f \in I} V(f)$ for some ideal). Thus the point of the result just proved is that every open set $U \subset V$ is a union of open sets $V_f$ which are affine varieties.

标准开集 $V_f$ 很重要，因为它们构成 $V$ 的 Zariski 拓扑的一组基：每个开集 $U \subset V$ 都是某些 $V_f$ 的并（因为每个闭子集都具有 $V(I) = \bigcap_{f \in I} V(f)$ 的形式，其中 $I$ 是某个理想）。因此刚才证明的结果的要点在于：$V$ 的每个开集 $U$ 都是一些仿射簇 $V_f$ 的并。
