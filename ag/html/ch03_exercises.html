Exercises to Chapter 3

第3章习题

3.1   An integral/ˈɪn.tɪ.ɡrəl/ 整的 domain $A$ is a principal ideal domain if every ideal $I$ of $A$ is principal, that is of the form $I = (a)$; show directly that the ideals in a PID satisfy the a.c.c.

3.1   整环 $A$ 是主理想整环（PID），如果 $A$ 的每个理想 $I$ 都是主理想，即形如 $I = (a)$；直接证明PID中的理想满足升链条件。

3.2   Show that an integral domain $A$ is a UFD if and only if every ascending chain of principal ideals terminates/ˈtɜː.mɪ.neɪts/ 终止, and every irreducible element of $A$ is prime.

3.2   证明整环 $A$ 是UFD当且仅当每条主理想升链都终止，且 $A$ 的每个不可约元都是素元。

3.3

(i) Prove Gauss's lemma: if $A$ is a UFD and $f, g \in A[X]$ are polynomials with coefficients in $A$, then a prime element of $A$ that is a common factor of the coefficients of the product $fg$ is a common factor of the coefficients of $f$ or $g$.

(ii) It is proved in undergraduate algebra that if $K$ is a field then $K[X]$ is a UFD. Use induction on $n$ to prove that $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ is a UFD; for this you will need to compare factorisations/ˌfæk.tər.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃənz/ 因式分解 in $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ with factorisations in $k(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1})[X_n]$, using Gauss's lemma to clear denominators.

3.3
(i) 证明Gauss引理：如果 $A$ 是UFD，$f, g \in A[X]$ 是 $A$ 上的多项式，那么 $A$ 的一个素元如果是乘积 $fg$ 的系数的公因子，则它是 $f$ 或 $g$ 的系数的公因子。
(ii) 本科代数中证明了：如果 $K$ 是域，则 $K[X]$ 是UFD。用对 $n$ 的归纳法证明 $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 是UFD；为此需要比较 $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 中的因式分解与 $k(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1})[X_n]$ 中的因式分解，利用Gauss引理来通分。

3.4   Prove Proposition 3.2, (ii): if $A$ is an integral domain with field of fractions $K$, and if $0 \notin S \subset A$ is a subset, define

$$B = A[S^{-1}] = \left\{\frac{a}{b} \in K \;\middle|\; a \in A, \text{ and } b = 1 \text{ or a product of elements of } S\right\}.$$

Prove that an ideal $I$ of $B$ is completely determined by its intersection with $A$, and deduce/dɪˈdjuːs/ 推导出 that $A$ Noetherian $\implies$ $B$ Noetherian.

3.4   证明命题 3.2 (ii)：如果 $A$ 是整环，分式域为 $K$，且 $0 \notin S \subset A$ 是子集，定义 $$B = A[S^{-1}] = \left\{\frac{a}{b} \in K \;\middle|\; a \in A,\; b = 1 \text{ 或 } S \text{ 中元素的乘积}\right\}.$$ 证明 $B$ 的理想 $I$ 完全由它与 $A$ 的交集决定，并推出 $A$ 是Noetherian环 $\implies$ $B$ 是Noetherian环。

3.5   Let $J = (XY, XZ, YZ) \subset k[X, Y, Z]$; find $V(J) \subset \mathbb{A}^3$; is it irreducible? Is it true that $J = I(V(J))$? Prove that $J$ cannot be generated by 2 elements. Now let $J' = (XY, (X-Y)Z)$; find $V(J')$, and calculate $\operatorname{rad} J'$.

3.5   设 $J = (XY, XZ, YZ) \subset k[X,Y,Z]$；求 $V(J) \subset \mathbb{A}^3$；它是不可约的吗？$J = I(V(J))$ 成立吗？证明 $J$ 不能由2个元素生成。再设 $J' = (XY, (X-Y)Z)$；求 $V(J')$，并计算 $\operatorname{rad} J'$。

3.6   Let $J = (X^2 + Y^2 - 1,\; Y - 1)$; find $f \in I(V(J)) \setminus J$.

3.6   设 $J = (X^2 + Y^2 - 1,\; Y - 1)$；找到 $f \in I(V(J)) \setminus J$。

3.7   Let $J = (X^2 + Y^2 + Z^2,\; XY + XZ + YZ)$; identify $V(J)$ and $I(V(J))$.

3.7   设 $J = (X^2 + Y^2 + Z^2,\; XY + XZ + YZ)$；确定 $V(J)$ 和 $I(V(J))$。

3.8   Prove that the irreducible components of an algebraic set are unique (this was stated without proof in (3.7, b)). That is, given two decompositions/ˌdiː.kɒm.pəˈzɪʃ.ənz/ 分解 $V = \bigcup_{i \in I} V_i = \bigcup_{j \in J} W_j$ of $V$ as a union of irreducibles, assumed to be irredundant/ˌɪr.ɪˈdʌn.dənt/ 无冗余的 (that is, $V_i \not\subset V_{i'}$ for $i \neq i'$), prove that the $V_i$ are just a renumbering of the $W_j$.

3.8   证明代数集的不可约分支是唯一的（这在 (3.7, b) 中未加证明地陈述了）。即，给定两个分解 $V = \bigcup_{i \in I} V_i = \bigcup_{j \in J} W_j$，其中 $V$ 被表示为不可约集的并，且假设分解是无冗余的（即 $V_i \not\subset V_{i'}$ 对 $i \neq i'$ 成立），证明 $V_i$ 不过是 $W_j$ 的重新编号。

3.9   Let $f = X^2 - Y^2$ and $g = X^3 + XY^2 - Y^3 - X^2Y - X + Y$; find the irreducible components of $V(f, g) \subset \mathbb{A}^2_{\mathbb{C}}$.

3.9   设 $f = X^2 - Y^2$，$g = X^3 + XY^2 - Y^3 - X^2Y - X + Y$；求 $V(f, g) \subset \mathbb{A}^2_{\mathbb{C}}$ 的不可约分支。

3.10   If $J = (uw - v^2,\; w^3 - u^5)$, show that $V(J)$ has two irreducible components, one of which is the curve $C$ of (3.11, b).

Prove that the same curve $C$ can be defined by two equations, $uw = v^2$ and $u^5 - 2u^2 vw + w^3 = 0$. The point here is that the second equation, restricted to the quadric cone $(uw = v^2)$, is trying to be a square.

3.10   如果 $J = (uw - v^2,\; w^3 - u^5)$，证明 $V(J)$ 有两个不可约分支，其中一个是 (3.11, b) 中的曲线 $C$。
证明同一条曲线 $C$ 可以由两个方程 $uw = v^2$ 和 $u^5 - 2u^2 vw + w^3 = 0$ 定义。这里的关键是第二个方程限制在二次锥面 $(uw = v^2)$ 上时，"试图成为一个平方"。

3.11   Let $f = v^2 - uw$, $g = u^4 - vw$, $h = w^2 - u^3 v$. Identify the variety $V(f, g, h) \subset \mathbb{A}^3$ in the spirit of (3.11, b). Find out whether $V(f, g)$, $V(f, h)$ and $V(g, h)$ have any other interesting components.

3.11   设 $f = v^2 - uw$，$g = u^4 - vw$，$h = w^2 - u^3 v$。仿照 (3.11, b) 的方式确定簇 $V(f, g, h) \subset \mathbb{A}^3$。考察 $V(f, g)$、$V(f, h)$ 和 $V(g, h)$ 是否有其他有趣的分支。

3.12

(i) Prove that for any field $k$, an algebraic set in $\mathbb{A}^1_k$ is either finite or the whole of $\mathbb{A}^1_k$. Deduce that the Zariski topology is the cofinite/kəʊˈfaɪ.naɪt/ 余有限的 topology.

(ii) Let $k$ be any field, and $f, g \in k[X, Y]$ irreducible elements, not multiples of one another. Prove that $V(f, g)$ is finite. [Hint: Write $K = k(X)$; prove first that $f, g$ have no common factors in the PID $K[Y]$. Deduce that there exist $p, q \in K[Y]$ such that $pf + qg = 1$; now by clearing denominators in $p, q$, show that there exists $h \in k[X]$ and $a, b \in k[X, Y]$ such that $h = af + bg$. Hence conclude that there are only finitely many possible values of the $X$-coordinate of points of $V(f, g)$.]

(iii) Prove that any algebraic set $V \subset \mathbb{A}^2_k$ is a finite union of points and curves.

3.12
(i) 证明对任意域 $k$，$\mathbb{A}^1_k$ 中的代数集要么是有限的，要么是整个 $\mathbb{A}^1_k$。推出Zariski拓扑就是余有限拓扑。
(ii) 设 $k$ 是任意域，$f, g \in k[X, Y]$ 是不可约元素且不互为倍数。证明 $V(f, g)$ 是有限集。[提示：记 $K = k(X)$；先证 $f, g$ 在PID $K[Y]$ 中没有公因子。推出存在 $p, q \in K[Y]$ 使得 $pf + qg = 1$；然后通分，证明存在 $h \in k[X]$ 和 $a, b \in k[X, Y]$ 使得 $h = af + bg$。由此得出 $V(f, g)$ 的点的 $X$ 坐标只有有限多个可能值。]
(iii) 证明 $\mathbb{A}^2_k$ 中的任意代数集 $V$ 是有限多个点和曲线的并。

3.13

(a) Let $k$ be an infinite field and $f \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$; suppose that $f$ is nonconstant, that is, $f \notin k$. Prove that $V(f) \neq \mathbb{A}^n_k$. [Hint: suppose that $f$ involves $X_n$, and consider $f = \sum a_i(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1}) X_n^i$; now use induction on $n$.]

(b) Now suppose that $k$ is algebraically closed, and let $f$ be as in (a). Suppose that $f$ has degree $m$ in $X_n$, and that its leading term is $a_m(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1})X_n^m$; show that wherever $a_m \neq 0$, there is a finite nonempty set of points of $V(f)$ corresponding to every value of $(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1})$. Deduce in particular that if $n \geq 2$ then $V(f)$ is infinite.

(c) Put together the results of (b) and of Ex. 3.12, (iii) to deduce that if the field $k$ is algebraically closed, then distinct irreducible polynomials $f \in k[X, Y]$ define distinct hypersurfaces of $\mathbb{A}^2_k$ (compare (3.11, a)).

(d) Generalise the result of (c) to $\mathbb{A}^n_k$.

3.13
(a) 设 $k$ 是无限域，$f \in k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$；假设 $f$ 不是常数，即 $f \notin k$。证明 $V(f) \neq \mathbb{A}^n_k$。[提示：假设 $f$ 涉及 $X_n$，考虑 $f = \sum a_i(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1}) X_n^i$；对 $n$ 用归纳法。]
(b) 现在假设 $k$ 是代数闭域，$f$ 如 (a) 所述。假设 $f$ 关于 $X_n$ 的次数为 $m$，首项为 $a_m(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1})X_n^m$；证明在 $a_m \neq 0$ 的地方，对 $(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1})$ 的每个取值，$V(f)$ 上都有一个有限非空的点集与之对应。特别地推出当 $n \geq 2$ 时 $V(f)$ 是无限集。
(c) 将 (b) 和习题 3.12 (iii) 的结果结合起来，推出如果域 $k$ 是代数闭的，则 $k[X, Y]$ 中不同的不可约多项式 $f$ 定义 $\mathbb{A}^2_k$ 的不同超曲面（比较 (3.11, a)）。
(d) 将 (c) 的结果推广到 $\mathbb{A}^n_k$。

3.14   Give an example to show that the proof of Noether normalisation given in (3.13) fails over a finite field $k$. [Hint: find a polynomial $f(X, Y)$ for which $F_d(\alpha, 1) = \alpha^q - \alpha$, so that $F_d(\alpha, 1) = 0$ for all $\alpha \in k$.]

3.14   举例说明 (3.13) 中给出的Noether正规化的证明对有限域 $k$ 失效。[提示：找一个多项式 $f(X, Y)$ 使得 $F_d(\alpha, 1) = \alpha^q - \alpha$，从而对所有 $\alpha \in k$ 都有 $F_d(\alpha, 1) = 0$。]

3.15   Let $A$ be a ring and $A \subset B$ a finite $A$-algebra. Prove that if $\mathfrak{m}$ is a maximal ideal of $A$ then $\mathfrak{m}B \neq B$. [Hint: by contradiction, suppose $B = \mathfrak{m}B$; if $B = \sum Ab_i$ then for each $i$, $b_i = \sum a_{ij} b_j$ with $a_{ij} \in \mathfrak{m}$. Now prove that

$$\Delta = \det(\delta_{ij} - a_{ij}) = 0,$$

and conclude that $1_B \in \mathfrak{m}$, a contradiction. See also [Atiyah and Macdonald, Prop. 2.4 and Cor. 2.5].]

3.15   设 $A$ 是环，$A \subset B$ 是有限 $A$-代数。证明如果 $\mathfrak{m}$ 是 $A$ 的极大理想，则 $\mathfrak{m}B \neq B$。[提示：反证，假设 $B = \mathfrak{m}B$；如果 $B = \sum Ab_i$，则对每个 $i$，$b_i = \sum a_{ij}b_j$，$a_{ij} \in \mathfrak{m}$。证明 $\Delta = \det(\delta_{ij} - a_{ij}) = 0$，从而得出 $1_B \in \mathfrak{m}$，矛盾。另见 [Atiyah and Macdonald, Prop. 2.4 及 Cor. 2.5]。]

3.16   Let $A = k[a_1, \dots, a_n]$ be as in the statement of Noether normalisation (3.13), write $I = \ker\{k[X_1, \dots, X_n] \to k[a_1, \dots, a_n] = A\}$, and consider $V = V(I)$ in $\mathbb{A}^n_k$; assume for simplicity that $I$ is prime.

Let $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$ be general linear forms in $X_1, \dots, X_m$, and write $\pi\colon \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$ for the linear projection defined by $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$; set $p = \pi|_V\colon V \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$. Prove that (i) and (ii) of (3.13) imply that above every $P \in \mathbb{A}^m_k$, $p^{-1}(P)$ is a finite set, and nonempty if $k$ is algebraically closed. [Hint: $I$ contains a monic relation for each $X_i$ over $k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m]$; the finiteness comes easily from this. For the nonemptiness, use Ex. 3.15 to show that for any $P = (b_1, \dots, b_m) \in \mathbb{A}^m_k$, the ideal $J_P = I + (Y_1 - b_1, \dots, Y_m - b_m) \neq k[X_1, \dots, X_m]$. Then apply the nonemptiness assertion of the Nullstellensatz.]

3.16   设 $A = k[a_1, \dots, a_n]$ 如Noether正规化 (3.13) 的条件所述，记 $I = \ker\{k[X_1, \dots, X_n] \to k[a_1, \dots, a_n] = A\}$，考虑 $V = V(I) \subset \mathbb{A}^n_k$；简单起见假设 $I$ 是素理想。

设 $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$ 是 $X_1, \dots, X_m$ 的一般线性形式，记 $\pi\colon \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$ 为由 $Y_1, \dots, Y_m$ 定义的线性投影；令 $p = \pi|_V\colon V \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$。证明 (3.13) 的 (i) 和 (ii) 蕴含：对每个 $P \in \mathbb{A}^m_k$，$p^{-1}(P)$ 是有限集，且当 $k$ 代数闭时非空。[提示：$I$ 包含每个 $X_i$ 在 $k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m]$ 上的首一关系；有限性由此容易得出。对于非空性，利用习题 3.15 证明对任意 $P = (b_1, \dots, b_m) \in \mathbb{A}^m_k$，理想 $J_P = I + (Y_1 - b_1, \dots, Y_m - b_m) \neq k[X_1, \dots, X_m]$。然后应用零点定理的非空性断言。]
