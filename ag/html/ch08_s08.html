8.8 $\mathbb{R}$ versus $\mathbb{C}$

$\mathbb{R}$ 与 $\mathbb{C}$

The reader with real interests may be disappointed that the treatment over $\mathbb{R}$ in §§1--2 gave way in §3 to considerations over an arbitrary field $k$, promptly assumed to be algebraically closed/ˌæl.dʒɪˈbreɪ.ɪ.kli kləʊzd/ 代数闭的. I advise this class of reader to persevere/ˌpɜː.sɪˈvɪər/ 坚持不懈; there are plenty of relations between real and complex geometry, including some that will come as a surprise. Asking about the real points of a real variety is a very hard question, and something of a minority/maɪˈnɒr.ɪ.ti/ 少数的 interest in algebraic geometry; in any case, knowing all about its complex points will usually be an essential prerequisite/priːˈrek.wɪ.zɪt/ 前提条件. Another direct relation between geometry over $\mathbb{R}$ and $\mathbb{C}$ is that an $n$-dimensional nonsingular complex variety is a $2n$-dimensional real manifold/ˈmæn.ɪ.fəʊld/ 流形 -- for example, algebraic surfaces are a principal source of constructions of smooth 4-manifolds.

对实数感兴趣的读者可能会失望，因为在 §§1--2 中对 $\mathbb{R}$ 的处理在 §3 中让位于对任意域 $k$ 的讨论，并迅速假设 $k$ 是代数闭的。我建议这类读者坚持下去；实几何和复几何之间有很多联系，包括一些令人惊喜的关系。询问一个实簇的实点是一个非常困难的问题，在代数几何中属于比较小众的兴趣；无论如何，了解它的复点通常是必不可少的前提。$\mathbb{R}$ 上的几何与 $\mathbb{C}$ 上的几何之间的另一个直接联系是：一个 $n$ 维非奇异复簇是一个 $2n$ 维实流形——例如，代数曲面是构造光滑四维流形的主要来源。

As well as these fairly obvious relations, there are more subtle/ˈsʌt.əl/ 微妙的 ones, for example: (a) singularities of plane curves $C \subset \mathbb{C}^2$ give rise to knots in $S^3$ by intersecting with the boundary of a small ball; and (b) the Penrose twistor construction views a 4-manifold (with a special kind of Riemannian metric) as the set of real valued points of a 4-dimensional complex variety that parametrises/pəˈræm.ɪ.traɪ.zɪz/ 参数化 rational curves on a complex 3-dimensional variety (thus the real 4-sphere $S^4$ we live in can be identified as the real locus in the complex Grassmannian $\operatorname{Gr}(2, 4)$ of lines in $\mathbb{P}^3_\mathbb{C}$).

除了这些相当明显的联系之外，还有更微妙的关系，例如：(a) 平面曲线 $C \subset \mathbb{C}^2$ 的奇点通过与小球的边界相交可以产生 $S^3$ 中的纽结；(b) Penrose旋量构造将一个四维流形（带有特殊类型的Riemann度量）看作一个四维复簇的实值点集，而这个复簇参数化了一个三维复簇上的有理曲线（因此我们生活在其中的实四维球面 $S^4$ 可以被认为是 $\mathbb{P}^3_\mathbb{C}$ 中直线的复Grassmann簇 $\operatorname{Gr}(2, 4)$ 中的实轨迹）。
