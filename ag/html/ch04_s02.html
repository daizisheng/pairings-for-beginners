4.2 $k[V]$ and Algebraic Subsets of $V$

$k[V]$ 与 $V$ 的代数子集

An algebraic set $X \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ is contained in $V$ if and only if $I(X) \supset I(V)$. On the other hand, ideals of $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ containing $I(V)$ are in obvious bijection/baɪˈdʒek.ʃən/ 双射 with ideals of $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]/I(V)$. (Think about this if it's not obvious to you: the ideal $J$ with $I(V) \subset J \subset k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ corresponds to $J/I(V)$; and conversely, an ideal $J_0$ of $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]/I(V)$ corresponds to its inverse/ɪnˈvɜːrs/ 逆的；反向的 image in $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$.)

一个代数集 $X \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 包含在 $V$ 中，当且仅当 $I(X) \supset I(V)$。另一方面，$k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 中包含 $I(V)$ 的理想，与 $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]/I(V)$ 的理想之间有自然的双射。（如果你觉得这不明显，请想一想：理想 $J$（满足 $I(V) \subset J \subset k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$）对应于 $J/I(V)$；反过来，$k[X_1, \dots, X_n]/I(V)$ 的理想 $J_0$ 对应于它在 $k[X_1, \dots, X_n]$ 中的原像。）

Hence the $I$ and $V$ correspondences

$$\{\text{ideals } I \subset k[V]\} \xrightarrow{\;V\;} \{\text{subsets } X \subset V\}$$

by

$$I \longmapsto V(I) = \bigl\{\, P \in V \;\big|\; f(P) = 0 \;\forall f \in I \,\bigr\}$$

and

$$\{\text{ideals } J \subset k[V]\} \xleftarrow{\;I\;} \{\text{subsets } X \subset V\}$$

by

$$I(X) = \bigl\{\, f \in k[V] \;\big|\; f(P) = 0 \;\forall P \in X \,\bigr\} \longleftarrow X$$

are defined as in §3, and have similar properties. In particular $V$ has a Zariski topology, in which the closed sets are the algebraic subsets (this is of course the subspace/ˈsʌb.speɪs/ 子空间 topology of the Zariski topology of $\mathbb{A}^n$).

因此，$I$ 和 $V$ 对应关系 $$\{\text{理想 } I \subset k[V]\} \xrightarrow{\;V\;} \{\text{子集 } X \subset V\}$$ 由 $I \longmapsto V(I) = \{ P \in V \mid f(P) = 0, \;\forall f \in I \}$ 给出，以及 $$\{\ \text{理想 } J \subset k[V]\} \xleftarrow{\;I\;} \{\text{子集 } X \subset V\}$$ 由 $I(X) = \{ f \in k[V] \mid f(P) = 0, \;\forall P \in X \} \longleftarrow X$ 给出， 这与 §3 中的定义方式相同，具有类似的性质。特别地，$V$ 具有一个 Zariski 拓扑，其中闭集就是代数子集（这当然就是 $\mathbb{A}^n$ 的 Zariski 拓扑的子空间拓扑）。

Proposition Let $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ be an algebraic subset. The following conditions are equivalent/ɪˈkwɪv.ə.lənt/ 等价的:

(i) $V$ is irreducible;

(ii) any two open subsets $\emptyset \neq U_1, U_2 \subset V$ have $U_1 \cap U_2 \neq \emptyset$;

(iii) any nonempty open subset $U \subset V$ is dense.

命题 设 $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 是一个代数子集。以下条件等价：
(i) $V$ 是不可约的；
(ii) $V$ 的任意两个非空开子集 $U_1, U_2$ 满足 $U_1 \cap U_2 \neq \emptyset$；
(iii) $V$ 的任意非空开子集 $U$ 是稠密的。

This is all quite trivial/ˈtrɪv.i.əl/ 平凡的: $V$ is irreducible means that $V$ is not a union of two proper closed subsets; (ii) is just a restatement in terms of complements, since

$$U_1 \cap U_2 = \emptyset \iff V = (V - U_1) \cup (V - U_2).$$

A subset of a topological space is dense if and only if it meets every open, so that (iii) is just a restatement of (ii).

这一切都相当平凡：$V$ 不可约意味着 $V$ 不能写成两个真闭子集的并；(ii) 只是用补集的语言重新表述了同样的事，因为 $$U_1 \cap U_2 = \emptyset \iff V = (V - U_1) \cup (V - U_2).$$ 拓扑空间的一个子集是稠密的，当且仅当它与每个开集相交非空，所以 (iii) 只是 (ii) 的另一种说法。
