4.12 Morphisms from an Open Subset of an Affine Variety

从仿射簇的开子集出发的态射

Let $V, W$ be affine varieties, and $U \subset V$ an open subset.

设 $V, W$ 是仿射簇，$U \subset V$ 是一个开子集。

Definition A morphism/ˈmɔːr.fɪ.zəm/ 态射 $f \colon U \to W$ is a rational map $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ such that $U \subset \operatorname{dom} f$, so that $f$ is regular at every $P \in U$.

定义 态射 $f \colon U \to W$ 是一个有理映射 $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$，满足 $U \subset \operatorname{dom} f$，即 $f$ 在 $U$ 中每个点 $P$ 处都是正则的。

If $U_1 \subset V$ and $U_2 \subset W$ are opens, then a morphism $f \colon U_1 \to U_2$ is just a morphism $f \colon U_1 \to W$ such that $f(U_1) \subset U_2$. An isomorphism is a morphism which has a two-sided inverse morphism.

如果 $U_1 \subset V$ 和 $U_2 \subset W$ 是开集，那么态射 $f \colon U_1 \to U_2$ 就是满足 $f(U_1) \subset U_2$ 的态射 $f \colon U_1 \to W$。同构是具有双侧逆态射的态射。

Note that if $V, W$ are affine varieties, then by Theorem 4.8, (II),

$$\bigl\{\text{morphisms } f \colon V \to W\bigr\} = \bigl\{\text{polynomial maps } f \colon V \to W\bigr\};$$

the left-hand side of the equation consists of rational objects subject to regularity/ˌreɡ.jʊˈlær.ɪ.ti/ 正则性 conditions, whereas the right-hand side is more directly in terms of polynomials.

注意如果 $V, W$ 是仿射簇，则由定理 4.8 的 (II)， $$\bigl\{\text{态射 } f \colon V \to W\bigr\} = \bigl\{\text{多项式映射 } f \colon V \to W\bigr\};$$ 等式左边由满足正则性条件的有理对象构成，而右边则更直接地用多项式来表述。

Example The parametrisation of the cuspidal/ˈkʌs.pɪ.dəl/ 尖点的 cubic $\mathbb{A}^1 \to C \colon (Y^2 = X^3)$ of (2.1) induces an isomorphism $\mathbb{A}^1 \setminus \{0\} \cong C \setminus \{(0, 0)\}$; see Ex. 4.5 for details.

例子 (2.1) 中尖点三次曲线的参数化 $\mathbb{A}^1 \to C \colon (Y^2 = X^3)$ 诱导出一个同构 $\mathbb{A}^1 \setminus \{0\} \cong C \setminus \{(0, 0)\}$；详见习题 4.5。
