1.12 Intersection of two conics

两条二次曲线的交

As we have seen above, it often happens that two conics meet in 4 points.

如前所述，两条二次曲线交于4个点是常见的情形。

Conversely/ˈkɒn.vɜːs.li/ 反过来 according to Corollary 1.11, given 4 points $P_1, \ldots, P_4 \in \mathbb{P}^2$, under suitable conditions $S_2(P_1, \ldots, P_4)$ is a 2-dimensional vector space, so choosing a basis $Q_1, Q_2$ for $S_2(P_1, \ldots, P_4)$ gives 2 conics $C_1, C_2$ such that $C_1 \cap C_2 = \{P_1, \ldots, P_4\}$.
