8.7 Computation versus Theory

计算与理论

Another point to make concerning the approach in these notes is that quite a lot of emphasis/ˈem.fə.sɪs/ 强调，重点 is given to cases that can be handled by explicit calculations. When general theory proves the existence of some construction, then doing it in terms of explicit coordinate expressions is a useful exercise that helps one to keep a grip on reality, and this is appropriate for an undergraduate textbook. This should not however be allowed to obscure/əbˈskjʊər/ 遮蔽，使模糊 the fact that the theory is really designed to handle the complicated cases, when explicit computations will often not tell us anything.

关于这些讲义的方法，还有一点需要指出：相当多的篇幅给予了能用显式计算处理的情形。当一般理论证明了某个构造的存在时，用显式坐标表达式来实现它是一个有用的练习，有助于保持对实际的把握，这对一本本科教材来说是合适的。然而，不应该因此就忽略这样一个事实：理论真正旨在处理的是复杂的情形，此时显式计算往往什么也告诉不了我们。
