3.14 Remarks

3.14 注记

(I) In fact, the proof of (3.13) shows that $y_1, \dots, y_m$ can be chosen to be $m$ general linear forms in $a_1, \dots, a_n$. To understand the significance of (3.13), write $I = \ker\{k[X_1, \dots, X_n] \to k[a_1, \dots, a_n] = A\}$, and assume for simplicity that $I$ is prime. Consider $V = V(I) \subset \mathbb{A}^n_k$; let $\pi\colon \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$ be the linear projection/prəˈdʒek.ʃən/ 投影 defined by $y_1, \dots, y_m$, and $p = \pi|_V\colon V \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$. It can be seen that the conclusions (i) and (ii) of (3.13) imply that above every $P \in \mathbb{A}^m_k$, $p^{-1}(P)$ is a finite nonempty set (see Ex. 3.16).

(I) 事实上，(3.13) 的证明表明 $y_1, \dots, y_m$ 可以取为 $a_1, \dots, a_n$ 的 $m$ 个一般线性形式。为理解 (3.13) 的意义，写 $I = \ker\{k[X_1, \dots, X_n] \to k[a_1, \dots, a_n] = A\}$，简单起见假设 $I$ 是素理想。考虑 $V = V(I) \subset \mathbb{A}^n_k$；设 $\pi\colon \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$ 是由 $y_1, \dots, y_m$ 定义的线性投影，$p = \pi|_V\colon V \to \mathbb{A}^m_k$。可以看出 (3.13) 的结论 (i) 和 (ii) 意味着在每个 $P \in \mathbb{A}^m_k$ 的上方，$p^{-1}(P)$ 是一个有限非空集合（见习题 3.16）。

(II) The proof of (3.13) has also a simple geometric interpretation: choosing $n-1$ linear forms in the $n$ variables $X_1, \dots, X_n$ corresponds to making a linear projection $\pi\colon \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \mathbb{A}^{n-1}_k$; the fibres/ˈfaɪ.bəz/ 纤维 of $\pi$ then form an $(n-1)$-dimensional family of parallel lines. Having chosen the polynomial $f \in I$, it is not hard to see that $f$ gives rise to a monic relation in the final $X_n$ if and only if none of the parallel lines are asymptotes/ˈæs.ɪm.təʊts/ 渐近线 of the variety $(f = 0)$; in terms of projective geometry, this means that the point at infinity $(0, \alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}, 1) \in \mathbb{P}^{n-1}_k$ specifying the parallel projection does not belong to the projective/prəˈdʒek.tɪv/ 射影的 closure of $(f = 0)$.

(II) (3.13) 的证明也有一个简单的几何解释：在 $n$ 个变量 $X_1, \dots, X_n$ 中选 $n-1$ 个线性形式，对应于做一个线性投影 $\pi\colon \mathbb{A}^n_k \to \mathbb{A}^{n-1}_k$；$\pi$ 的纤维构成一族 $(n-1)$ 维的平行线。选定多项式 $f \in I$ 之后，不难看出 $f$ 给出关于 $X_n$ 的首一关系式当且仅当这些平行线中没有一条是簇 $(f = 0)$ 的渐近线；用射影几何的语言来说，就是指定平行投影的无穷远点 $(0, \alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}, 1) \in \mathbb{P}^{n-1}_k$ 不属于 $(f = 0)$ 的射影闭包。

(III) The above proof of (3.13) does not work for a finite field (see Ex. 3.14). However, the theorem itself is true without any condition on $k$ (see [Mumford, Introduction, p. 4] or [Atiyah and Macdonald, (7.9)]).

(III) 上面 (3.13) 的证明对有限域不适用（见习题 3.14）。但定理本身不需要对 $k$ 加任何条件就成立（见 [Mumford, Introduction, p. 4] 或 [Atiyah and Macdonald, (7.9)]）。
