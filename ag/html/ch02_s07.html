2.7 Corollary: cubic through 8 points

2.7 推论：过8个点的三次曲线

Corollary 2.7   Let $C_1, C_2$ be two cubic curves whose intersection consists of 9 distinct points, $C_1 \cap C_2 = \{P_1, \ldots, P_9\}$. Then a cubic $D$ through $P_1, \ldots, P_8$ also passes through $P_9$.

推论 2.7   设 $C_1, C_2$ 是两条三次曲线，它们的交集由 9 个不同的点组成，$C_1 \cap C_2 = \{P_1, \ldots, P_9\}$。则过 $P_1, \ldots, P_8$ 的三次曲线 $D$ 也过 $P_9$。

Proof   If 4 of the points $P_1, \ldots, P_8$ were on a line $L$, then each of $C_1$ and $C_2$ would meet $L$ in $\geq 4$ points, and thus contain $L$, which contradicts/ˌkɒn.trəˈdɪkts/ 矛盾 the assumption on $C_1 \cap C_2$. For exactly the same reason, no 7 of the points can be conconic. Therefore the assumptions of (2.6) are satisfied, so I can conclude that

$$\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) = 2;$$

this means that the equations $F_1, F_2$ of the two cubics $C_1, C_2$ form a basis/ˈbeɪ.sɪs/ 基 of $S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8)$, and hence $D : (G = 0)$, where $G = \lambda F_1 + \mu F_2$. Now $F_1, F_2$ vanish/ˈvæn.ɪʃ/ 消失，为零 at $P_9$, hence so does $G$.   Q.E.D.

证明   如果 $P_1, \ldots, P_8$ 中有 4 个点在一条直线 $L$ 上，那么 $C_1$ 和 $C_2$ 各自与 $L$ 交于 $\geq 4$ 个点，因此都包含 $L$，这与 $C_1 \cap C_2$ 的假设矛盾。出于完全相同的原因，没有 7 个点共圆锥曲线。所以 (2.6) 的假设被满足，我可以得出 $$\dim S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8) = 2；$$ 这意味着两条三次曲线 $C_1, C_2$ 的方程 $F_1, F_2$ 构成 $S_3(P_1, \ldots, P_8)$ 的一组基，因此 $D : (G = 0)$，其中 $G = \lambda F_1 + \mu F_2$。由于 $F_1, F_2$ 在 $P_9$ 处为零，$G$ 也是。   Q.E.D.

This corollary is a beautiful and surprising result: two cubics meet in 9 points, and any cubic through 8 of them is forced/fɔːrst/ 被迫的 to pass through the 9th. It is the key ingredient in the proof of the group law on a cubic curve.

这个推论是一个漂亮又令人惊讶的结果：两条三次曲线交于 9 个点，而过其中 8 个点的任意三次曲线必然也过第 9 个点。它是证明三次曲线上群律的关键工具。
