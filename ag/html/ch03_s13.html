3.13 Noether normalisation

3.13 Noether正规化

Theorem (Noether normalisation lemma)   Let $k$ be an infinite field, and $A = k[a_1, \dots, a_n]$ a finitely generated $k$-algebra. Then there exist $m \leq n$ and $y_1, \dots, y_m \in A$ such that

(i) $y_1, \dots, y_m$ are algebraically/ˌæl.dʒɪˈbreɪ.ɪ.kəl.i/ 代数地 independent over $k$; and

(ii) $A$ is a finite $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$-algebra.

((i) means as usual that there are no nonzero polynomial relations holding between the $y_i$; an algebraist's way of saying this is that the natural (surjective/sɜːˈdʒek.tɪv/ 满射的) map $k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m] \to k[y_1, \dots, y_m] \subset A$ is injective.)

定理（Noether正规化引理）   设 $k$ 是无限域，$A = k[a_1, \dots, a_n]$ 是有限生成的 $k$-代数。则存在 $m \leq n$ 和 $y_1, \dots, y_m \in A$ 使得：
(i) $y_1, \dots, y_m$ 在 $k$ 上代数无关；
(ii) $A$ 是有限 $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$-代数。

（(i) 的意思照例是 $y_i$ 之间没有非零多项式关系；代数学家的说法是自然的满射 $k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m] \to k[y_1, \dots, y_m] \subset A$ 是单射。）

It is being asserted/əˈsɜː.tɪd/ 断言的 that, as you might expect, the extension of rings can be built up by first throwing in algebraically independent elements, then 'making an algebraic extension'; however, the statement (ii) is far more precise than this, since it says that every element of $A$ is not just algebraic over $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$, but satisfies a monic equation over it.

这里断言的是，正如你可能预期的那样，环的扩张可以通过先加入代数无关的元素、再"做一个代数扩张"来构建；然而，(ii) 的陈述比这精确得多，因为它说 $A$ 的每个元素不仅仅在 $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$ 上是代数的，而且满足其上的首一方程。

Proof   Let $I$ be the kernel of the natural surjection/sɜːˈdʒek.ʃən/ 满射,

$$I = \ker\big\{k[X_1, \dots, X_n] \to k[a_1, \dots, a_n] = A\big\}.$$

Suppose that $0 \neq f \in I$; the idea of the proof is to replace $X_1, \dots, X_{n-1}$ by certain $X_1', \dots, X_{n-1}'$ so that $f$ becomes a monic equation for $a_n$ over $A' = k[a_1', \dots, a_{n-1}']$. So write

$$a_1' = a_1 - \alpha_1 a_n, \quad \dots, \quad a_{n-1}' = a_{n-1} - \alpha_{n-1} a_n$$

(where the $\alpha_i \in k$ are elements to be specified later). Then

$$0 = f(a_1' + \alpha_1 a_n, \dots, a_{n-1}' + \alpha_{n-1} a_n, a_n).$$

Claim   For suitable choice of $\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1} \in k$, the polynomial

$$f(X_1' + \alpha_1 X_n, \dots, X_{n-1}' + \alpha_{n-1} X_n, X_n)$$

is monic in $X_n$.

证明   设 $I$ 为自然满射的核， $$I = \ker\big\{k[X_1, \dots, X_n] \to k[a_1, \dots, a_n] = A\big\}.$$ 假设 $0 \neq f \in I$；证明的思路是将 $X_1, \dots, X_{n-1}$ 替换为某些 $X_1', \dots, X_{n-1}'$，使得 $f$ 变成 $a_n$ 在 $A' = k[a_1', \dots, a_{n-1}']$ 上的首一方程。于是写 $$a_1' = a_1 - \alpha_1 a_n, \quad \dots, \quad a_{n-1}' = a_{n-1} - \alpha_{n-1} a_n$$ （其中 $\alpha_i \in k$ 是待定元素）。则 $$0 = f(a_1' + \alpha_1 a_n, \dots, a_{n-1}' + \alpha_{n-1} a_n, a_n).$$ 断言   对 $\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1} \in k$ 的适当选取，多项式 $f(X_1' + \alpha_1 X_n, \dots, X_{n-1}' + \alpha_{n-1} X_n, X_n)$ 关于 $X_n$ 是首一的。

Using the claim, the theorem is proved by induction/ɪnˈdʌk.ʃən/ 归纳法 on $n$: if $I = 0$ then there's nothing to prove, since $a_1, \dots, a_n$ are algebraically independent. Otherwise, pick $0 \neq f \in I$, and let $\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}$ be as in the claim; then $f$ gives a monic relation satisfied by $a_n$ with coefficients in $A' = k[a_1', \dots, a_{n-1}'] \subset A$. By the inductive/ɪnˈdʌk.tɪv/ 归纳的 assumption, there exist $y_1, \dots, y_m \in A'$ such that

(1) $y_1, \dots, y_m$ are algebraically independent over $k$;

(2) $A'$ is a finite $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$-algebra.

Then $A = A'[a_n]$ is finite over $A'$ (by (3.12, iii)), so by (3.12, i), $A$ is finite over $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$, proving the theorem.

利用这个断言，定理可以对 $n$ 用归纳法证明：如果 $I = 0$，则没什么要证的，因为 $a_1, \dots, a_n$ 代数无关。否则，取 $0 \neq f \in I$，令 $\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}$ 如断言所述；则 $f$ 给出 $a_n$ 在 $A' = k[a_1', \dots, a_{n-1}'] \subset A$ 上满足的首一关系式。由归纳假设，存在 $y_1, \dots, y_m \in A'$ 使得：(1) $y_1, \dots, y_m$ 在 $k$ 上代数无关；(2) $A'$ 是有限 $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$-代数。则 $A = A'[a_n]$ 在 $A'$ 上是有限的（由 (3.12, iii)），从而由 (3.12, i)，$A$ 在 $k[y_1, \dots, y_m]$ 上是有限的，定理证毕。

It only remains to prove the claim. Let $d = \deg f$, and write

$$f = F_d + G,$$

with $F_d$ homogeneous/ˌhɒm.əˈdʒiː.ni.əs/ 齐次的 of degree $d$, and $\deg G \leq d - 1$. Then

$$\begin{aligned} f(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1}, X_n) &= f(X_1' + \alpha_1 X_n, \dots, X_{n-1}' + \alpha_{n-1} X_n, X_n) \\ &= F_d(\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}, 1) \cdot X_n^d \\ &\quad + (\text{stuff involving } X_n \text{ to power} \leq d-1); \end{aligned}$$

I'm now home provided that $F_d(\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}, 1) \neq 0$. Since $F_d$ is a nonzero polynomial, it's not hard to check that this is the case for 'almost all' values of $\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}$ (the proof of this is discussed in Ex. 3.13).   Q.E.D.

只剩下证明断言。设 $d = \deg f$，写 $f = F_d + G$，其中 $F_d$ 是 $d$ 次齐次部分，$\deg G \leq d-1$。则 $$f(X_1, \dots, X_{n-1}, X_n) = F_d(\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}, 1) \cdot X_n^d + (\text{关于 } X_n \text{ 的次数} \leq d-1 \text{ 的项});$$ 只要 $F_d(\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}, 1) \neq 0$ 就大功告成了。由于 $F_d$ 是非零多项式，不难验证对"几乎所有" $\alpha_1, \dots, \alpha_{n-1}$ 的取值都满足这一条件（这一点的证明见习题 3.13）。证毕。
