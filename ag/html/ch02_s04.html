2.4 Linear systems

2.4 线性系

Write $S_d = \{\text{forms of degree } d \text{ in } (X, Y, Z)\}$; (recall that a form is just a homogeneous/ˌhɒm.əˈdʒiː.ni.əs/ 齐次的 polynomial). Any element $F \in S_d$ can be written in a unique way as

$$F = \sum a_{ijk} X^i Y^j Z^k$$

with $a_{ijk} \in k$, and the sum taken over all $i, j, k \geq 0$ with $i + j + k = d$; this means of course that $S_d$ is a $k$-vector space with basis

$$\begin{array}{c} Z^d \\ XZ^{d-1} \quad YZ^{d-1} \\ \cdots \quad \cdots \\ X^{d-1}Z \quad X^{d-2}YZ \quad \cdots \quad XY^{d-2}Z \\ X^d \quad X^{d-1}Y \quad X^{d-2}Y^2 \quad \cdots \quad Y^d \end{array}$$

and in particular, $\dim S_d = \binom{d+2}{2}$. For $P_1, \ldots, P_n \in \mathbb{P}^2$, let

$$S_d(P_1, \ldots, P_n) = \bigl\{ F \in S_d \;\big|\; F(P_i) = 0 \text{ for } i = 1, \ldots, n \bigr\} \subset S_d.$$

Each of the conditions $F(P_i) = 0$ (more precisely, $F(X_i, Y_i, Z_i) = 0$, where $P_i = (X_i : Y_i : Z_i)$) is one linear condition on $F$, so that $S_d(P_1, \ldots, P_n)$ is a vector space of dimension/dɪˈmen.ʃən/ 维数 $\geq \binom{d+2}{2} - n$.

记 $S_d = \{\text{(X,Y,Z) 的 } d \text{ 次齐次式}\}$；（回忆一下，齐次式就是齐次多项式。）$S_d$ 的任意元素 $F$ 可以唯一地写成 $$F = \sum a_{ijk} X^i Y^j Z^k，$$ 其中 $a_{ijk} \in k$，求和遍历所有 $i, j, k \geq 0$ 且 $i+j+k = d$；这当然意味着 $S_d$ 是一个 $k$-向量空间，基底排列成三角形，特别地，$\dim S_d = \binom{d+2}{2}$。对 $P_1, \ldots, P_n \in \mathbb{P}^2$，令 $$S_d(P_1, \ldots, P_n) = \bigl\{ F \in S_d \;\big|\; F(P_i) = 0,\; i = 1, \ldots, n \bigr\} \subset S_d.$$ 每个条件 $F(P_i) = 0$（准确地说是 $F(X_i, Y_i, Z_i) = 0$，其中 $P_i = (X_i:Y_i:Z_i)$）是对 $F$ 的一个线性条件，所以 $S_d(P_1, \ldots, P_n)$ 是维数 $\geq \binom{d+2}{2} - n$ 的向量空间。
