6.6 Corollary–Definition: $\dim T_P V$

6.6 推论-定义：$\dim T_P V$

Corollary–Definition 6.6   There exists an integer $r$ and a dense open subset $V_0 \subset V$ such that

$$\dim T_P V = r \text{ for } P \in V_0, \quad \text{and} \quad \dim T_P V \geq r \text{ for all } P \in V.$$

Define $r$ to be the dimension/dɪˈmen.ʃən/ 维数 of $V$, and write $\dim V = r$; and say that $P \in V$ is nonsingular if $\dim T_P V = r$, and singular if $\dim T_P V > r$. A variety $V$ is nonsingular if it is nonsingular at each point $P \in V$.

推论-定义 6.6：存在整数 $r$ 和稠密开子集 $V_0 \subset V$，使得 $$\dim T_P V = r \text{ 对 } P \in V_0, \quad \text{且} \quad \dim T_P V \geq r \text{ 对所有 } P \in V.$$ 定义 $r$ 为 $V$ 的维数，记 $\dim V = r$；如果 $\dim T_P V = r$，则称 $P \in V$ 为非奇异的；如果 $\dim T_P V > r$，则称 $P$ 为奇异的。如果簇 $V$ 在每个点 $P \in V$ 都是非奇异的，则称 $V$ 是非奇异簇。

Proof   Let $r = \min\{\dim T_P V\}$, taken over all points $P \in V$. Then clearly

$$S(r-1) = \emptyset, \quad S(r) = V, \quad \text{and} \quad S(r+1) \subsetneq V;$$

therefore $S(r) \setminus S(r+1) = \{P \in V \mid \dim T_P V = r\}$ is open and nonempty.   Q.E.D.

证明：取 $r = \min\{\dim T_P V\}$，遍取 $V$ 的所有点 $P$。则显然 $$S(r-1) = \emptyset, \quad S(r) = V, \quad \text{且} \quad S(r+1) \subsetneq V;$$ 因此 $S(r) \setminus S(r+1) = \{P \in V \mid \dim T_P V = r\}$ 是开集且非空。Q.E.D.
