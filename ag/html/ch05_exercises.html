Exercises to Chapter 5

第5章习题

5.1   Prove that a regular function on $\mathbb{P}^1$ is a constant. [Hint: use the notation of (5.0); suppose that $f \in k(\mathbb{P}^1)$ is regular at every point of $\mathbb{P}^1$. Apply (4.8, II) to the affine piece $\mathbb{A}^1_{(0)}$, to show that $f = p(x_0) \in k[x_0]$; on the other affine piece $\mathbb{A}^1_{(\infty)}$, $f = p(1/y_1) \in k[y_1]$. Now, how can it happen that $p(1/y_1)$ is a polynomial?] Deduce that there are no nonconstant morphisms $\mathbb{P}^1 \to \mathbb{A}^m$ for any $m$.

5.1   证明 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 上的正则函数是常数。[提示：用 (5.0) 的记号；假设 $f \in k(\mathbb{P}^1)$ 在 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 的每个点正则。对仿射片 $\mathbb{A}^1_{(0)}$ 应用 (4.8, II)，说明 $f = p(x_0) \in k[x_0]$；在另一仿射片 $\mathbb{A}^1_{(\infty)}$ 上，$f = p(1/y_1) \in k[y_1]$。那么 $p(1/y_1)$ 什么时候才是多项式呢？] 由此推出不存在非常值态射 $\mathbb{P}^1 \to \mathbb{A}^m$（任意 $m$）。

5.2   The quadric surface in $\mathbb{P}^3$.

(i) Show that the Segre embedding of $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ (as in (5.10)) gives an isomorphism of $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ with the quadric

$$S_{1,1} = Q : (X_0 X_3 = X_1 X_2) \subset \mathbb{P}^3.$$

(ii) What are the images in $Q$ of the two families of lines $\{p\} \times \mathbb{P}^1$ and $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \{p\}$ in $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$? Use this to find some disjoint/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/ 不相交的 lines in $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$, and conclude from this that $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1 \not\cong \mathbb{P}^2$. (The fact that a quadric surface has two rulings by straight lines has applications in civil engineering: if you're trying to build a curved surface out of concrete/ˈkɒŋ.kriːt/ 混凝土, it's an obvious advantage to be able to determine the shape of the surface by imposing linear constraints/kənˈstreɪnts/ 约束. See [M. Berger, 14.4.6–7 and 15.3.3] for a discussion and pictures.)

(iii) Show that there are two lines of $Q$ passing through the point $P = (1,0,0,0)$, and that the complement $U$ of these two lines is the image of $\mathbb{A}^1 \times \mathbb{A}^1$ under the Segre embedding.

(iv) Show that under the projection $\pi|_Q \colon Q \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^2$ (in the notation of (5.7, II)), $U$ maps isomorphically to a copy of $\mathbb{A}^2$, and the two lines through $P$ are mapped to two points of $\mathbb{P}^2$.

(v) In the notation of (5.7, II), find $\operatorname{dom} \pi$ and $\operatorname{dom} \varphi$, and give a geometric interpretation of the singularities of $\pi$ and $\varphi$.

5.2   $\mathbb{P}^3$ 中的二次曲面。

(i) 说明 $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ 的 Segre 嵌入（如 (5.10)）给出 $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ 与二次曲面 $S_{1,1} = Q : (X_0 X_3 = X_1 X_2) \subset \mathbb{P}^3$ 的同构。

(ii) $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ 中两族直线 $\{p\} \times \mathbb{P}^1$ 和 $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \{p\}$ 在 $Q$ 中的像是什么？用它们找到 $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1$ 中不相交的直线，从而推出 $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1 \not\cong \mathbb{P}^2$。（二次曲面有两族直线这一事实在土木工程中有应用：如果要用混凝土建一个曲面，能通过施加线性约束来确定曲面形状显然是个优势。参见 [M. Berger, 14.4.6–7 和 15.3.3]。）

(iii) 说明过点 $P = (1,0,0,0)$ 有两条 $Q$ 上的直线，且这两条直线的补集 $U$ 是 $\mathbb{A}^1 \times \mathbb{A}^1$ 在 Segre 嵌入下的像。

(iv) 说明在投影 $\pi|_Q \colon Q \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^2$ 下（用 (5.7, II) 的记号），$U$ 同构地映到 $\mathbb{A}^2$ 的一个副本，而过 $P$ 的两条直线映到 $\mathbb{P}^2$ 的两个点。

(v) 用 (5.7, II) 的记号，找出 $\operatorname{dom} \pi$ 和 $\operatorname{dom} \varphi$，并给出 $\pi$ 和 $\varphi$ 奇异性的几何解释。

5.3   Which of the following expressions define rational maps $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^n \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^m$ (with $n, m = 1$ or $2$) between projective spaces of the appropriate dimensions? In each case, determine $\operatorname{dom} \varphi$, say if $\varphi$ is birational, and if so describe the inverse map.

(a) $(x, y, z) \mapsto (x, y)$;   (b) $(x, y) \mapsto (x, y, 1)$;   (c) $(x, y) \mapsto (x, y, 0)$;

(d) $(x, y, z) \mapsto (1/x, 1/y, 1/z)$;   (e) $(x, y, z) \mapsto ((x^3 + y^3)/z^3, \; y^2/z^2, \; 1)$;

(f) $(x, y, z) \mapsto (x^2 + y^2, \; y^2, \; y^2)$.

5.3   下面哪些表达式定义了适当维数的射影空间之间的有理映射 $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^n \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^m$（$n, m = 1$ 或 $2$）？对每种情况，确定 $\operatorname{dom} \varphi$，判断 $\varphi$ 是否双有理，若是则描述逆映射。

(a) $(x,y,z) \mapsto (x,y)$；(b) $(x,y) \mapsto (x,y,1)$；(c) $(x,y) \mapsto (x,y,0)$；

(d) $(x,y,z) \mapsto (1/x,1/y,1/z)$；(e) $(x,y,z) \mapsto ((x^3+y^3)/z^3, y^2/z^2, 1)$；

(f) $(x,y,z) \mapsto (x^2+y^2, y^2, y^2)$。

5.4   The rational normal curve (see (5.7, I)) of degree 3 is the curve $C \subset \mathbb{P}^3$ defined by the 3 quadrics $C = Q_1 \cap Q_2 \cap Q_3$, where

$$Q_1 : (XZ = Y^2), \quad Q_2 : (XT = YZ), \quad Q_3 : (YT = Z^2);$$

this curve is also well known as the twisted cubic, where 'twisted' refers to the fact that it is not a plane curve. Check that for any two of the quadrics $Q_i, Q_j$, the intersection $Q_i \cap Q_j = C \cup \ell_{ij}$, where $\ell_{ij}$ is a certain line. So this curve in 3-space is not the intersection of any 2 of the quadrics.

5.4   3 次有理正规曲线（见 (5.7, I)）是 $\mathbb{P}^3$ 中由三个二次曲面 $C = Q_1 \cap Q_2 \cap Q_3$ 定义的曲线，其中 $$Q_1 : (XZ = Y^2), \quad Q_2 : (XT = YZ), \quad Q_3 : (YT = Z^2);$$ 这条曲线也叫扭三次曲线（twisted cubic），"扭"是指它不是平面曲线。验证对任意两个 $Q_i, Q_j$，交集 $Q_i \cap Q_j = C \cup \ell_{ij}$，其中 $\ell_{ij}$ 是某条直线。所以这条三维空间中的曲线不是任何两个二次曲面的交。

5.5   Let $Q_1 : (XZ = Y^2)$ and $F : (XT^2 - 2YZT + Z^3 = 0)$; prove that $C = Q_1 \cap F$ is the twisted cubic curve of Ex. 5.4. [Hint: start by multiplying $F$ by $X$; subtracting a suitable multiple of $Q_1$, this becomes a perfect square.]

5.5   设 $Q_1 : (XZ = Y^2)$ 和 $F : (XT^2 - 2YZT + Z^3 = 0)$；证明 $C = Q_1 \cap F$ 就是习题 5.4 的扭三次曲线。[提示：先用 $X$ 乘 $F$；减去 $Q_1$ 的适当倍数后变成完全平方。]

5.6   Let $C \subset \mathbb{P}^3$ be an irreducible curve defined by $C = Q_1 \cap Q_2$, where $Q_1 : (TX = q_1)$, $Q_2 : (TY = q_2)$, with $q_1, q_2$ quadratic forms in $X, Y, Z$. Show that the projection $\pi \colon \mathbb{P}^3 \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^2$ defined by $(X, Y, Z, T) \mapsto (X, Y, Z)$ restricts to an isomorphism of $C$ with the plane curve $D \subset \mathbb{P}^2$ given by $Xq_2 = Yq_1$.

5.6   设 $C \subset \mathbb{P}^3$ 是由 $C = Q_1 \cap Q_2$ 定义的不可约曲线，其中 $Q_1 : (TX = q_1)$，$Q_2 : (TY = q_2)$，$q_1, q_2$ 是 $X, Y, Z$ 的二次型。证明投影 $\pi \colon \mathbb{P}^3 \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^2$（$(X,Y,Z,T) \mapsto (X,Y,Z)$）限制到 $C$ 上给出 $C$ 与平面曲线 $D \subset \mathbb{P}^2$（由 $Xq_2 = Yq_1$ 给出）的同构。

5.7   Let $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^1 \to \mathbb{P}^1$ be an isomorphism; identify the graph of $\varphi$ as a subvariety of $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1 \cong Q \subset \mathbb{P}^3$. Now do the same if $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^1 \to \mathbb{P}^1$ is the two-to-one map given by $(X, Y) \mapsto (X^2, Y^2)$.

5.7   设 $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^1 \to \mathbb{P}^1$ 是同构；把 $\varphi$ 的图像看作 $\mathbb{P}^1 \times \mathbb{P}^1 \cong Q \subset \mathbb{P}^3$ 的子簇。再对 $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^1 \to \mathbb{P}^1$ 是由 $(X,Y) \mapsto (X^2, Y^2)$ 给出的二对一映射做同样的事。

5.8   Prove that any irreducible quadric $Q \subset \mathbb{P}^{n+1}$ is rational; that is, as in the picture of (5.7, II), show that if $P \in Q$ is a nonsingular point, then the linear projection of $\mathbb{P}^{n+1}$ to $\mathbb{P}^n$ induces a birational map $Q \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^n$.

5.8   证明任何不可约二次超曲面 $Q \subset \mathbb{P}^{n+1}$ 是有理的；即如 (5.7, II) 的图示，若 $P \in Q$ 是非奇异点，则从 $\mathbb{P}^{n+1}$ 到 $\mathbb{P}^n$ 的线性投影诱导双有理映射 $Q \dashrightarrow \mathbb{P}^n$。

5.9   For each of the following plane curves, write down the 3 standard affine pieces, and determine the intersection of the curve with the 3 coordinate axes:

(a) $y^2 z = x^3 + axz^2 + bz^3$;   (b) $x^2 y^2 + x^2 z^2 + y^2 z^2 = 2xyz(x + y + z)$;   (c) $xz^3 = (x^2 + z^2)y^2$.

5.9   对下面每条平面曲线，写出 3 个标准仿射片，并确定曲线与 3 条坐标轴的交：

(a) $y^2 z = x^3 + axz^2 + bz^3$；(b) $x^2y^2 + x^2z^2 + y^2z^2 = 2xyz(x+y+z)$；(c) $xz^3 = (x^2+z^2)y^2$。

5.10

(i) Prove that the product of two irreducible algebraic sets is again irreducible. [Hint: the subsets $V \times \{w\}$ are irreducible for $w \in W$; given an expression $V \times W = U_1 \cup U_2$, consider the subsets $W_i = \{w \in W \mid V \times \{w\} \subset U_i\}$ for $i = 1, 2$.]

(ii) Describe the closed sets of the topology on $\mathbb{A}^2 = \mathbb{A}^1 \times \mathbb{A}^1$ which is the product of the Zariski topologies on the two factors; now find a closed subset of the Zariski topology of $\mathbb{A}^2$ not of this form.

5.10

(i) 证明两个不可约代数集的乘积仍不可约。[提示：$V \times \{w\}$ 对 $w \in W$ 不可约；给定 $V \times W = U_1 \cup U_2$，考虑子集 $W_i = \{w \in W \mid V \times \{w\} \subset U_i\}$（$i = 1,2$）。]

(ii) 描述 $\mathbb{A}^2 = \mathbb{A}^1 \times \mathbb{A}^1$ 上两个因子 Zariski 拓扑的乘积拓扑的闭集；然后找一个 $\mathbb{A}^2$ 的 Zariski 拓扑的闭集不属于这种形式。

5.11

(a) If $\mathbb{A}^n_{(0)}$ and $\mathbb{A}^m_{(0)}$ are standard affine pieces of $\mathbb{P}^n$ and $\mathbb{P}^m$ respectively, verify that the Segre embedding of (5.11) maps $\mathbb{A}^n_{(0)} \times \mathbb{A}^m_{(0)}$ isomorphically to an affine piece of the variety $S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N$, say $S_{(0)} \subset \mathbb{A}^N$, and that the $N$ coordinates of $\mathbb{A}^N$ restrict to $X_1, \ldots, X_n, Y_1, \ldots, Y_m$ and the $nm$ terms $X_i Y_j$.

(b) If $V \subset \mathbb{P}^n$ and $W \subset \mathbb{P}^m$, prove that the product $V \times W$ is a projective subvariety of $\mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m = S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N$. [Hint: the product of the affine pieces $V_{(0)} \times W_{(0)} \subset \mathbb{A}^{n+m}$ is a subvariety defined by polynomials as explained in (5.11); show that each of these is the restriction to $\mathbb{A}^{n+m} \cong S_{(0)}$ of a homogeneous polynomial in the $U_{ij}$.]

5.11

(a) 如果 $\mathbb{A}^n_{(0)}$ 和 $\mathbb{A}^m_{(0)}$ 分别是 $\mathbb{P}^n$ 和 $\mathbb{P}^m$ 的标准仿射片，验证 (5.11) 的 Segre 嵌入将 $\mathbb{A}^n_{(0)} \times \mathbb{A}^m_{(0)}$ 同构地映到簇 $S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ 的一个仿射片 $S_{(0)} \subset \mathbb{A}^N$，且 $\mathbb{A}^N$ 的 $N$ 个坐标限制为 $X_1, \ldots, X_n$，$Y_1, \ldots, Y_m$ 以及 $nm$ 项 $X_i Y_j$。

(b) 若 $V \subset \mathbb{P}^n$，$W \subset \mathbb{P}^m$，证明乘积 $V \times W$ 是 $\mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m = S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ 的射影子簇。[提示：仿射片的乘积 $V_{(0)} \times W_{(0)} \subset \mathbb{A}^{n+m}$ 是如 (5.11) 所述由多项式定义的子簇；说明其中每个多项式是 $U_{ij}$ 的齐次多项式限制到 $\mathbb{A}^{n+m} \cong S_{(0)}$ 上的结果。]

5.12   Let $C$ be the cubic curve of (5.0); prove that any regular function $f$ on $C$ is constant. Proceed in the following steps:

Step 1   Applying (4.8, II) to the affine piece $C_{(0)}$, write $f = p(x,y) \in k[x,y]$.

Step 2   Subtracting a suitable multiple of the relation $y^2 - x^3 - ax - b$, assume that $p(x,y) = q(x) + yr(x)$, with $q, r \in k[x]$.

Step 3   Applying (4.8, II) to the affine piece $C_{(\infty)}$ gives $f = q(x_1/z_1) + (1/z_1)r(x_1/z_1) \in k[C_{(\infty)}]$, and hence there exists a polynomial $S(x_1, z_1)$ such that $q(x_1/z_1) + (1/z_1)r(x_1/z_1) = S(x_1, z_1)$.

Step 4   Clear the denominator, and use the fact that $k[C_{(\infty)}] = k[x_1, z_1]/g$, where $g = z_1 - x_1^3 - ax_1 z_1^2 - bz_1^3$, to deduce a polynomial identity

$$Q_m(x_1, z_1) + R_{m-1}(x_1, z_1) \equiv S(x_1, z_1) z_1^m + A(x_1, z_1) g$$

in $k[x_1, z_1]$, with $Q_m$ and $R_{m-1}$ homogeneous of the indicated degrees.

Step 5   Now if we write $S = S^+ + S^-$ and $A = A^+ + A^-$ for the decomposition into terms of even and odd degree, and note that $g$ has only terms of odd degree, this identity splits into two:

$$Q_m \equiv S^+ z_1^m + A^- g \quad \text{and} \quad R_{m-1} \equiv S^- z_1^m + A^+ g$$

if $m$ is even, and an analogous expression if $m$ is odd.

Step 6   $Q_m$ is homogeneous of degree $m$, and hence $A^- g$ has degree $\geq m$; by considering the term of least degree in $A^- g$, prove that $Q_m$ is divisible by $z_1$. Similarly for $R_{m-1}$. By taking the minimum value of $m$ in the identity of Step 4, deduce that $q(x)$ has degree 0 and $r(x) = 0$.

5.12   设 $C$ 是 (5.0) 的三次曲线；证明 $C$ 上的任何正则函数 $f$ 是常数。按以下步骤进行：

第1步   对仿射片 $C_{(0)}$ 应用 (4.8, II)，写出 $f = p(x,y) \in k[x,y]$。

第2步   减去关系式 $y^2 - x^3 - ax - b$ 的适当倍数后，设 $p(x,y) = q(x) + yr(x)$，$q, r \in k[x]$。

第3步   对仿射片 $C_{(\infty)}$ 应用 (4.8, II) 得 $f = q(x_1/z_1) + (1/z_1)r(x_1/z_1) \in k[C_{(\infty)}]$，从而存在多项式 $S(x_1, z_1)$ 使得 $q(x_1/z_1) + (1/z_1)r(x_1/z_1) = S(x_1, z_1)$。

第4步   通分，利用 $k[C_{(\infty)}] = k[x_1, z_1]/g$（其中 $g = z_1 - x_1^3 - ax_1 z_1^2 - bz_1^3$），推出多项式恒等式

$$Q_m(x_1, z_1) + R_{m-1}(x_1, z_1) \equiv S(x_1, z_1) z_1^m + A(x_1, z_1) g$$

其中 $Q_m$ 和 $R_{m-1}$ 是标注次数的齐次多项式。

第5步   将 $S = S^+ + S^-$ 和 $A = A^+ + A^-$ 按偶次和奇次项分解，注意 $g$ 只有奇次项，恒等式分裂为两个：

$$Q_m \equiv S^+ z_1^m + A^- g, \quad R_{m-1} \equiv S^- z_1^m + A^+ g$$

（$m$ 为偶数时；$m$ 为奇数时有类似表达式。）

第6步   $Q_m$ 是 $m$ 次齐次的，因此 $A^- g$ 的次数 $\geq m$；考虑 $A^- g$ 的最低次项，证明 $Q_m$ 被 $z_1$ 整除。$R_{m-1}$ 类似。取第4步恒等式中 $m$ 的最小值，推出 $q(x)$ 是 $0$ 次的且 $r(x) = 0$。

5.13   Veronese surface. Study the embedding $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^2 \to \mathbb{P}^5$ given by $(X, Y, Z) \mapsto (X^2, XY, XZ, Y^2, YZ, Z^2)$; write down the equations defining the image $S = \varphi(\mathbb{P}^2)$, and prove that $\varphi$ is an isomorphism (by writing down the equations of the inverse morphism). Prove that the lines of $\mathbb{P}^2$ go over into conics of $\mathbb{P}^5$, and that conics of $\mathbb{P}^2$ go over into twisted quartics of $\mathbb{P}^5$ (see (5.7)).

For any line $\ell \subset \mathbb{P}^2$, write $\pi(\ell) \subset \mathbb{P}^5$ for the projective plane spanned by the conic $\varphi(\ell)$. Prove that the union of $\pi(\ell)$ taken over all $\ell \subset \mathbb{P}^2$ is a cubic hypersurface $\Sigma \subset \mathbb{P}^5$. [Hint: as in (5.7) and (5.11), you can write the equations defining $S$ in the form $\operatorname{rank} M \leq 1$, where $M$ is a symmetric $3 \times 3$ matrix with entries the 6 coordinates of $\mathbb{P}^5$; then show that $\Sigma : (\det M = 0)$. See [Semple and Roth, p. 128] for more details.]

5.13   Veronese 曲面。 研究嵌入 $\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^2 \to \mathbb{P}^5$，$(X,Y,Z) \mapsto (X^2, XY, XZ, Y^2, YZ, Z^2)$；写出定义像 $S = \varphi(\mathbb{P}^2)$ 的方程，并证明 $\varphi$ 是同构（写出逆态射的方程）。证明 $\mathbb{P}^2$ 的直线映为 $\mathbb{P}^5$ 的圆锥曲线，$\mathbb{P}^2$ 的圆锥曲线映为 $\mathbb{P}^5$ 的扭四次曲线（见 (5.7)）。

对任意直线 $\ell \subset \mathbb{P}^2$，记 $\pi(\ell) \subset \mathbb{P}^5$ 为圆锥曲线 $\varphi(\ell)$ 张成的射影平面。证明所有 $\pi(\ell)$（$\ell \subset \mathbb{P}^2$）的并集是一个三次超曲面 $\Sigma \subset \mathbb{P}^5$。[提示：如 (5.7) 和 (5.11)，定义 $S$ 的方程可以写成 $\operatorname{rank} M \leq 1$ 的形式，其中 $M$ 是以 $\mathbb{P}^5$ 的 6 个坐标为元素的对称 $3 \times 3$ 矩阵；然后证明 $\Sigma : (\det M = 0)$。更多细节参见 [Semple and Roth, p. 128]。]
