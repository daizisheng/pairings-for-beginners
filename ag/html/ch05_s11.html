5.11 Products

5.11 乘积

If $V$ and $W$ are two affine varieties then there is a natural sense in which $V \times W$ is again a variety: if $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ and $W \subset \mathbb{A}^m$ then $V \times W$ is the subset of $\mathbb{A}^{n+m}$ given by

$$\left\{\big((\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_n); (\beta_1, \ldots, \beta_m)\big) \;\middle|\; \begin{array}{l} f(\underline{\alpha}) = 0 \text{ for all } f \in I(V)\\ g(\underline{\beta}) = 0 \text{ for all } g \in I(W)\end{array}\right\}$$

It's easy to check that $V \times W$ remains irreducible/ˌɪr.ɪˈdjuː.sə.bl/ 不可约的. Note however that the Zariski topology of the product is not the product of the Zariski topologies (see Ex. 5.10).

如果 $V$ 和 $W$ 是两个仿射簇，那么 $V \times W$ 自然也是簇：若 $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$，$W \subset \mathbb{A}^m$，则 $V \times W$ 是 $\mathbb{A}^{n+m}$ 中满足如下条件的子集： $$\left\{\big((\alpha_1, \ldots, \alpha_n); (\beta_1, \ldots, \beta_m)\big) \;\middle|\; f(\underline{\alpha}) = 0, \;\forall f \in I(V); \quad g(\underline{\beta}) = 0, \;\forall g \in I(W)\right\}$$ 容易验证 $V \times W$ 仍然不可约。但要注意，乘积的 Zariski 拓扑不是两个因子 Zariski 拓扑的乘积拓扑（见习题 5.10）。

The case of projective varieties is not so obvious; to be able to define products, we need to know that $\mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m$ is itself a projective variety. Notice that it is definitely not isomorphic to $\mathbb{P}^{n+m}$ (see Ex. 5.2, ii). To do this, I use a construction rather similar in spirit to that of (5.7, I): make an embedding/ɪmˈbed.ɪŋ/ 嵌入 (the 'Segre embedding')

$$\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m \to S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N,$$

where $N = (n+1)(m+1) - 1$ as follows: $\mathbb{P}^N$ is the projective space with homogeneous coordinates $(U_{ij})_{\substack{i=0,\ldots,n \\ j=0,\ldots,m}}$.

It's useful to think of the $U_{ij}$ as being set out in a matrix

$$\begin{pmatrix} U_{00} & \cdots & U_{0m} \\ U_{10} & \cdots & \cdots \\ \cdots & \cdots & U_{nm} \end{pmatrix}$$

Then define $\varphi$ by $\big((X_0, \ldots, X_n), (Y_0, \ldots, Y_m)\big) \mapsto (X_i Y_j)_{\substack{i=0,\ldots,n \\ j=0,\ldots,m}}$. This is obviously a well defined morphism, and the image $S_{n,m}$ is easily seen to be the projective subvariety/ˌsʌb.vəˈraɪ.ə.ti/ 子簇 given by

$$\operatorname{rank}\begin{pmatrix} U_{00} & \cdots & U_{0m} \\ U_{10} & \cdots & \cdots \\ \cdots & \cdots & U_{nm} \end{pmatrix} \leq 1, \quad \text{that is,} \quad \det\begin{pmatrix} U_{ik} & U_{i\ell} \\ U_{jk} & U_{j\ell} \end{pmatrix} = 0$$

for all $i, j = 0, \ldots, n$ and $k, \ell = 0, \ldots, m$.

射影簇的情形就没那么显然了；为了定义乘积，我们需要知道 $\mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m$ 本身是射影簇。注意它绝对不同构于 $\mathbb{P}^{n+m}$（见习题 5.2, ii）。为此，我使用一个与 (5.7, I) 精神类似的构造：做一个嵌入（"Segre 嵌入"） $$\varphi \colon \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m \to S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N,$$ 其中 $N = (n+1)(m+1) - 1$。$\mathbb{P}^N$ 以 $(U_{ij})$ 为齐次坐标（$i = 0,\ldots,n$，$j = 0,\ldots,m$）。

把 $U_{ij}$ 排成矩阵来看是很有用的：

$$\begin{pmatrix} U_{00} & \cdots & U_{0m} \\ U_{10} & \cdots & \cdots \\ \cdots & \cdots & U_{nm} \end{pmatrix}$$

然后定义 $\varphi$ 为 $\big((X_0,\ldots,X_n),(Y_0,\ldots,Y_m)\big) \mapsto (X_i Y_j)$。这显然是良定义的态射，其像 $S_{n,m}$ 容易看出是由如下条件给出的射影子簇

$$\operatorname{rank}\begin{pmatrix} U_{00} & \cdots & U_{0m} \\ \vdots & & \vdots \\ U_{n0} & \cdots & U_{nm} \end{pmatrix} \leq 1, \quad \text{即 } \det\begin{pmatrix} U_{ik} & U_{i\ell} \\ U_{jk} & U_{j\ell} \end{pmatrix} = 0$$

对所有 $i,j = 0,\ldots,n$ 和 $k,\ell = 0,\ldots,m$。

We get an inverse map $S_{n,m} \to \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m$ as follows. For $P \in S_{n,m}$ there exists at least one pair $(i,j)$ such that $U_{ij}(P) \neq 0$; fixing this $(i,j)$, send

$$S_{n,m} \ni P \mapsto \big((U_{0j}, \ldots, U_{nj}), (U_{i0}, \ldots, U_{im})\big) \in \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m.$$

Note that the choice of $(i,j)$ doesn't matter, since the matrix $U_{ij}(P)$ has rank 1, and hence all its rows and all its columns are proportional/prəˈpɔː.ʃən.əl/ 成比例的.

逆映射 $S_{n,m} \to \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m$ 如下构造：对 $P \in S_{n,m}$，至少存在一对 $(i,j)$ 使得 $U_{ij}(P) \neq 0$；固定该 $(i,j)$，令 $$S_{n,m} \ni P \mapsto \big((U_{0j}, \ldots, U_{nj}), (U_{i0}, \ldots, U_{im})\big) \in \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m.$$ 注意 $(i,j)$ 的选取无关紧要，因为矩阵 $U_{ij}(P)$ 秩为 1，所以它的所有行和所有列都成比例。

From this it is not hard to see that if $V \subset \mathbb{P}^n$ and $W \subset \mathbb{P}^m$ are projective varieties, then $V \times W \subset \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m \cong S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ is again a projective variety (see Ex. 5.11).

由此不难看出，如果 $V \subset \mathbb{P}^n$ 和 $W \subset \mathbb{P}^m$ 是射影簇，那么 $V \times W \subset \mathbb{P}^n \times \mathbb{P}^m \cong S_{n,m} \subset \mathbb{P}^N$ 仍然是射影簇（见习题 5.11）。
