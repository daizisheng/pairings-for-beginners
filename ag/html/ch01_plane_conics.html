Chapter 1
Plane Conics

第1章 平面二次曲线

I start by studying the geometry of conics/ˈkɒn.ɪks/ 二次曲线（圆锥曲线） as motivation for the projective/prəˈdʒek.tɪv/ 射影的 plane $\mathbb{P}^2$. Projective geometry is usually mentioned in 2nd year undergraduate geometry courses, and I recall some of the salient/ˈseɪ.li.ənt/ 突出的，显著的 features, with some emphasis on homogeneous/ˌhɒm.əˈdʒiː.ni.əs/ 齐次的 coordinates, although I completely ignore the geometry of linear subspaces and the 'cross-ratio'. The most important aim for the student should be to grasp/ɡrɑːsp/ 理解，掌握 the way in which geometric ideas (for example, the idea that 'points at infinity' correspond to asymptotic/ˌæs.ɪmpˈtɒt.ɪk/ 渐近的 directions of curves) are expressed in terms of coordinates. The interplay between the intuitive/ɪnˈtjuː.ɪ.tɪv/ 直觉的 geometric picture (which tells you what you should be expecting), and the precise formulation in terms of coordinates (which allows you to cash in on your intuition) is a fascinating/ˈfæs.ɪ.neɪ.tɪŋ/ 迷人的 aspect of algebraic geometry.

我从研究二次曲线的几何开始，以此作为引入射影平面 $\mathbb{P}^2$ 的动机。射影几何通常在大二的几何课程中被提及，我在这里回顾一些重要特征，重点放在齐次坐标上，而完全忽略线性子空间的几何和"交比"。学生最需要掌握的是：几何思想（例如"无穷远点"对应曲线的渐近方向）如何用坐标来表达。直觉的几何图像（告诉你该期待什么）与坐标的精确表述（让你把直觉兑现）之间的互动，是代数几何迷人的一面。
