6.8 Intrinsic nature of $T_P V$

6.8 $T_P V$ 的内蕴性质

From now on, given $P = (a_1, \ldots, a_n) \in V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$, I take new coordinates $X_i' = X_i - a_i$ to bring $P$ to the origin, and thus assume that $P = (0, \ldots, 0)$. Then $T_P V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ is a vector subspace of $k^n$.

从现在起，给定 $P = (a_1, \ldots, a_n) \in V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$，取新坐标 $X_i' = X_i - a_i$ 将 $P$ 平移到原点，从而假设 $P = (0, \ldots, 0)$。则 $T_P V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 是 $k^n$ 的一个向量子空间。

Notation   Write $\mathfrak{m}_P = $ ideal of $P$ in $k[V]$, and

$$M_P = \text{the ideal } (X_1, \ldots, X_n) \subset k[X_1, \ldots, X_n].$$

Then of course $\mathfrak{m}_P = M_P / I(V) \subset k[V]$.

记号：记 $\mathfrak{m}_P$ 为 $P$ 在 $k[V]$ 中的理想，以及 $$M_P = \text{理想 } (X_1, \ldots, X_n) \subset k[X_1, \ldots, X_n].$$ 当然 $\mathfrak{m}_P = M_P / I(V) \subset k[V]$。

Theorem   In the above notation,

(a) there is a natural isomorphism/ˌaɪ.səˈmɔː.fɪ.zəm/ 同构 of vector spaces

$$(T_P V)^* = \mathfrak{m}_P / \mathfrak{m}_P^2,$$

where $( )^*$ denotes the dual/ˈdjuː.əl/ 对偶的 of a vector space.

(b) If $f \in k[V]$ is such that $f(P) \neq 0$, and $V_f \subset V$ is the standard affine open as in (4.13), then the natural map

$$T_P(V_f) \to T_P V$$

is an isomorphism.

定理：在上述记号下，
(a) 存在向量空间的自然同构 $$(T_P V)^* = \mathfrak{m}_P / \mathfrak{m}_P^2,$$ 其中 $( )^*$ 表示向量空间的对偶。

(b) 如果 $f \in k[V]$ 满足 $f(P) \neq 0$，且 $V_f \subset V$ 是如(4.13)中的标准仿射开集，则自然映射 $$T_P(V_f) \to T_P V$$ 是同构。

Proof of (a)   Write $(k^n)^*$ for the vector space of linear forms on $k^n$; this is the vector space with basis $X_1, \ldots, X_n$. Since $P = (0, \ldots, 0)$, for any $f \in k[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$, the linear part $f_P^{(1)}$ is naturally an element of $(k^n)^*$; define a map $d\colon M_P \to (k^n)^*$ by taking $f \in M_P$ into $df = f_P^{(1)}$.

Now $d$ is surjective/sərˈdʒek.tɪv/ 满射的, since the $X_i \in M_P$ go into the natural basis of $(k^n)^*$; also $\ker d = M_P^2$, since

$$f_P^{(1)} = 0 \iff f \text{ starts with quadratic terms in } X_1, \ldots, X_n \iff f \in M_P^2.$$

Hence $M_P / M_P^2 \cong (k^n)^*$. This is statement (a) for the special case $V = \mathbb{A}^n$. In the general case, dual to the inclusion $T_P V \subset k^n$, there is a restriction map $(k^n)^* \to (T_P V)^*$, taking a linear form $\lambda$ on $k^n$ into its restriction to $T_P V$; composing then defines a map

$$D\colon M_P \to (k^n)^* \to (T_P V)^*.$$

The composite $D$ is surjective since each factor is. I claim that the kernel of $D$ is just $M_P^2 + I(V)$, so that

$$\mathfrak{m}_P / \mathfrak{m}_P^2 = M_P / (M_P^2 + I(V)) \cong (T_P V)^*,$$

as required. To prove the claim,

$$f \in \ker D \iff f_P^{(1)}|_{T_P V} = 0$$ $$\iff f_P^{(1)} = \sum_i a_i g_{i,P}^{(1)} \text{ for some } g_i \in I(V)$$

(since $T_P V \subset k^n$ is the vector subspace defined by $(g_P^{(1)} = 0)$ for $g \in I(V)$)

$$\iff f - \sum_i a_i g_i \in M_P^2 \text{ for some } g_i \in I(V) \iff f \in M_P^2 + I(V).$$

The proof of (b) of Theorem 6.8 is left to the reader (see Ex. 6.2).   Q.E.D.

(a)的证明：记 $(k^n)^*$ 为 $k^n$ 上线性形式的向量空间，以 $X_1, \ldots, X_n$ 为基。因为 $P = (0, \ldots, 0)$，对任意 $f \in k[X_1, \ldots, X_n]$，线性部分 $f_P^{(1)}$ 自然是 $(k^n)^*$ 的元素；定义映射 $d\colon M_P \to (k^n)^*$，将 $f \in M_P$ 映为 $df = f_P^{(1)}$。

$d$ 是满射的，因为 $X_i \in M_P$ 映到 $(k^n)^*$ 的自然基；并且 $\ker d = M_P^2$，因为 $$f_P^{(1)} = 0 \iff f \text{ 以 } X_1, \ldots, X_n \text{ 的二次项开始} \iff f \in M_P^2.$$ 因此 $M_P / M_P^2 \cong (k^n)^*$。这就是 $V = \mathbb{A}^n$ 特殊情形下的(a)。在一般情形下，对偶于包含 $T_P V \subset k^n$，有限制映射 $(k^n)^* \to (T_P V)^*$，将 $k^n$ 上的线性形式 $\lambda$ 限制到 $T_P V$ 上；复合得到映射 $$D\colon M_P \to (k^n)^* \to (T_P V)^*.$$ 复合映射 $D$ 是满射的，因为每个因子都是。我断言 $D$ 的核恰好是 $M_P^2 + I(V)$，因此 $$\mathfrak{m}_P / \mathfrak{m}_P^2 = M_P / (M_P^2 + I(V)) \cong (T_P V)^*,$$ 这正是所需。为证明此断言， $$f \in \ker D \iff f_P^{(1)}|_{T_P V} = 0$$ $$\iff f_P^{(1)} = \sum_i a_i g_{i,P}^{(1)} \text{ 对某些 } g_i \in I(V)$$ （因为 $T_P V \subset k^n$ 是由 $(g_P^{(1)} = 0)$（$g \in I(V)$）定义的向量子空间） $$\iff f - \sum_i a_i g_i \in M_P^2 \text{ 对某些 } g_i \in I(V) \iff f \in M_P^2 + I(V).$$ 定理6.8的(b)的证明留给读者（见习题6.2）。Q.E.D.
