0.6 Plan of the book

0.6 本书计划

As to the structure of the book, Part I and Part III aim to indicate some worthwhile problems which can be studied by means of algebraic geometry. Part II is an introduction to the commutative algebra referred to in (0.4) and to the categorical/ˌkæt.ɪˈɡɒr.ɪ.kəl/ 范畴的 framework of algebraic geometry; the student who is prone/proʊn/ 容易…的，倾向于 to headaches could perhaps take some of the proofs for granted here, since the material is standard, and the author is a professional algebraic geometer of the highest moral fibre/ˈfaɪ.bər/ 品格，素质（英式拼写）.

本书结构：
  • Part I（第1–2章）和 Part III（第5–7章）：旨在展示一些可以用代数几何方法研究的有意义的问题。
  • Part II（第3–4章）：介绍 (0.4) 中提到的交换代数以及代数几何的范畴框架。容易头疼的学生可以把这里的一些证明当作已知结论来接受，因为材料都是标准的——而且作者是一位具有最高道德品格的专业代数几何学家。（这是Reid典型的英式幽默。）

§8 contains odds and ends that may be of interest or of use to the student, but that don't fit in the main text: a little of the history and sociology/ˌsoʊ.siˈɒl.ə.dʒi/ 社会学（此处指学科社区的文化） of the modern subject, hints as to relations of the subject matter with more advanced topics, technical footnotes, etc.

§8 收录了一些零碎但可能对学生有趣或有用的内容，它们不适合放在正文中：关于现代代数几何的一些历史和"社会学"、与更高深课题的联系提示、技术性脚注等。
