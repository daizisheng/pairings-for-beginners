6.9 Corollary: $T_P V$ depends on $P$

6.9 推论：$T_P V$ 仅依赖于 $P$ 的邻域

Corollary 6.9   $T_P V$ only depends on a neighbourhood of $P \in V$ up to isomorphism. More precisely, if $P \in V_0 \subset V$ and $Q \in W_0 \subset W$ are open subsets of affine varieties, and $\varphi\colon V_0 \to W_0$ an isomorphism taking $P$ into $Q$, there is a natural isomorphism $T_P V_0 \to T_Q W_0$; hence $\dim T_P V_0 = \dim T_Q W_0$.

推论 6.9：$T_P V$ 只依赖于 $P \in V$ 的邻域（在同构意义下）。更精确地说，如果 $P \in V_0 \subset V$ 和 $Q \in W_0 \subset W$ 是仿射簇的开子集，$\varphi\colon V_0 \to W_0$ 是将 $P$ 映为 $Q$ 的同构，则存在自然同构 $T_P V_0 \to T_Q W_0$；因此 $\dim T_P V_0 = \dim T_Q W_0$。

In particular, if $V$ and $W$ are birationally/baɪˈræʃ.ən.əl.i/ 双有理地 equivalent varieties then $\dim V = \dim W$.

特别地，如果 $V$ 和 $W$ 是双有理等价的簇，则 $\dim V = \dim W$。

Proof   By passing to a smaller neighbourhood of $P$ in $V$, I can assume $V_0$ is isomorphic to an affine variety (Proposition 4.13). Then so is $W_0$, and $\varphi$ induces an isomorphism $k[V_0] \cong k[W_0]$ taking $\mathfrak{m}_P$ into $\mathfrak{m}_Q$. The final sentence holds because by (5.8), $V$ and $W$ contain dense open subsets which are isomorphic.

证明：通过缩小 $P$ 在 $V$ 中的邻域，可以假设 $V_0$ 同构于一个仿射簇（命题4.13）。$W_0$ 也是如此，而 $\varphi$ 诱导同构 $k[V_0] \cong k[W_0]$，将 $\mathfrak{m}_P$ 映为 $\mathfrak{m}_Q$。最后一句成立是因为，由(5.8)，$V$ 和 $W$ 包含同构的稠密开子集。
