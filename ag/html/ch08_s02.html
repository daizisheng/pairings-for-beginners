8.2 Prehistory

史前时期

Algebraic geometry developed in the 19th century from several different sources. Firstly, the geometric tradition itself: projective/prəˈdʒek.tɪv/ 射影的 geometry (and descriptive/dɪˈskrɪp.tɪv/ 描述性的 geometry, of great interest to the military at the time of Napoleon), the study of curves and surfaces for their own sake, configuration/kənˌfɪɡ.jəˈreɪ.ʃən/ 构型 geometry; then complex function theory, the view of a compact Riemann surface as an algebraic curve, and the purely algebraic reconstruction/ˌriː.kənˈstrʌk.ʃən/ 重构 of it from its function field. On top of this, the deep analogy/əˈnæl.ə.dʒi/ 类比 between algebraic curves and the ring of integers of a number field, and the need for a language in algebra and geometry for invariant/ɪnˈveə.ri.ənt/ 不变量 theory, which played an important role in the development of abstract algebra at the start of the 20th century.

代数几何在19世纪从几个不同的源头发展起来。首先是几何传统本身：射影几何（以及描述几何——在拿破仑时代对军事极为重要）、对曲线和曲面本身的研究、构型几何；其次是复函数论、将紧Riemann面看作代数曲线的观点、以及从函数域出发对它的纯代数重构。在此之上，还有代数曲线与数域整数环之间的深刻类比，以及代数与几何中对不变量理论语言的需要——后者在20世纪初抽象代数的发展中起了重要作用。

The first decades of the 20th century saw a deep division. On the one hand, the geometric tradition of studying curves and surfaces, as pursued/pəˈsjuːd/ 追求的，从事的 notably by the brilliant Italian school; alongside its own quite considerable achievements, this played a substantial motivating/ˈməʊ.tɪ.veɪ.tɪŋ/ 激发性的 role in the development of topology and differential geometry, but became increasingly dependent on arguments 'by geometric intuition/ˌɪn.tjuˈɪʃ.ən/ 直觉' that even the Maestri were unable to sustain/səˈsteɪn/ 维持，支撑 rigorously. On the other hand, the newly emerging forces of commutative algebra were laying foundations and providing techniques of proof. An example of the difference between the two approaches was the argument between Chow and van der Waerden, who established rigorously the existence of an algebraic variety parametrising/pəˈræm.ɪ.traɪ.zɪŋ/ 参数化的 space curves of given degree and genus, and Severi, who had been making creative use of such parameter spaces all his working life, and who in his declining/dɪˈklaɪ.nɪŋ/ 衰退的，晚年的 years bitterly resented/rɪˈzen.tɪd/ 怨恨，愤恨 the intrusion/ɪnˈtruː.ʒən/ 侵入，干涉 of algebraists (nonItalians at that!) into his field, and most especially the implicit suggestion that the work of his own school lacked rigour.

20世纪最初几十年出现了深刻的分裂。一方面，是以杰出的意大利学派为代表的、研究曲线和曲面的几何传统；在其本身相当可观的成就之外，它在拓扑学和微分几何的发展中起了重要的推动作用，但却越来越依赖于"凭几何直觉"的论证——即使是大师们也无法严格地维持这种论证。另一方面，新兴的交换代数力量正在奠定基础并提供证明技术。两种方法之间差异的一个例证，是Chow和van der Waerden（他们严格地建立了参数化给定次数和亏格的空间曲线的代数簇的存在性）与Severi之间的争论——后者毕生都在创造性地使用这类参数空间，但在其晚年却痛苦地怨恨代数学家们（而且还是非意大利人！）侵入他的领域，尤其怨恨其中暗含的对他自己学派的工作缺乏严格性的暗示。
