5.9 Rational varieties

5.9 有理簇

The notion of birational equivalence discussed in (5.8) is of key importance in algebraic geometry. Condition (iii) in the proposition says that the 'meat' of the varieties $V$ and $W$ is the same, although they may differ a bit around the edges; an example of the use of birational transformations/ˌtræns.fɔːˈmeɪ.ʃənz/ 变换 is blowing up a singular/ˈsɪŋ.ɡjʊ.lər/ 奇异的 variety to obtain a nonsingular one, see (6.12) below. An important particular case of Proposition 5.8 is the following result.

(5.8) 讨论的双有理等价概念在代数几何中至关重要。命题中的条件 (iii) 说的是簇 $V$ 和 $W$ 的"主体部分"相同，只是在边缘处可能略有不同；双有理变换的一个应用实例是对奇异簇做爆破以得到非奇异簇，参见下面的 (6.12)。命题 5.8 的一个重要特殊情形是下面的结果。

Corollary   Given a variety $V$, the following two conditions are equivalent:

(a) the function field $k(V)$ is a purely transcendental/ˌtræn.senˈden.təl/ 超越的 extension of $k$, that is $k(V) \cong k(t_1, \ldots, t_n)$ for some $n$;

(b) there exists a dense open set $V_0 \subset V$ which is isomorphic to a dense open subset $U_0 \subset \mathbb{A}^n$.

推论   对于簇 $V$，下面两个条件等价：

(a) 函数域 $k(V)$ 是 $k$ 的纯超越扩张，即 $k(V) \cong k(t_1, \ldots, t_n)$（某个 $n$）；

(b) 存在 $V$ 的稠密开集 $V_0 \subset V$ 与 $\mathbb{A}^n$ 的某个稠密开子集 $U_0$ 同构。

A variety satisfying these conditions is said to be rational. Condition (b) is a precise version of the statement that $V$ can be parametrised/ˈpær.ə.mɪ.traɪzd/ 参数化的 by $n$ independent variables. This notion has already appeared implicitly/ɪmˈplɪs.ɪt.li/ 隐含地 several times in these notes (for example, (1.1), (2.1), (3.11, b), (5.7, II)). A large proportion of the elementary applications of algebraic geometry to other branches of math are related one way or another to rational varieties.

满足这些条件的簇称为有理的。条件 (b) 是"$V$ 可以用 $n$ 个独立变量参数化"这一说法的精确表述。这个概念在本讲义中已多次隐式出现（例如 (1.1)、(2.1)、(3.11, b)、(5.7, II)）。代数几何对其他数学分支的初等应用中，相当大一部分都与有理簇有关。
