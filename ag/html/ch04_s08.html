4.8 Criterion for $\operatorname{dom} f$

$\operatorname{dom} f$ 的判别准则

Theorem 4.8 (I) $\operatorname{dom} f$ is open and dense in the Zariski topology.

Suppose that the field $k$ is algebraically closed; then

(II)

$$\operatorname{dom} f = V \iff f \in k[V];$$

(that is polynomial function = regular rational function). Furthermore, for any $h \in k[V]$, let

$$V_h = V \setminus V(h) = \bigl\{\, P \in V \;\big|\; h(P) \neq 0 \,\bigr\};$$

then

(III)

$$\operatorname{dom} f \supset V_h \iff f \in k[V][h^{-1}].$$
定理 4.8 (I) $\operatorname{dom} f$ 在 Zariski 拓扑中是开的且稠密的。 假设域 $k$ 是代数封闭的，则： (II) $\operatorname{dom} f = V \iff f \in k[V]$ （即多项式函数 = 正则有理函数）。此外，对任意 $h \in k[V]$，令 $$V_h = V \setminus V(h) = \bigl\{\, P \in V \;\big|\; h(P) \neq 0 \,\bigr\};$$ 则 (III) $\operatorname{dom} f \supset V_h \iff f \in k[V][h^{-1}].$

Proof Define the ideal of denominators/dɪˈnɒm.ɪ.neɪ.tərz/ 分母 of $f \in k(V)$ by

$$D_f = \bigl\{\, h \in k[V] \;\big|\; hf \in k[V] \,\bigr\} \subset k[V]$$ $$= \bigl\{\, h \in k[V] \;\big|\; \exists \text{ an expression } f = g/h \text{ with } g \in k[V] \,\bigr\} \cup \{0\}.$$

From the first line, $D_f$ is obviously an ideal of $k[V]$. Then formally,

$$V \setminus \operatorname{dom} f = \bigl\{\, P \in V \;\big|\; h(P) = 0 \text{ for all } h \in D_f \,\bigr\} = V(D_f),$$

so that $V \setminus \operatorname{dom} f$ is an algebraic set of $V$; hence $\operatorname{dom} f = V \setminus V(D_f)$ is the complement of a closed set, so open in the Zariski topology. It is obvious that $\operatorname{dom} f$ is nonempty, hence dense by Proposition 4.2.

证明 定义 $f \in k(V)$ 的分母理想为 $$D_f = \bigl\{\, h \in k[V] \;\big|\; hf \in k[V] \,\bigr\} \subset k[V]$$ $$= \bigl\{\, h \in k[V] \;\big|\; \exists \text{ 表达式 } f = g/h，其中 g \in k[V] \,\bigr\} \cup \{0\}.$$ 由第一行可知，$D_f$ 显然是 $k[V]$ 的一个理想。于是形式上有 $$V \setminus \operatorname{dom} f = \bigl\{\, P \in V \;\big|\; h(P) = 0, \;\forall h \in D_f \,\bigr\} = V(D_f),$$ 因此 $V \setminus \operatorname{dom} f$ 是 $V$ 的一个代数集；从而 $\operatorname{dom} f = V \setminus V(D_f)$ 是闭集的补集，在 Zariski 拓扑中是开的。显然 $\operatorname{dom} f$ 非空，因此由命题 4.2 可知它是稠密的。

Now using (b) of the Nullstellensatz,

$$\operatorname{dom} f = V \iff V(D_f) = \emptyset \iff 1 \in D_f, \quad \text{that is, } f \in k[V].$$

Finally,

$$\operatorname{dom} f \supset V_h \iff h \text{ vanishes on } V(D_f),$$

and using (c) of the Nullstellensatz,

$$\iff h^n \in D_f \text{ for some } n, \text{ that is, } f = g/h^n \in k[V][h^{-1}]. \quad \text{Q.E.D.}$$
利用零点定理的 (b)， $$\operatorname{dom} f = V \iff V(D_f) = \emptyset \iff 1 \in D_f, \quad \text{即 } f \in k[V].$$ 最后， $$\operatorname{dom} f \supset V_h \iff h \text{ 在 } V(D_f) \text{ 上为零},$$ 利用零点定理的 (c)， $$\iff h^n \in D_f \text{ 对某个 } n，\text{即 } f = g/h^n \in k[V][h^{-1}]. \quad \text{Q.E.D.}$$
