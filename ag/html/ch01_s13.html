1.13 Degenerate conics in a pencil

线性系中的退化二次曲线

Definition A pencil/ˈpen.sɪl/ 线性系（铅笔） of conics is a family of the form

$$C_{(\lambda,\mu)}: (\lambda Q_1 + \mu Q_2 = 0);$$

each element is a plane curve, depending in a linear way on the parameters $(\lambda, \mu)$; think of the ratio $(\lambda : \mu)$ as a point of $\mathbb{P}^1$.

定义 二次曲线的线性系（pencil）是如下形式的族：$C_{(\lambda,\mu)}: (\lambda Q_1 + \mu Q_2 = 0)$；每个元素是一条平面曲线，以线性方式依赖于参数 $(\lambda, \mu)$；把比值 $(\lambda : \mu)$ 看作 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 的一个点。

Looking at the examples, one expects that for special values of $(\lambda : \mu)$ the conic $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ is degenerate. In fact, writing $\det(Q)$ for the determinant/dɪˈtɜː.mɪ.nənt/ 行列式 of the symmetric $3 \times 3$ matrix corresponding to the quadratic form $Q$, it is clear that

$$C_{(\lambda,\mu)} \text{ is degenerate} \iff \det(\lambda Q_1 + \mu Q_2) = 0.$$

Writing out $Q_1$ and $Q_2$ as symmetric matrixes expresses this condition as

$$F(\lambda, \mu) = \det\left|\lambda \begin{pmatrix} a & b & d \\ b & c & e \\ d & e & f \end{pmatrix} + \mu \begin{pmatrix} a' & b' & d' \\ b' & c' & e' \\ d' & e' & f' \end{pmatrix}\right| = 0.$$

Now notice that $F(\lambda, \mu)$ is a homogeneous/ˌhɒm.əˈdʒiː.ni.əs/ 齐次的 cubic form in $\lambda, \mu$. In turn I can apply (1.8) to $F$ to deduce/dɪˈdjuːs/ 推导出:

观察这些例子，我们预期对于 $(\lambda : \mu)$ 的某些特殊值，二次曲线 $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ 是退化的。事实上，用 $\det(Q)$ 表示二次型 $Q$ 对应的 $3 \times 3$ 对称矩阵的行列式，显然 $$C_{(\lambda,\mu)} \text{ 退化} \iff \det(\lambda Q_1 + \mu Q_2) = 0.$$ 把 $Q_1$ 和 $Q_2$ 写成对称矩阵，这一条件表示为 $$F(\lambda, \mu) = \det\left|\lambda \begin{pmatrix} a & b & d \\ b & c & e \\ d & e & f \end{pmatrix} + \mu \begin{pmatrix} a' & b' & d' \\ b' & c' & e' \\ d' & e' & f' \end{pmatrix}\right| = 0.$$ 注意 $F(\lambda, \mu)$ 是 $\lambda, \mu$ 的三次齐次式。对 $F$ 应用 (1.8) 即可推出：

Proposition Suppose $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ is a pencil of conics of $\mathbb{P}^2_k$, with at least one nondegenerate conic (so that $F(\lambda, \mu)$ is not identically/aɪˈden.tɪ.kəl.i/ 恒等地 zero). Then the pencil has at most 3 degenerate conics. If $k = \mathbb{R}$ then the pencil has at least one degenerate conic.

Proof A cubic form has $\leq 3$ zeros. Also over $\mathbb{R}$, it must have at least one zero.

命题 设 $C_{(\lambda,\mu)}$ 是 $\mathbb{P}^2_k$ 中二次曲线的线性系，且至少包含一条非退化二次曲线（即 $F(\lambda, \mu)$ 不恒为零）。那么该线性系中至多有3条退化二次曲线。若 $k = \mathbb{R}$，则至少有一条退化二次曲线。 证明 三次齐次式至多有3个零点。在 $\mathbb{R}$ 上，它至少有一个零点。
