6.10 Theorem: $\dim V = \operatorname{tr\,deg}$

6.10 定理：$\dim V = \operatorname{tr\,deg}$

Theorem 6.10   For any variety $V$, $\dim V = \operatorname{tr\,deg} k(V)$.

定理 6.10：对任意簇 $V$，$\dim V = \operatorname{tr\,deg}_k\, k(V)$。

Proof   This is known if $V$ is a hypersurface/ˈhaɪ.pə.sɜː.fɪs/ 超曲面. On the other hand, every variety is birational/baɪˈræʃ.ən.əl/ 双有理的 to a hypersurface (by (5.10)), and both sides of the required relation are the same for birationally equivalent varieties.   Q.E.D.

证明：如果 $V$ 是超曲面，这已经知道了。另一方面，每个簇都双有理等价于一个超曲面（由(5.10)），而所需等式的两边对双有理等价的簇都相同。Q.E.D.
