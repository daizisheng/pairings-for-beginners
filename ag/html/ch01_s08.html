1.8 Homogeneous Form in 2 Variables

二元齐次形式

Let $F(U, V)$ be a nonzero homogeneous polynomial of degree $d$ in $U, V$, with coefficients/ˌkəʊ.ɪˈfɪʃ.ənts/ 系数 in a fixed field $k$; (I will follow tradition, and use the word form for 'homogeneous polynomial'):

$$F(U, V) = a_d U^d + a_{d-1} U^{d-1} V + \cdots + a_i U^i V^{d-i} + \cdots + a_0 V^d.$$
设 $F(U,V)$ 是 $U, V$ 的非零 $d$ 次齐次多项式，系数在固定的域 $k$ 中。（我将遵循传统，用"形式"(form) 一词来表示"齐次多项式"。） $$F(U, V) = a_d U^d + a_{d-1} U^{d-1} V + \cdots + a_i U^i V^{d-i} + \cdots + a_0 V^d.$$

$F$ has an associated inhomogeneous/ˌɪn.hə.məˈdʒiː.ni.əs/ 非齐次的 polynomial in 1 variable,

$$f(u) = a_d u^d + a_{d-1} u^{d-1} + \cdots + a_i u^i + \cdots + a_0.$$

Clearly for $\alpha \in k$,

$$f(\alpha) = 0 \iff (u - \alpha) \mid f(u) \iff (U - \alpha V) \mid F(U, V) \iff F(\alpha, 1) = 0;$$

so zeros of $f$ correspond to zeros of $F$ on $\mathbb{P}^1$ away from the point $(1, 0)$, the 'point $\alpha = \infty$.' What does it mean for $F$ to have a zero at infinity?

$$F(1, 0) = 0 \iff a_d = 0 \iff \deg f < d.$$
$F$ 有一个关联的单变量非齐次多项式 $f(u) = a_d u^d + a_{d-1} u^{d-1} + \cdots + a_0$。显然对 $\alpha \in k$，$f(\alpha) = 0 \iff (u - \alpha) \mid f(u) \iff (U - \alpha V) \mid F(U,V) \iff F(\alpha, 1) = 0$。因此 $f$ 的零点对应于 $F$ 在 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 上除 $(1,0)$（即"$\alpha = \infty$ 点"）以外的零点。$F$ 在无穷远处有零点意味着什么呢？$F(1,0) = 0 \iff a_d = 0 \iff \deg f < d$。

Now define the multiplicity/ˌmʌl.tɪˈplɪs.ɪ.ti/ 重数 of a zero of $F$ on $\mathbb{P}^1$ to be

  1. the multiplicity of $f$ at the corresponding $\alpha \in k$; or
  2. $d - \deg f$ if $(1, 0)$ is the zero.

So the multiplicity of zero of $F$ at a point $(\alpha, 1)$ is the greatest power of $(U - \alpha V)$ dividing $F$, and at $(1, 0)$ it is the greatest power of $V$ dividing $F$.

现在定义 $F$ 在 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 上零点的重数为：(i) 如果零点对应于 $\alpha \in k$，则为 $f$ 在 $\alpha$ 处的重数；(ii) 如果零点是 $(1,0)$，则为 $d - \deg f$。换言之，$F$ 在点 $(\alpha, 1)$ 处零点的重数是 $(U - \alpha V)$ 整除 $F$ 的最高次幂，而在 $(1,0)$ 处则是 $V$ 整除 $F$ 的最高次幂。

Proposition

Let $F(U, V)$ be a nonzero form of degree $d$ in $U, V$. Then $F$ has at most $d$ zeros on $\mathbb{P}^1$; furthermore, if $k$ is algebraically/ˌæl.dʒɪˈbreɪ.ɪ.kli/ 代数地 closed, then $F$ has exactly $d$ zeros on $\mathbb{P}^1$ provided these are counted with multiplicities as defined above.

命题 设 $F(U,V)$ 是 $U, V$ 的非零 $d$ 次形式。则 $F$ 在 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 上至多有 $d$ 个零点；进一步地，如果 $k$ 是代数封闭域，则按上述定义的重数计算，$F$ 在 $\mathbb{P}^1$ 上恰好有 $d$ 个零点。

Proof

Let $m_\infty$ be the multiplicity of the zero of $F$ at $(1, 0)$; then by definition, $d - m_\infty$ is the degree of the inhomogeneous polynomial $f$, and the proposition/ˌprɒp.əˈzɪʃ.ən/ 命题 reduces to the well known fact that a polynomial in one variable has at most $\deg f$ roots.  Q.E.D.

证明 设 $m_\infty$ 是 $F$ 在 $(1,0)$ 处零点的重数；则按定义，$d - m_\infty$ 是非齐次多项式 $f$ 的次数，于是命题归结为众所周知的事实：一元多项式至多有 $\deg f$ 个根。Q.E.D.

Note that over an algebraically closed field, $F$ will factorise/ˈfæk.tər.aɪz/ 分解因式 as a product $F = \prod \lambda_i^{m_i}$ of linear forms $\lambda_i = (a_i U + b_i V)$, and treated in this way, the point $(1, 0)$ corresponds to the form $\lambda_\infty = V$, and is on the same footing as all other points.

注意在代数封闭域上，$F$ 将分解为线性形式的乘积 $F = \prod \lambda_i^{m_i}$，其中 $\lambda_i = (a_i U + b_i V)$。这样处理时，点 $(1,0)$ 对应于形式 $\lambda_\infty = V$，与其他所有点处于同等地位。
