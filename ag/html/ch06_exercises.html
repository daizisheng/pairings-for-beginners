Exercises to Chapter 6

第6章习题

6.1   Let $k \subset K$ be a field extension, and $(u_1, \ldots, u_r)$, $(v_1, \ldots, v_s)$ two sets of elements of $K$; suppose that $(u_1, \ldots, u_r)$ are algebraically independent, and that $(v_1, \ldots, v_s)$ span the extension $k \subset K$ algebraically. Prove that $r \leq s$. [Hint: the inductive/ɪnˈdʌk.tɪv/ 归纳的 step consists of assuming that $(u_1, \ldots, u_i, v_{i+1}, \ldots, v_s)$ span $K/k$ algebraically, and considering $u_{i+1}$.] Deduce that any two transcendence bases of $K/k$ have the same number of elements.

6.1   设 $k \subset K$ 是域扩张，$(u_1, \ldots, u_r)$、$(v_1, \ldots, v_s)$ 是 $K$ 的两组元素；假设 $(u_1, \ldots, u_r)$ 代数独立，$(v_1, \ldots, v_s)$ 代数地张成扩张 $k \subset K$。证明 $r \leq s$。[提示：归纳步骤是假设 $(u_1, \ldots, u_i, v_{i+1}, \ldots, v_s)$ 代数地张成 $K/k$，然后考虑 $u_{i+1}$。] 由此推出 $K/k$ 的任意两组超越基有相同数量的元素。

6.2   Prove Theorem 6.8, (b). [Hint:

$$I(V_f) = (I(V), Yf - 1) \subset k[X_1, \ldots, X_n, Y],$$

so that if $Q = (a_1, \ldots, a_n, b) \in V_f$, then $T_Q V_f \subset \mathbb{A}^{n+1}$ is defined by the equations for $T_P V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$, together with one equation involving $Y$.]

6.2   证明定理6.8的(b)。[提示： $$I(V_f) = (I(V), Yf - 1) \subset k[X_1, \ldots, X_n, Y],$$ 因此如果 $Q = (a_1, \ldots, a_n, b) \in V_f$，则 $T_Q V_f \subset \mathbb{A}^{n+1}$ 由 $T_P V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ 的方程加上一个涉及 $Y$ 的方程来定义。]

6.3   Determine all the singular points of the following curves in $\mathbb{A}^2$.

(a) $y^2 = x^3 - x$;

(b) $y^2 = x^3 - 6x^2 + 9x$;

(c) $x^2 y^2 + x^2 + y^2 + 2xy(x + y + 1) = 0$;

(d) $x^2 = x^4 + y^4$;

(e) $xy = x^6 + y^6$;

(f) $x^3 = y^2 + x^4 + y^4$;

(g) $x^2 y + xy^2 = x^4 + y^4$.

6.3   求下列 $\mathbb{A}^2$ 中曲线的所有奇异点：
(a) $y^2 = x^3 - x$；
(b) $y^2 = x^3 - 6x^2 + 9x$；
(c) $x^2 y^2 + x^2 + y^2 + 2xy(x + y + 1) = 0$；
(d) $x^2 = x^4 + y^4$；
(e) $xy = x^6 + y^6$；
(f) $x^3 = y^2 + x^4 + y^4$；
(g) $x^2 y + xy^2 = x^4 + y^4$。

6.4   Find all the singular points of the surfaces in $\mathbb{A}^3$ given by

(a) $xy^2 = z^2$;

(b) $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$;

(c) $xy + x^3 + y^3 = 0$.

(You will find it useful to sketch the real parts of the surfaces, to the limits of your ability; algebraic geometers/dʒiˈɒm.ɪ.tərz/ 几何学家 usually can't draw.)

6.4   求下列 $\mathbb{A}^3$ 中曲面的所有奇异点：
(a) $xy^2 = z^2$；
(b) $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$；
(c) $xy + x^3 + y^3 = 0$。
（你会发现画出这些曲面的实数部分的草图是有帮助的，尽你所能；代数几何学家通常不擅长画图。）

6.5   Show that the hypersurface $V_d \subset \mathbb{P}^n$ defined by

$$X_0^d + X_1^d + \cdots + X_n^d = 0$$

is nonsingular (if $\operatorname{char} k$ does not divide $d$).

6.5   证明：由 $$X_0^d + X_1^d + \cdots + X_n^d = 0$$ 定义的超曲面 $V_d \subset \mathbb{P}^n$ 是非奇异的（如果 $\operatorname{char} k$ 不整除 $d$）。

6.6   (a) Let $C_n \subset \mathbb{A}^2$ be the curve given by $f_n : y^2 - x^{2n+1}$ and $\sigma\colon B \to \mathbb{A}^2$ be as in (6.12), with $\ell = \sigma^{-1}(0)$; show that $\sigma^{-1}(C_n)$ decomposes/ˌdiː.kəmˈpəʊ.zɪz/ 分解 as the union of $\ell$ together with a curve isomorphic to $C_{n-1}$. Deduce that $C_n$ can be resolved by a chain of $n$ blowups.

(b) Show how to resolve the following curve singularities by making one or more blowups:

(i) $y^3 = x^4$;

(ii) $y^3 = x^5$;

(iii) $(y^2 - x^2)(y^2 - x^5) = x^8$.

6.6   (a) 设 $C_n \subset \mathbb{A}^2$ 是由 $f_n : y^2 - x^{2n+1}$ 给出的曲线，$\sigma\colon B \to \mathbb{A}^2$ 如(6.12)中所述，$\ell = \sigma^{-1}(0)$；证明 $\sigma^{-1}(C_n)$ 分解为 $\ell$ 与一条同构于 $C_{n-1}$ 的曲线的并。推出 $C_n$ 可以通过 $n$ 次爆破来消解。

(b) 说明如何通过一次或多次爆破来消解下列曲线奇点：
(i) $y^3 = x^4$；
(ii) $y^3 = x^5$；
(iii) $(y^2 - x^2)(y^2 - x^5) = x^8$。

6.7   Prove that the intersection of a hypersurface $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$ (not a hyperplane) with the tangent hyperplane $T_P V$ is singular at $P$.

6.7   证明：超曲面 $V \subset \mathbb{A}^n$（不是超平面）与切超平面 $T_P V$ 的交集在 $P$ 处是奇异的。
