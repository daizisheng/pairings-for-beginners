2.2 The curve $(y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda))$ has no rational parametrisation

2.2 曲线 $(y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda))$ 没有有理参数化

Parametrised curves are nice; for example, if you're interested in Diophantine/ˌdaɪ.əˈfæn.tiːn/ 丢番图的 problems, you could hope for a rule giving all $\mathbb{Q}$-valued points, as in (1.1). The parametrisation of (1.1) was of the form $x = f(t)$, $y = g(t)$, where $f$ and $g$ were rational functions/ˈræʃ.ən.əl ˈfʌŋk.ʃənz/ 有理函数, that is, quotients of two polynomials.

参数化的曲线很好用；比如，如果你对丢番图问题感兴趣，你可能希望有一个规则把所有 $\mathbb{Q}$-值点都给出来，就像 (1.1) 那样。(1.1) 的参数化形如 $x = f(t)$，$y = g(t)$，其中 $f$ 和 $g$ 是有理函数，也就是两个多项式的商。

Theorem   Let $k$ be a field of characteristic $\neq 2$, and let $\lambda \in k$ with $\lambda \neq 0, 1$; let $f, g \in k(t)$ be rational functions such that

$$f^2 = g(g - 1)(g - \lambda). \qquad (*)$$

Then $f, g \in k$.

定理   设 $k$ 是特征 $\neq 2$ 的域，$\lambda \in k$ 且 $\lambda \neq 0, 1$；设 $f, g \in k(t)$ 是有理函数，满足 $$f^2 = g(g-1)(g-\lambda). \qquad (*)$$ 则 $f, g \in k$。

This is equivalent/ɪˈkwɪv.əl.ənt/ 等价的 to saying that there does not exist any nonconstant map $\mathbb{R}^1 \dashrightarrow C : (y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda))$ given by rational functions. This reflects a very strong 'rigidity/rɪˈdʒɪd.ɪ.ti/ 刚性' property of varieties.

这等价于说不存在由有理函数给出的非常值映射 $\mathbb{R}^1 \dashrightarrow C : (y^2 = x(x-1)(x-\lambda))$。这反映了簇的一种非常强的"刚性"性质。

The proof of the theorem is arithmetic/əˈrɪθ.mə.tɪk/ 算术的 in the field $k(t)$ using the fact that $k(t)$ is the field of fractions of the UFD $k[t]$. It's quite a long proof, so either be prepared to study it in detail, or skip it for now (GOTO 2.4). In Ex. 2.12, there is a very similar example of a nonexistence proof by arithmetic in $\mathbb{Q}$.

定理的证明是在域 $k(t)$ 中做算术运算，用到 $k(t)$ 是唯一分解整环 $k[t]$ 的分式域这一事实。这个证明相当长，所以你要么准备好仔细研读，要么先跳过（直接去看 2.4）。在习题 2.12 中有一个非常类似的在 $\mathbb{Q}$ 中用算术方法证明不存在性的例子。

Proof   Using the fact that $k[t]$ is a UFD, I write

$$f = r/s \quad \text{with } r, s \in k[t] \text{ and coprime},$$ $$g = p/q \quad \text{with } p, q \in k[t] \text{ and coprime}.$$

Clearing denominators/dɪˈnɒm.ɪ.neɪ.tərz/ 分母, $(*)$ becomes

$$r^2 q^3 = s^2 p(p - q)(p - \lambda q).$$

Then since $r$ and $s$ are coprime, the factor $s^2$ on the right-hand side must divide $q^3$, and in the same way, since $p$ and $q$ are coprime, the left-hand factor $q^3$ must divide $s^2$. Therefore,

$$s^2 \mid q^3 \quad \text{and} \quad q^3 \mid s^2, \quad \text{so that } s^2 = a q^3 \quad \text{with } a \in k$$

($a$ is a unit of $k[t]$, therefore in $k$).

Then

$$aq = (s/q)^2 \quad \text{is a square in } k[t].$$

Also,

$$r^2 = a p(p - q)(p - \lambda q),$$

so that by considering factorisation/ˌfæk.tər.aɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ 因式分解 into primes, there exist nonzero constants $b, c, d \in k$ such that

$$bp, \quad c(p - q), \quad d(p - \lambda q)$$

are all squares in $k[t]$. If I can prove that $p, q$ are constants, then it follows from what's already been said that $r, s$ are also, proving the theorem. To prove that $p, q$ are constants, set $K$ for the algebraic closure/ˌæl.dʒɪˌbreɪ.ɪk ˈkloʊ.ʒər/ 代数闭包 of $k$; then $p, q \in K[t]$ satisfy the conditions of the next lemma.

证明   利用 $k[t]$ 是唯一分解整环，我写 $$f = r/s，\quad r, s \in k[t] \text{ 且互素}，$$ $$g = p/q，\quad p, q \in k[t] \text{ 且互素}。$$ 通分后，$(*)$ 变成 $$r^2 q^3 = s^2 p(p-q)(p-\lambda q).$$ 因为 $r$ 和 $s$ 互素，右边的因子 $s^2$ 必须整除 $q^3$；同理，因为 $p$ 和 $q$ 互素，左边的因子 $q^3$ 必须整除 $s^2$。所以 $$s^2 \mid q^3 \quad \text{且} \quad q^3 \mid s^2, \quad \text{从而 } s^2 = aq^3，\quad a \in k$$ （$a$ 是 $k[t]$ 的单位，因此属于 $k$）。于是 $$aq = (s/q)^2 \quad \text{是 } k[t] \text{ 中的平方}。$$ 另外， $$r^2 = ap(p-q)(p-\lambda q),$$ 考虑素元分解，存在非零常数 $b, c, d \in k$ 使得 $bp$，$c(p-q)$，$d(p-\lambda q)$ 都是 $k[t]$ 中的平方。如果我能证明 $p, q$ 是常数，那么由前面已经说过的，$r, s$ 也是常数，定理就证完了。为了证明 $p, q$ 是常数，设 $K$ 是 $k$ 的代数闭包；则 $p, q \in K[t]$ 满足下一个引理的条件。
