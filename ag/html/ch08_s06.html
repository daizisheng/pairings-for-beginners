This section mixes elementary and advanced topics; since it is partly a 'word to the wise' for university teachers using this as a textbook, or to guide advanced students into the pitfalls/ˈpɪt.fɔːlz/ 陷阱，隐患 of the subject, some of the material may seem obscure/əbˈskjʊər/ 晦涩的.

8.6 Choice of Topics

话题的选择

The topics and examples treated in this book have been chosen partly pragmatically/præɡˈmæt.ɪ.kəl.i/ 务实地 on the basis of small degree and ease of computation. However, they also hint at the 'classification of varieties': the material on conics applies in a sense to every rational curve, and cubic surfaces are the most essential examples of del Pezzo rational surfaces. Cubic curves with their group law are examples of Abelian varieties; the fact (2.2) that a nonsingular cubic is not rational is the very first step in classification. The intersection of two plane conics in (1.12--14) and the intersection of two quadrics of $\mathbb{P}^3_k$ referred to in Ex. 5.6 could also be fitted into a similar pattern, with the intersection of two quadrics in $\mathbb{P}^4_k$ providing another class of del Pezzo surfaces, and the family of lines on the intersection of two quadrics in $\mathbb{P}^5_k$ providing 2-dimensional Abelian varieties.

本书处理的话题和例子，部分是从低次数和计算方便的角度务实地选择的。然而，它们也暗示了"簇的分类"：关于二次曲线的材料在某种意义上适用于每一条有理曲线，三次曲面是del Pezzo有理曲面最核心的例子。三次曲线及其群律是Abel簇的例子；非奇异三次曲线不是有理的这一事实 (2.2) 是分类的第一步。(1.12--14) 中两条平面二次曲线的交和习题 5.6 中所提到的 $\mathbb{P}^3_k$ 中两个二次曲面的交也可以纳入类似的模式，$\mathbb{P}^4_k$ 中两个二次曲面的交提供了另一类del Pezzo曲面，而 $\mathbb{P}^5_k$ 中两个二次曲面交上的直线族提供了二维Abel簇。

The genus of a curve, and the division into 3 cases tabulated on p. 46 is classification in a nutshell/ˈnʌt.ʃel/ 简而言之. I would have liked to include more material on the genus of a curve, in particular how to calculate it in terms of topological Euler characteristic or of intersection numbers in algebraic geometry, essential five finger exercises for young geometers. However, this would comfortably occupy a separate undergraduate lecture course, as would the complex analytic theory of elliptic/ɪˈlɪp.tɪk/ 椭圆的 curves.

曲线的亏格，以及第46页表格中分为三种情形的划分，就是分类的缩影。我本想包含更多关于曲线亏格的内容，特别是如何用拓扑Euler示性数或代数几何中的相交数来计算它——这些是年轻几何学家的基本练习。然而，这些内容足以单独占据一门本科课程，复分析的椭圆曲线理论同样如此。
