7.3 Proposition: Lines Meeting a Given Line

命题：与给定直线相交的直线

Proposition 7.3 Given a line $\ell \subset S$, there exist exactly 5 pairs $(\ell_i, \ell_i')$ of lines of $S$ meeting $\ell$, in such a way that

  1. for $i = 1, \ldots, 5$, $\ell \cup \ell_i \cup \ell_i'$ is coplanar/kəʊˈpleɪ.nər/ 共面的, and
  2. for $i \ne j$, $(\ell_i \cup \ell_i') \cap (\ell_j \cup \ell_j') = \varnothing$.
命题 7.3 给定一条直线 $\ell \subset S$，恰好存在5对 $S$ 上的直线 $(\ell_i, \ell_i')$ 与 $\ell$ 相交，满足
  1. 对 $i = 1, \ldots, 5$，$\ell \cup \ell_i \cup \ell_i'$ 共面；并且
  2. 对 $i \ne j$，$(\ell_i \cup \ell_i') \cap (\ell_j \cup \ell_j') = \varnothing$。

Proof (taken from [Beauville, p. 51]) If $\Pi$ is a plane of $\mathbb{P}^3$ containing $\ell$ then $\Pi \cap S = \ell + \text{conic}$ (since $f|_\Pi$ is divisible/dɪˈvɪz.ə.bl/ 可整除的 by the equation of $\ell$). This conic can either be singular or nonsingular.

证明（取自 [Beauville, p. 51]）如果 $\Pi$ 是 $\mathbb{P}^3$ 中包含 $\ell$ 的一个平面，那么 $\Pi \cap S = \ell + \text{二次曲线}$（因为 $f|_\Pi$ 可被 $\ell$ 的方程整除）。这条二次曲线可以是奇异的或非奇异的。

I have to prove that there are exactly 5 distinct planes $\Pi_i \supset \ell$ for which the singular case occurs. The fact stated as property (ii) that lines in different planes are disjoint/dɪsˈdʒɔɪnt/ 不相交的 will then follow from (7.1, a).

我需要证明恰好有5个不同的平面 $\Pi_i \supset \ell$ 使得奇异情形出现。性质 (ii) 所陈述的不同平面中的直线不相交这一事实随后由 (7.1, a) 得出。

Suppose that $\ell : (Z = T = 0)$; then I can expand $f$ out as

$$f = AX^2 + BXY + CY^2 + DX + EY + F, \qquad (*)$$

where $A, B, C, D, E, F \in k[Z, T]$, with $A$, $B$ and $C$ linear forms, $D$ and $E$ quadratic forms, and $F$ a cubic form. If I consider this equation as a variable conic in $X$ and $Y$, it is singular if and only if

$$\Delta(Z, T) = \det\begin{vmatrix} A & B & D \\ B & C & E \\ D & E & F \end{vmatrix} = 4ACF + BDE - AE^2 - B^2 F - CD^2 = 0.$$
设 $\ell : (Z = T = 0)$；则我可以把 $f$ 展开为 $$f = AX^2 + BXY + CY^2 + DX + EY + F, \qquad (*)$$ 其中 $A, B, C, D, E, F \in k[Z, T]$，$A$、$B$、$C$ 是线性形式，$D$、$E$ 是二次形式，$F$ 是三次形式。如果把这个方程视为 $X$ 和 $Y$ 的活动二次曲线，它是奇异的当且仅当 $$\Delta(Z, T) = \det\begin{vmatrix} A & B & D \\ B & C & E \\ D & E & F \end{vmatrix} = 4ACF + BDE - AE^2 - B^2 F - CD^2 = 0.$$

Now $\Delta(Z, T)$ is a homogeneous quintic/ˈkwɪn.tɪk/ 五次的, so by (1.8), it has 5 roots counted with multiplicities. To prove the proposition, I have to show that it doesn't have multiple roots; this also is a consequence of the nonsingularity of $S$.

现在 $\Delta(Z, T)$ 是一个齐次五次多项式，所以由 (1.8)，算上重数它有5个根。为了证明这个命题，我需要证明它没有重根；这也是 $S$ 的非奇异性的一个推论。

Claim $\Delta(Z, T)$ has only simple roots.

Suppose $Z = 0$ is a root of $\Delta$, and let $\Pi : (Z = 0)$ be the corresponding plane; I have to prove that $\Delta$ is not divisible by $Z^2$. By the above picture, $\Pi \cap S$ is a set of 3 lines, and according to whether they are concurrent/kənˈkʌr.ənt/ 共点的, I can arrange the coordinates so that

either (i) $\ell : (T = 0)$, $\ell_1 : (X = 0)$, $\ell_1' : (Y = 0)$,

or (ii) $\ell : (T = 0)$, $\ell_1 : (X = 0)$, $\ell_1' : (X = T)$.

断言 $\Delta(Z, T)$ 只有单根。 设 $Z = 0$ 是 $\Delta$ 的一个根，令 $\Pi : (Z = 0)$ 是对应的平面；我要证明 $\Delta$ 不能被 $Z^2$ 整除。由上面的图景，$\Pi \cap S$ 是3条直线的集合，根据它们是否共点，我可以选取坐标使得 情形 (i) $\ell : (T = 0)$，$\ell_1 : (X = 0)$，$\ell_1' : (Y = 0)$； 或情形 (ii) $\ell : (T = 0)$，$\ell_1 : (X = 0)$，$\ell_1' : (X = T)$。

Hence, in case (i), $f = XYT + Zg$, with $g$ quadratic, and in terms of the expression $(*)$, this means that $B = T + aZ$, and $Z \mid A, C, D, E, F$. Therefore, modulo terms divisible by $Z^2$,

$$\Delta \equiv -T^2 F \mod Z^2.$$

In addition, the point $P = (0, 0, 0, 1) \in S$, and nonsingularity at $P$ means that $F$ must contain the term $ZT^2$ with nonzero coefficient. In particular, $Z^2$ does not divide $F$. Therefore $(Z = 0)$ is a simple root of $\Delta$.

Case (ii) is a similar calculation (see Ex. 7.1).

因此，在情形 (i) 中，$f = XYT + Zg$，其中 $g$ 是二次的，用表达式 $(*)$ 来说，这意味着 $B = T + aZ$，并且 $Z \mid A, C, D, E, F$。因此，模 $Z^2$ 可整除的项， $$\Delta \equiv -T^2 F \mod Z^2.$$ 此外，点 $P = (0, 0, 0, 1) \in S$，$P$ 处的非奇异性意味着 $F$ 必须包含系数非零的项 $ZT^2$。特别地，$Z^2$ 不整除 $F$。因此 $(Z = 0)$ 是 $\Delta$ 的一个单根。 情形 (ii) 的计算类似（参见习题 7.1）。
