4.10 Composition of Rational Maps

有理映射的复合

The composite $g \circ f$ of rational maps $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ and $g \colon W \dashrightarrow U$ may not be defined. This is a difficulty caused by the fact that a rational map is not a map: in a natural and obvious sense, the composite is a map defined on $\operatorname{dom} f \cap f^{-1}(\operatorname{dom} g)$; however, it can perfectly well happen that this is empty (see Ex. 4.10).

有理映射 $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ 和 $g \colon W \dashrightarrow U$ 的复合 $g \circ f$ 可能没有定义。这是由有理映射并非真正映射这一事实造成的困难：从自然而显然的意义上说，复合映射定义在 $\operatorname{dom} f \cap f^{-1}(\operatorname{dom} g)$ 上；然而，这个集合完全可能是空集（见习题 4.10）。

Expressed algebraically/ˌæl.dʒɪˈbreɪ.ɪ.kli/ 代数地, the same problem also occurs: suppose that $f$ is given by $f_1, \dots, f_m \in k(V)$, so that

$$f \colon V \dashrightarrow W \subset \mathbb{A}^m \quad \text{by} \quad P \mapsto \bigl(f_1(P), \dots, f_m(P)\bigr)$$

for $P \in \bigcap \operatorname{dom} f_i$; any $g \in k[W]$ is of the form $g = G \bmod I(W)$ for some $G \in k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m]$, and $g \circ f = G(f_1, \dots, f_m)$ is well defined in $k(V)$. So exactly as in (4.4), there is a $k$-algebra homomorphism

$$f^* \colon k[W] \to k(V)$$

corresponding to $f$. However, if $h \in k[W]$ is in the kernel of $f^*$, then no meaning can be attached to $f^*(g/h)$, so that $f^*$ cannot be extended to a field homomorphism $k(W) \to k(V)$.

用代数语言表述，同样的问题也会出现：假设 $f$ 由 $f_1, \dots, f_m \in k(V)$ 给出，即 $$f \colon V \dashrightarrow W \subset \mathbb{A}^m, \quad P \mapsto \bigl(f_1(P), \dots, f_m(P)\bigr)$$ 对 $P \in \bigcap \operatorname{dom} f_i$ 成立；任何 $g \in k[W]$ 具有 $g = G \bmod I(W)$ 的形式（$G \in k[Y_1, \dots, Y_m]$），而 $g \circ f = G(f_1, \dots, f_m)$ 在 $k(V)$ 中是良定义的。于是正如 (4.4) 中一样，存在一个 $k$-代数同态 $$f^* \colon k[W] \to k(V)$$ 与 $f$ 对应。但是，如果 $h \in k[W]$ 在 $f^*$ 的核中，那么 $f^*(g/h)$ 就没有意义，因此 $f^*$ 无法扩展为域同态 $k(W) \to k(V)$。

Definition $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ is dominant/ˈdɒm.ɪ.nənt/ 占优的 if $f(\operatorname{dom} f)$ is dense in $W$ for the Zariski topology.

定义 称 $f \colon V \dashrightarrow W$ 是占优的（dominant），如果 $f(\operatorname{dom} f)$ 在 $W$ 的 Zariski 拓扑中是稠密的。

Geometrically, this means that $f^{-1}(\operatorname{dom} g) \subset \operatorname{dom} f$ is a dense open set for any rational map $g \colon W \dashrightarrow U$, so that $g \circ f$ is defined on a dense open set of $V$, so is a partially defined map $V \dashrightarrow U$.

Algebraically,

$$f \text{ is dominant} \iff f^* \colon k[W] \to k(V) \text{ is injective.}$$

For given $g \in k[W]$,

$$g \in \ker f^* \iff f(\operatorname{dom} f) \subset V(g),$$

that is, $f^*$ is not injective if and only if $f(\operatorname{dom} f)$ is contained in a strict algebraic subset of $W$.

从几何角度看，这意味着对任何有理映射 $g \colon W \dashrightarrow U$，$f^{-1}(\operatorname{dom} g) \subset \operatorname{dom} f$ 是一个稠密开集，于是 $g \circ f$ 定义在 $V$ 的一个稠密开集上，因此是一个部分定义的映射 $V \dashrightarrow U$。 从代数角度看， $$f \text{ 是占优的} \iff f^* \colon k[W] \to k(V) \text{ 是单射.}$$ 对给定的 $g \in k[W]$， $$g \in \ker f^* \iff f(\operatorname{dom} f) \subset V(g),$$ 即 $f^*$ 不是单射，当且仅当 $f(\operatorname{dom} f)$ 包含在 $W$ 的一个真代数子集中。

Clearly, the composite $g \circ f$ of rational maps $f$ and $g$ is defined provided that $f$ is dominant: $g \circ f$ is the rational map whose components are $f^*(g_i)$. Notice that the domain of $g \circ f$ certainly contains $f^{-1}(\operatorname{dom} g) \cap \operatorname{dom} f$, but may very well be larger (see Ex. 4.6).

显然，只要 $f$ 是占优的，有理映射 $f$ 和 $g$ 的复合 $g \circ f$ 就有定义：$g \circ f$ 是以 $f^*(g_i)$ 为分量的有理映射。注意 $g \circ f$ 的定义域当然包含 $f^{-1}(\operatorname{dom} g) \cap \operatorname{dom} f$，但完全可能更大（见习题 4.6）。
