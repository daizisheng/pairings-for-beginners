9.1 Taylor Series

9.1 泰勒级数

Suppose \( f \) is analytic on the open disk \( |z - z_0| < r \). Let \( z \) be any point in this disk and choose \( C \) to be the positively oriented circle of radius \( \rho \), where \( |z - z_0| < \rho < r \). Then for \( s \in C \) we have

\[ \frac{1}{s - z} = \frac{1}{s - z_0 - (z - z_0)} = \frac{1}{s - z_0} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{z - z_0}{s - z_0}} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(z - z_0)^j}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}} \]

since \( \left|\frac{z - z_0}{s - z_0}\right| < 1 \). The convergence is uniform, so we may integrate:

\[ \int_C \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j, \]

or

\[ f(z) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j. \]

假设 \( f \) 在开圆盘 \( |z - z_0| < r \) 上解析。设 \( z \) 是该圆盘内的任意一点,选取 \( C \) 为半径为 \( \rho \) 的正向圆,其中 \( |z - z_0| < \rho < r \)。那么对于 \( s \in C \),我们有 \[ \frac{1}{s - z} = \frac{1}{s - z_0} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{z - z_0}{s - z_0}} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(z - z_0)^j}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}} \] 因为 \( \left|\frac{z - z_0}{s - z_0}\right| < 1 \)。收敛是一致的,所以我们可以逐项积分。

We have thus produced a power series having the given analytic function as a limit:

\[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j, \quad |z - z_0| < r, \]

where

\[ c_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds. \]

This is the celebrated Taylor Series for \( f \) at \( z = z_0 \).

我们因此得到了一个以给定解析函数为极限的幂级数: \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j, \quad |z - z_0| < r, \] 其中 \[ c_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds. \] 这就是著名的 \( f \) 在 \( z = z_0 \) 处的泰勒级数。

We know we may differentiate the series to get

\[ f'(z) = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} j\, c_j (z - z_0)^{j-1} \]

and this one converges uniformly where the series for \( f \) does. We can thus differentiate again and again to obtain

\[ f^{(n)}(z) = \sum_{j=n}^{\infty} j(j-1)(j-2)\cdots(j-n+1)\, c_j (z - z_0)^{j-n}. \]

Hence,

\[ f^{(n)}(z_0) = n!\, c_n, \quad \text{or} \quad c_n = \frac{f^{(n)}(z_0)}{n!}. \]

我们知道可以对级数逐项求导,得到 \[ f'(z) = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} j\, c_j (z - z_0)^{j-1}, \] 并且此级数在 \( f \) 的级数收敛的地方一致收敛。因此我们可以反复求导,得到 \[ f^{(n)}(z_0) = n!\, c_n, \quad \text{即} \quad c_n = \frac{f^{(n)}(z_0)}{n!}. \]

But we also know that

\[ c_n = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{n+1}}\,ds. \]

This gives us

\[ f^{(n)}(z_0) = \frac{n!}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{n+1}}\,ds, \quad \text{for } n = 0, 1, 2, \dots \]

This is the famous Generalized Cauchy Integral Formula. Recall that we previously derived this formula for \( n = 0 \) and \( n = 1 \).

但我们也知道 \[ c_n = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{n+1}}\,ds. \] 这给出了 \[ f^{(n)}(z_0) = \frac{n!}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{n+1}}\,ds, \quad n = 0, 1, 2, \dots \] 这就是著名的广义柯西积分公式。回忆我们之前已经对 \( n = 0 \) 和 \( n = 1 \) 的情形推导过此公式。

What does all this tell us about the radius of convergence of a power series? Suppose we have

\[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j, \]

and the radius of convergence is \( R \). Then we know, of course, that the limit function \( f \) is analytic for \( |z - z_0| < R \). We showed that if \( f \) is analytic in \( |z - z_0| < r \), then the series converges for \( |z - z_0| < r \). Thus \( r \leq R \), and so \( f \) cannot be analytic at any point \( z \) for which \( |z - z_0| = R \). In other words, the circle of convergence is the largest circle centered at \( z_0 \) inside of which the limit \( f \) is analytic.

这一切告诉我们关于幂级数收敛半径的什么信息?假设我们有 \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j, \] 收敛半径为 \( R \)。那么极限函数 \( f \) 在 \( |z - z_0| < R \) 内解析。我们已经证明,如果 \( f \) 在 \( |z - z_0| < r \) 内解析,则级数在 \( |z - z_0| < r \) 内收敛。因此 \( r \leq R \),所以 \( f \) 在满足 \( |z - z_0| = R \) 的任何点 \( z \) 处都不解析。换句话说,收敛圆是以 \( z_0 \) 为圆心、使得极限 \( f \) 在其内部解析的最大圆。

Example

Let \( f(z) = \exp(z) = e^z \). Then \( f(0) = f'(0) = \cdots = f^{(n)}(0) = 1 \), and the Taylor series for \( f \) at \( z_0 = 0 \) is

\[ e^z = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!}\, z^j \]

and this is valid for all values of \( z \) since \( f \) is entire. (We also showed earlier that this particular series has an infinite radius of convergence.)

例子

设 \( f(z) = \exp(z) = e^z \)。那么 \( f(0) = f'(0) = \cdots = f^{(n)}(0) = 1 \),\( f \) 在 \( z_0 = 0 \) 处的泰勒级数为 \[ e^z = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!}\, z^j \] 这对所有 \( z \) 的值都成立,因为 \( f \) 是整函数。(我们之前也证明了这个特定级数有无穷大的收敛半径。)

9.2 Laurent Series

9.2 洛朗级数

Suppose \( f \) is analytic in the region \( R_1 < |z - z_0| < R_2 \), and let \( C \) be a positively oriented simple closed curve around \( z_0 \) in this region. (Note: we include the possibilities that \( R_1 \) can be 0, and \( R_2 = \infty \).) We shall show that for \( z \) not on \( C \) in this region

\[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} a_j (z - z_0)^j + \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \frac{b_j}{(z - z_0)^j}, \]

where

\[ a_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds, \quad \text{for } j = 0, 1, 2, \dots \]

and

\[ b_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C f(s)(s - z_0)^{j-1}\,ds, \quad \text{for } j = 1, 2, \dots \]

假设 \( f \) 在区域 \( R_1 < |z - z_0| < R_2 \) 内解析,设 \( C \) 是该区域内围绕 \( z_0 \) 的正向简单闭合曲线。(注意:我们包括 \( R_1 \) 可以为 0 以及 \( R_2 = \infty \) 的情形。)我们将证明对该区域内不在 \( C \) 上的点 \( z \), \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} a_j (z - z_0)^j + \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \frac{b_j}{(z - z_0)^j}, \] 其中 \[ a_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds, \quad j = 0, 1, 2, \dots \] \[ b_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C f(s)(s - z_0)^{j-1}\,ds, \quad j = 1, 2, \dots \]

The sum of the limits of these two series is frequently written

\[ f(z) = \sum_{j=-\infty}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j, \]

where

\[ c_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds, \quad j = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \dots \]

This recipe for \( f(z) \) is called a Laurent series, although it is important to keep in mind that it is really two series.

这两个级数的极限之和通常写成 \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=-\infty}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j, \] 其中 \[ c_j = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds, \quad j = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \dots \] \( f(z) \) 的这种表示称为洛朗级数,但重要的是要记住它实际上是两个级数。

Okay, now let's derive the above formula. First, let \( r_1 \) and \( r_2 \) be so that \( R_1 < r_1 < |z - z_0| < r_2 < R_2 \) and so that the point \( z \) and the curve \( C \) are included in the region \( r_1 < |z - z_0| < r_2 \). Also, let \( \gamma \) be a circle centered at \( z \) and such that \( \gamma \) is included in this region.

现在让我们推导上述公式。首先,设 \( r_1 \) 和 \( r_2 \) 满足 \( R_1 < r_1 < |z - z_0| < r_2 < R_2 \),并使得点 \( z \) 和曲线 \( C \) 都包含在区域 \( r_1 < |z - z_0| < r_2 \) 内。另设 \( \gamma \) 为以 \( z \) 为圆心的圆,使得 \( \gamma \) 也包含在该区域内。

Figure: The annular region for the Laurent series derivation

图:洛朗级数推导中的环形区域

Then \( \frac{f(s)}{s - z} \) is an analytic function (of \( s \)) on the region bounded by \( C_1 \), \( C_2 \), and \( \gamma \), where \( C_1 \) is the circle \( |z - z_0| = r_1 \) and \( C_2 \) is the circle \( |z - z_0| = r_2 \). Thus,

\[ \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds + \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds. \]

(All three circles are positively oriented, of course.) But \( \displaystyle\int_{\gamma} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = 2\pi i f(z) \), and so we have

\[ 2\pi i\, f(z) = \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds - \int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds. \]

那么 \( \frac{f(s)}{s - z} \) 是在由 \( C_1 \)、\( C_2 \) 和 \( \gamma \) 所围区域上关于 \( s \) 的解析函数,其中 \( C_1 \) 是圆 \( |z - z_0| = r_1 \),\( C_2 \) 是圆 \( |z - z_0| = r_2 \)。因此, \[ \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds + \int_{\gamma} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds. \] (当然,三个圆都是正向的。)但 \( \displaystyle\int_{\gamma} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = 2\pi i\, f(z) \),因此 \[ 2\pi i\, f(z) = \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds - \int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds. \]

Look at the first of the two integrals on the right-hand side of this equation. For \( s \in C_2 \), we have \( |z - z_0| < |s - z_0| \), and so

\[ \frac{1}{s - z} = \frac{1}{s - z_0 - (z - z_0)} = \frac{1}{s - z_0} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{z - z_0}{s - z_0}} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(z - z_0)^j}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}. \]

Hence,

\[ \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j. \]

看等式右边两个积分中的第一个。对于 \( s \in C_2 \),我们有 \( |z - z_0| < |s - z_0| \),因此 \[ \frac{1}{s - z} = \frac{1}{s - z_0} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{z - z_0}{s - z_0}} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(z - z_0)^j}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}. \] 从而 \[ \int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j. \]

For the second of these two integrals, note that for \( s \in C_1 \) we have \( |s - z_0| < |z - z_0| \), and so

\[ \frac{-1}{s - z} = \frac{1}{z - z_0 - (s - z_0)} = \frac{1}{z - z_0} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{s - z_0}{z - z_0}} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(s - z_0)^j}{(z - z_0)^{j+1}} = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \frac{(s - z_0)^{j-1}}{(z - z_0)^j}. \]

As before,

\[ -\int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)\,(s - z_0)^{j-1}}{1}\,ds \cdot \frac{1}{(z - z_0)^j} = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \int_C f(s)(s - z_0)^{j-1}\,ds \cdot \frac{1}{(z - z_0)^j}. \]

对于第二个积分,注意对于 \( s \in C_1 \),我们有 \( |s - z_0| < |z - z_0| \),因此 \[ \frac{-1}{s - z} = \frac{1}{z - z_0} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{s - z_0}{z - z_0}} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{(s - z_0)^j}{(z - z_0)^{j+1}} = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \frac{(s - z_0)^{j-1}}{(z - z_0)^j}. \] 如前所述, \[ -\int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \int_C f(s)(s - z_0)^{j-1}\,ds \cdot \frac{1}{(z - z_0)^j}. \]

Putting this all together, we have the Laurent series:

\[ f(z) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_{C_2} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds - \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_{C_1} \frac{f(s)}{s - z}\,ds \]

\[ = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j + \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C f(s)(s - z_0)^{j-1}\,ds \cdot \frac{1}{(z - z_0)^j}. \]

把这些合在一起,我们得到洛朗级数: \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(s)}{(s - z_0)^{j+1}}\,ds \cdot (z - z_0)^j + \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C f(s)(s - z_0)^{j-1}\,ds \cdot \frac{1}{(z - z_0)^j}. \]

Example

Let \( f \) be defined by

\[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z(z - 1)}. \]

First, observe that \( f \) is analytic in the region \( 0 < |z| < 1 \). Let's find the Laurent series for \( f \) valid in this region. First,

\[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z(z - 1)} = -\frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - z}. \]

From our vast knowledge of the geometric series, we have

\[ f(z) = -\frac{1}{z}\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} z^j = -\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} z^{j-1} = -\frac{1}{z} - 1 - z - z^2 - \cdots \]

例子

设 \( f \) 由 \[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z(z - 1)} \] 定义。首先注意 \( f \) 在区域 \( 0 < |z| < 1 \) 内解析。我们来求 \( f \) 在此区域内有效的洛朗级数。首先, \[ f(z) = -\frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - z}. \] 利用我们对几何级数的丰富知识, \[ f(z) = -\frac{1}{z}\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} z^j = -\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} z^{j-1}. \]

Example (continued)

Now let's find another Laurent series for \( f \), the one valid for the region \( 1 < |z| < \infty \). First,

\[ \frac{1}{z - 1} = \frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{z}}. \]

Now since \( \left|\frac{1}{z}\right| < 1 \), we have

\[ \frac{1}{z - 1} = \frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{z}} = \frac{1}{z}\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{z^j} = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} z^{-j}, \]

and so

\[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{z - 1} = \frac{1}{z}\sum_{j=1}^{\infty} z^{-j} = \sum_{j=2}^{\infty} z^{-j}. \]

例子(续)

现在让我们求 \( f \) 的另一个洛朗级数,即在区域 \( 1 < |z| < \infty \) 内有效的那个。首先, \[ \frac{1}{z - 1} = \frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{z}}. \] 由于 \( \left|\frac{1}{z}\right| < 1 \),我们有 \[ \frac{1}{z - 1} = \frac{1}{z}\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{z^j} = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} z^{-j}, \] 因此 \[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z} \cdot \frac{1}{z - 1} = \frac{1}{z}\sum_{j=1}^{\infty} z^{-j} = \sum_{j=2}^{\infty} z^{-j}. \]
