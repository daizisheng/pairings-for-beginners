6.1 Cauchy's Integral Formula

Suppose \( f \) is analytic in a region containing a simple closed contour \( C \) with the usual positive orientation and its inside, and suppose \( z_0 \) is inside \( C \). Then it turns out that

\[ f(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz. \]

This is the famous Cauchy Integral Formula. Let's see why it's true.

假设 \( f \) 在包含简单闭合轮廓 \( C \) 及其内部的区域上解析,且 \( z_0 \) 在 \( C \) 内。那么\[ f(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz. \]这就是著名的柯西积分公式。

Let \( \varepsilon > 0 \) be any positive number. We know that \( f \) is continuous at \( z_0 \) and so there is a number \( \delta \) such that \( |f(z) - f(z_0)| < \varepsilon \) whenever \( |z - z_0| < \delta \). Now let \( \rho > 0 \) be a number such that \( \rho < \delta \) and the circle \( C_\rho = \{z : |z - z_0| = \rho\} \) is also inside \( C \). Now, the function \( \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0} \) is analytic in the region between \( C \) and \( C_\rho \); thus

\[ \int_C \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz = \int_{C_\rho} \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz. \]

We know that \( \int_{C_\rho} \frac{1}{z-z_0}\,dz = 2\pi i \), so we can write

\[ \int_{C_\rho} \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz - 2\pi if(z_0) = \int_{C_\rho} \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz - f(z_0)\int_{C_\rho} \frac{1}{z-z_0}\,dz = \int_{C_\rho} \frac{f(z) - f(z_0)}{z-z_0}\,dz. \]

For \( z \in C_\rho \) we have \( \frac{|f(z) - f(z_0)|}{|z-z_0|} \leq \frac{\varepsilon}{\rho} \). Thus, \( \left|\int_{C_\rho} \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz - 2\pi if(z_0)\right| \leq \frac{\varepsilon}{\rho} \cdot 2\pi\rho = 2\pi\varepsilon \). But \( \varepsilon \) is any positive number, and so \( \int_{C_\rho} \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz = 2\pi if(z_0) \), or \( f(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz \), which is exactly what we set out to show.

经过证明,我们得到了柯西积分公式。它说如果 \( f \) 在简单闭合曲线上及其内部解析,并且我们知道曲线上每个 \( z \) 的值 \( f(z) \),那么我们就知道曲线内部每个点的函数值——确实非常了不起。

Meditate on this result. It says that if \( f \) is analytic on and inside a simple closed curve and we know the values \( f(z) \) for every \( z \) on the simple closed curve, then we know the value for the function at every point inside the curve—quite remarkable indeed.

Example

Let \( C \) be the circle \( |z| = 4 \) traversed once in the counterclockwise direction. Let's evaluate the integral \( \int_C \frac{\cos z}{z^2 + 6z + 5}\,dz \). We simply write the integrand as \( \frac{\cos z}{z^2 + 6z + 5} = \frac{\cos z}{(z+5)(z+1)} = \frac{f(z)}{z+1} \), where \( f(z) = \frac{\cos z}{z+5} \). Observe that \( f \) is analytic on and inside \( C \), and so, \( \int_C \frac{\cos z}{z^2 + 6z + 5}\,dz = \int_C \frac{f(z)}{z+1}\,dz = 2\pi if(-1) = 2\pi i\frac{\cos(-1)}{-1+5} = \frac{\pi i\cos 1}{2} \).

例子

设 \( C \) 是逆时针方向遍历一次的圆 \( |z| = 4 \)。我们求积分 \( \int_C \frac{\cos z}{z^2 + 6z + 5}\,dz \)。经计算得 \( \frac{\pi i\cos 1}{2} \)。

6.2 Functions Defined by Integrals

Suppose \( C \) is a curve (not necessarily a simple closed curve, just a curve) and suppose the function \( g \) is continuous on \( C \) (not necessarily analytic, just continuous). Let the function \( G \) be defined by \( G(z) = \int_C \frac{g(s)}{s-z}\,ds \) for all \( z \notin C \). We shall show that \( G \) is analytic.

假设 \( C \) 是一条曲线(不一定是简单闭合曲线,只是曲线),假设函数 \( g \) 在 \( C \) 上连续(不一定解析,只是连续)。设函数 \( G \) 定义为 \( G(z) = \int_C \frac{g(s)}{s-z}\,ds \),对所有 \( z \notin C \)。我们将证明 \( G \) 是解析的。

Consider, \( \frac{G(z+\Delta z) - G(z)}{\Delta z} = \frac{1}{\Delta z}\left[\frac{1}{s-z-\Delta z} - \frac{1}{s-z}\right]g(s)\,ds = \int_C \frac{g(s)}{(s-z-\Delta z)(s-z)}\,ds \). Through careful estimation using continuity and showing the limit exists, we can prove that \( G'(z) = \int_C \frac{g(s)}{(s-z)^2}\,ds \). Truly a miracle!

通过仔细的估计和极限分析,我们证明了 \( G'(z) = \int_C \frac{g(s)}{(s-z)^2}\,ds \)。真是奇迹!

6.3 Derivatives of Analytic Functions

Now suppose \( f \) is analytic in a region containing a simple closed curve \( C \) and its inside. From Cauchy's Integral Formula, for any \( z_0 \) inside \( C \), we have \( f(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{z-z_0}\,dz \). Using the result from the previous section, we can differentiate this integral:

\[ f'(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{(z-z_0)^2}\,dz. \]

More generally, for the \( n \)-th derivative:

\[ f^{(n)}(z_0) = \frac{n!}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{(z-z_0)^{n+1}}\,dz. \]

现在假设 \( f \) 在包含简单闭合曲线 \( C \) 及其内部的区域上解析。使用柯西积分公式和上一节的结果,我们可以对积分求导,得到 \( f'(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{(z-z_0)^2}\,dz \)。更一般地,\( f^{(n)}(z_0) = \frac{n!}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f(z)}{(z-z_0)^{n+1}}\,dz \)。

6.4 Liouville's Theorem

A function that is analytic in the entire complex plane is called an entire function. A function \( f \) is said to be bounded if there is a number \( M \) such that \( |f(z)| \leq M \) for all \( z \).

Liouville's Theorem: If \( f \) is a bounded entire function, then \( f \) is constant.

在整个复平面上解析的函数称为整函数。如果存在数 \( M \) 使得对所有 \( z \) 有 \( |f(z)| \leq M \),则称函数 \( f \) 是有界的。刘维尔定理:如果 \( f \) 是有界整函数,那么 \( f \) 是常数。

Figure: Liouville's Theorem

图:刘维尔定理

Proof: Let \( f \) be a bounded entire function. Then there is \( M \) such that \( |f(z)| \leq M \) for all \( z \). For any \( z_0 \) and any \( R > 0 \), let \( C_R \) be the circle \( |z-z_0| = R \). Then \( f'(z_0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_{C_R} \frac{f(z)}{(z-z_0)^2}\,dz \). Taking absolute values: \( |f'(z_0)| \leq \frac{1}{2\pi} \cdot \frac{M}{R^2} \cdot 2\pi R = \frac{M}{R} \). Since this holds for all \( R > 0 \), letting \( R \to \infty \) gives \( f'(z_0) = 0 \). Since \( z_0 \) was arbitrary, \( f' = 0 \) everywhere, so \( f \) is constant.

证明:设 \( f \) 是有界整函数。那么存在 \( M \) 使得对所有 \( z \) 有 \( |f(z)| \leq M \)。通过在任意半径 \( R \) 的圆上应用柯西积分公式并估计,我们得到 \( |f'(z_0)| \leq \frac{M}{R} \)。由于这对所有 \( R > 0 \) 成立,令 \( R \to \infty \) 得 \( f'(z_0) = 0 \)。由于 \( z_0 \) 是任意的,\( f' = 0 \) 处处成立,所以 \( f \) 是常数。
