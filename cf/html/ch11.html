11.1 Argument Principle

11.1 辐角原理

Let \( C \) be a simple closed curve, and suppose \( f \) is analytic on \( C \). Suppose moreover that the only singularities of \( f \) inside \( C \) are poles. If \( f(z) \neq 0 \) for all \( z \in C \), then \( \Gamma = f(C) \) is a closed curve which does not pass through the origin. If

\[ \gamma(t), \quad \alpha \leq t \leq \beta \]

is a complex description of \( C \), then

\[ \zeta(t) = f(\gamma(t)), \quad \alpha \leq t \leq \beta \]

is a complex description of \( \Gamma \).

设 \( C \) 是一条简单闭合曲线,假设 \( f \) 在 \( C \) 上解析。进一步假设 \( f \) 在 \( C \) 内部的奇点全部是极点。如果对所有 \( z \in C \) 有 \( f(z) \neq 0 \),那么 \( \Gamma = f(C) \) 是一条不经过原点的闭曲线。如果 \( \gamma(t),\; \alpha \leq t \leq \beta \) 是 \( C \) 的复参数描述,那么 \( \zeta(t) = f(\gamma(t)),\; \alpha \leq t \leq \beta \) 是 \( \Gamma \) 的复参数描述。

Now, let's compute

\[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = \int_\alpha^\beta \frac{f'(\gamma(t))}{f(\gamma(t))}\,\gamma'(t)\,dt. \]

But notice that \( \zeta'(t) = f'(\gamma(t))\,\gamma'(t) \). Hence,

\[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = \int_\alpha^\beta \frac{f'(\gamma(t))}{f(\gamma(t))}\,\gamma'(t)\,dt = \int_\alpha^\beta \frac{\zeta'(t)}{\zeta(t)}\,dt = \int_\Gamma \frac{1}{z}\,dz = n \cdot 2\pi i, \]

where \( |n| \) is the number of times \( \Gamma \) "winds around" the origin. The integer \( n \) is positive in case \( \Gamma \) is traversed in the positive direction, and negative in case the traversal is in the negative direction.

现在我们来计算 \[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = \int_\alpha^\beta \frac{f'(\gamma(t))}{f(\gamma(t))}\,\gamma'(t)\,dt. \] 注意到 \( \zeta'(t) = f'(\gamma(t))\,\gamma'(t) \),因此 \[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = \int_\alpha^\beta \frac{\zeta'(t)}{\zeta(t)}\,dt = \int_\Gamma \frac{1}{z}\,dz = n \cdot 2\pi i, \] 其中 \( |n| \) 是 \( \Gamma \) 绕原点"缠绕"的次数。整数 \( n \) 在 \( \Gamma \) 沿正方向遍历时为正,沿负方向遍历时为负。

Next, we shall use the Residue Theorem to evaluate the integral \( \displaystyle\int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz \). The singularities of the integrand \( \dfrac{f'(z)}{f(z)} \) are the poles of \( f \) together with the zeros of \( f \). Let's find the residues at these points.

接下来,我们用留数定理来计算积分 \( \displaystyle\int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz \)。被积函数 \( \dfrac{f'(z)}{f(z)} \) 的奇点是 \( f \) 的极点和 \( f \) 的零点。我们来求这些点处的留数。

Residues at the Zeros of \( f \)

在 \( f \) 的零点处的留数

First, let \( Z = \{z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_K\} \) be the set of all zeros of \( f \). Suppose the order of the zero \( z_j \) is \( n_j \). Then

\[ f(z) = (z - z_j)^{n_j} h(z) \quad \text{and} \quad h(z_j) \neq 0. \]

Thus,

\[ \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = \frac{(z - z_j)^{n_j} h'(z) + n_j (z - z_j)^{n_j - 1} h(z)}{(z - z_j)^{n_j} h(z)} = \frac{h'(z)}{h(z)} + \frac{n_j}{z - z_j}. \]

Then

\[ \varphi(z) = (z - z_j)\frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = (z - z_j)\frac{h'(z)}{h(z)} + n_j, \]

and

\[ \operatorname{Res}_{z = z_j} \frac{f'}{f} = n_j. \]

The sum of all these residues is thus

\[ N = n_1 + n_2 + \cdots + n_K. \]

设 \( Z = \{z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_K\} \) 是 \( f \) 的所有零点的集合。假设零点 \( z_j \) 的阶为 \( n_j \)。那么 \[ f(z) = (z - z_j)^{n_j} h(z), \quad h(z_j) \neq 0. \] 因此 \[ \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = \frac{h'(z)}{h(z)} + \frac{n_j}{z - z_j}. \] 从而 \( \displaystyle\operatorname{Res}_{z = z_j} \frac{f'}{f} = n_j \)。所有这些留数之和为 \( N = n_1 + n_2 + \cdots + n_K \)。

Residues at the Poles of \( f \)

在 \( f \) 的极点处的留数

Next, we go after the residues at the poles of \( f \). Let the set of poles of \( f \) be \( P = \{p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_J\} \). Suppose \( p_j \) is a pole of order \( m_j \). Then

\[ h(z) = (z - p_j)^{m_j} f(z) \]

is analytic at \( p_j \). In other words,

\[ f(z) = \frac{h(z)}{(z - p_j)^{m_j}}. \]

Hence,

\[ \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = \frac{(z - p_j)^{m_j} h'(z) - m_j (z - p_j)^{m_j - 1} h(z)}{(z - p_j)^{2m_j}} \cdot \frac{(z - p_j)^{m_j}}{h(z)} = \frac{h'(z)}{h(z)} - \frac{m_j}{z - p_j}. \]

Now then,

\[ \varphi(z) = (z - p_j)\frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = (z - p_j)\frac{h'(z)}{h(z)} - m_j, \]

and so

\[ \operatorname{Res}_{z = p_j} \frac{f'}{f} = \varphi(p_j) = -m_j. \]

The sum of all these residues is

\[ -P = -m_1 - m_2 - \cdots - m_J. \]

接下来求 \( f \) 的极点处的留数。设 \( f \) 的极点集合为 \( P = \{p_1, p_2, \ldots, p_J\} \)。假设 \( p_j \) 是 \( m_j \) 阶极点。那么 \( h(z) = (z - p_j)^{m_j} f(z) \) 在 \( p_j \) 处解析,即 \[ f(z) = \frac{h(z)}{(z - p_j)^{m_j}}. \] 由此得到 \[ \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)} = \frac{h'(z)}{h(z)} - \frac{m_j}{z - p_j}. \] 因此 \( \displaystyle\operatorname{Res}_{z = p_j} \frac{f'}{f} = -m_j \)。所有这些留数之和为 \( -P = -m_1 - m_2 - \cdots - m_J \)。

The Argument Principle

辐角原理

Then,

\[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = 2\pi i(N - P); \]

and we already found that

\[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = n \cdot 2\pi i, \]

where \( n \) is the "winding number", or the number of times \( \Gamma \) winds around the origin -- \( n > 0 \) means \( \Gamma \) winds in the positive sense, and \( n \) negative means it winds in the negative sense. Finally, we have

\[ \boxed{n = N - P,} \]

where \( N = n_1 + n_2 + \cdots + n_K \) is the number of zeros inside \( C \), counting multiplicity (or the order of the zeros), and \( P = m_1 + m_2 + \cdots + m_J \) is the number of poles, counting the order. This result is the celebrated Argument Principle.

于是 \[ \int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = 2\pi i(N - P), \] 而我们已经得到 \( \displaystyle\int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz = n \cdot 2\pi i \)。最终我们得到 \[ \boxed{n = N - P,} \] 其中 \( N = n_1 + n_2 + \cdots + n_K \) 是 \( C \) 内部零点的个数(计入重数),\( P = m_1 + m_2 + \cdots + m_J \) 是极点的个数(计入阶数)。这就是著名的辐角原理。

11.2 Rouché's Theorem

11.2 儒歇定理

Suppose \( f \) and \( g \) are analytic on and inside a simple closed contour \( C \). Suppose moreover that \( |f(z)| > |g(z)| \) for all \( z \in C \). Then we shall see that \( f \) and \( f + g \) have the same number of zeros inside \( C \). This result is Rouché's Theorem.

假设 \( f \) 和 \( g \) 在简单闭合轮廓 \( C \) 上及其内部解析。进一步假设对所有 \( z \in C \) 有 \( |f(z)| > |g(z)| \)。那么 \( f \) 和 \( f + g \) 在 \( C \) 内部有相同数目的零点。这就是儒歇定理。

To see why it is so, start by defining the function \( \Psi(t) \) on the interval \( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \):

\[ \Psi(t) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f'(z) + t\,g'(z)}{f(z) + t\,g(z)}\,dz. \]

Observe that this is okay -- that is, the denominator of the integrand is never zero:

\[ |f(z) + t\,g(z)| \geq \bigl||f(z)| - t|g(z)|\bigr| \geq \bigl||f(z)| - |g(z)|\bigr| > 0. \]

为了说明这一点,我们在区间 \( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \) 上定义函数 \[ \Psi(t) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f'(z) + t\,g'(z)}{f(z) + t\,g(z)}\,dz. \] 注意被积函数的分母永远不为零: \[ |f(z) + t\,g(z)| \geq \bigl||f(z)| - t|g(z)|\bigr| \geq \bigl||f(z)| - |g(z)|\bigr| > 0. \]

Observe that \( \Psi \) is continuous on the interval \( [0, 1] \) and is integer-valued -- \( \Psi(t) \) is the number of zeros of \( f + tg \) inside \( C \). Being continuous and integer-valued on the connected set \( [0, 1] \), it must be constant. In particular, \( \Psi(0) = \Psi(1) \). This does the job!

\[ \Psi(0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz \]

is the number of zeros of \( f \) inside \( C \), and

\[ \Psi(1) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f'(z) + g'(z)}{f(z) + g(z)}\,dz \]

is the number of zeros of \( f + g \) inside \( C \).

注意 \( \Psi \) 在区间 \( [0, 1] \) 上连续且取整数值 -- \( \Psi(t) \) 是 \( f + tg \) 在 \( C \) 内部的零点个数。在连通集 \( [0, 1] \) 上连续且取整数值的函数必为常数。特别地,\( \Psi(0) = \Psi(1) \)。这就完成了证明!其中 \[ \Psi(0) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f'(z)}{f(z)}\,dz \] 是 \( f \) 在 \( C \) 内部的零点个数,而 \[ \Psi(1) = \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{f'(z) + g'(z)}{f(z) + g(z)}\,dz \] 是 \( f + g \) 在 \( C \) 内部的零点个数。

Example

How many solutions of the equation \( z^6 - 5z^5 + z^3 - 2 = 0 \) are inside the circle \( |z| = 1 \)? Rouché's Theorem makes it quite easy to answer this. Simply let \( f(z) = -5z^5 \) and let \( g(z) = z^6 + z^3 - 2 \). Then \( |f(z)| = 5 \) and \( |g(z)| \leq |z|^6 + |z|^3 + 2 = 4 \) for all \( |z| = 1 \). Hence \( |f(z)| > |g(z)| \) on the unit circle. From Rouché's Theorem we know then that \( f \) and \( f + g \) have the same number of zeros inside \( |z| = 1 \). Thus, there are 5 such solutions.

例子

方程 \( z^6 - 5z^5 + z^3 - 2 = 0 \) 在圆 \( |z| = 1 \) 内有多少个解?利用儒歇定理很容易回答。令 \( f(z) = -5z^5 \),\( g(z) = z^6 + z^3 - 2 \)。当 \( |z| = 1 \) 时,\( |f(z)| = 5 \) 而 \( |g(z)| \leq |z|^6 + |z|^3 + 2 = 4 \)。因此在单位圆上 \( |f(z)| > |g(z)| \)。由儒歇定理,\( f \) 和 \( f + g \) 在 \( |z| = 1 \) 内部有相同数目的零点。因此有 5 个解。

Hurwitz's Theorem

赫尔维茨定理

The following nice result follows easily from Rouché's Theorem. Suppose \( U \) is an open set (i.e., every point of \( U \) is an interior point) and suppose that a sequence \( \{f_n\} \) of functions analytic on \( U \) converges uniformly to the function \( f \). Suppose further that \( f \) is not zero on the circle \( C = \{z : |z - z_0| = R\} \subset U \). Then there is an integer \( N \) so that for all \( n \geq N \), the functions \( f_n \) and \( f \) have the same number of zeros inside \( C \).

下面这个漂亮的结果很容易从儒歇定理推出。假设 \( U \) 是一个开集(即 \( U \) 的每个点都是内点),假设在 \( U \) 上解析的函数列 \( \{f_n\} \) 一致收敛到函数 \( f \)。进一步假设 \( f \) 在圆 \( C = \{z : |z - z_0| = R\} \subset U \) 上不为零。那么存在整数 \( N \) 使得对所有 \( n \geq N \),函数 \( f_n \) 和 \( f \) 在 \( C \) 内部有相同数目的零点。

This result, called Hurwitz's Theorem, is an easy consequence of Rouché's Theorem. Simply observe that for \( z \in C \), we have \( |f(z)| > \varepsilon > 0 \) for some \( \varepsilon \). Now let \( N \) be large enough to insure that \( |f_n(z) - f(z)| < \varepsilon \) on \( C \). It follows from Rouché's Theorem that \( f \) and \( f + (f_n - f) = f_n \) have the same number of zeros inside \( C \).

这个结果称为赫尔维茨定理,是儒歇定理的简单推论。只需注意对 \( z \in C \),有 \( |f(z)| > \varepsilon > 0 \) 对某个 \( \varepsilon \) 成立。取 \( N \) 足够大使得在 \( C \) 上 \( |f_n(z) - f(z)| < \varepsilon \)。由儒歇定理,\( f \) 和 \( f + (f_n - f) = f_n \) 在 \( C \) 内部有相同数目的零点。

Example

On any bounded set, the sequence \( \{f_n\} \), where \( f_n(z) = 1 + z + \dfrac{z^2}{2!} + \cdots + \dfrac{z^n}{n!} \), converges uniformly to \( f(z) = e^z \), and \( f(z) \neq 0 \) for all \( z \). Thus for any \( R \), there is an \( N \) so that for \( n > N \), every zero of \( 1 + z + \dfrac{z^2}{2!} + \cdots + \dfrac{z^n}{n!} \) has modulus \( > R \). Or to put it another way, given any \( R \) there is an \( N \) so that for \( n > N \) no polynomial \( 1 + z + \dfrac{z^2}{2!} + \cdots + \dfrac{z^n}{n!} \) has a zero inside the circle of radius \( R \).

例子

在任意有界集上,函数列 \( f_n(z) = 1 + z + \dfrac{z^2}{2!} + \cdots + \dfrac{z^n}{n!} \) 一致收敛到 \( f(z) = e^z \),且对所有 \( z \) 有 \( f(z) \neq 0 \)。因此对任意 \( R \),存在 \( N \) 使得当 \( n > N \) 时,\( 1 + z + \dfrac{z^2}{2!} + \cdots + \dfrac{z^n}{n!} \) 的每个零点的模都大于 \( R \)。换言之,给定任意 \( R \),存在 \( N \) 使得当 \( n > N \) 时,多项式 \( 1 + z + \dfrac{z^2}{2!} + \cdots + \dfrac{z^n}{n!} \) 在半径为 \( R \) 的圆内没有零点。
