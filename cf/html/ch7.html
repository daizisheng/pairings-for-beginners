7.1 Power Series

7.1 幂级数

A power series is a series of the form \( \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n(z-z_0)^n \) where \( a_n \) and \( z_0 \) are complex numbers.

幂级数是形式为 \( \sum_{n=0}^\infty a_n(z-z_0)^n \) 的级数,其中 \( a_n \) 和 \( z_0 \) 是复数。

7.2 Taylor Series

7.2 泰勒级数

If \( f \) is analytic in a disk \( |z-z_0| < R \), then \( f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{f^{(n)}(z_0)}{n!}(z-z_0)^n \).

如果 \( f \) 在圆盘 \( |z-z_0| < R \) 中解析,那么 \( f(z) = \sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{f^{(n)}(z_0)}{n!}(z-z_0)^n \)。
