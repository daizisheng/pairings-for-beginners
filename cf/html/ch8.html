8.1 Sequences

8.1 序列

The basic definitions for complex sequences and series are essentially the same as for the real case. A sequence of complex numbers is a function \( g: \mathbb{Z}^+ \to \mathbb{C} \) from the positive integers into the complex numbers. It is traditional to use subscripts to indicate the values of the function. Thus we write \( g(n) = z_n \) and an explicit name for the sequence is seldom used; we write simply \( \{z_n\} \) to stand for the sequence \( g \) which is such that \( g(n) = z_n \). For example, \( \{i^n\} \) is the sequence \( g \) for which \( g(n) = i^n \).

复序列和级数的基本定义与实数情形本质上相同。复数的序列是从正整数到复数的函数 \( g: \mathbb{Z}^+ \to \mathbb{C} \)。按照惯例,我们用下标来表示函数的值。因此我们写 \( g(n) = z_n \),序列的显式名称很少使用;我们简单地用 \( \{z_n\} \) 来表示序列 \( g \),其中 \( g(n) = z_n \)。例如,\( \{i^n\} \) 是满足 \( g(n) = i^n \) 的序列 \( g \)。

The number \( L \) is a limit of the sequence \( \{z_n\} \) if given an \( \varepsilon > 0 \), there is an integer \( N \) such that \( |z_n - L| < \varepsilon \) for all \( n > N_\varepsilon \). If \( L \) is a limit of \( \{z_n\} \), we sometimes say that \( \{z_n\} \) converges to \( L \). We frequently write \( \lim z_n = L \).

数 \( L \) 是序列 \( \{z_n\} \) 的极限,如果对于给定的 \( \varepsilon > 0 \),存在整数 \( N \) 使得当 \( n > N_\varepsilon \) 时 \( |z_n - L| < \varepsilon \)。如果 \( L \) 是 \( \{z_n\} \) 的极限,我们有时说 \( \{z_n\} \) 收敛到 \( L \)。我们经常写作 \( \lim z_n = L \)。

It is relatively easy to see that if the complex sequence \( \{z_n\} = \{u_n + iv_n\} \) converges to \( L \), then the two real sequences \( \{u_n\} \) and \( \{v_n\} \) each have a limit: \( \{u_n\} \) converges to \( \operatorname{Re} L \) and \( \{v_n\} \) converges to \( \operatorname{Im} L \). Conversely, if the two real sequences \( \{u_n\} \) and \( \{v_n\} \) each have a limit, then so also does the complex sequence \( \{u_n + iv_n\} \). All the usual nice properties of limits of sequences are thus true:

\[ \lim(z_n + w_n) = \lim z_n + \lim w_n; \]

\[ \lim(z_n w_n) = (\lim z_n)(\lim w_n); \quad \text{and} \]

\[ \lim \frac{z_n}{w_n} = \frac{\lim z_n}{\lim w_n}, \]

provided that \( \lim z_n \) and \( \lim w_n \) exist. (And in the last equation, we must, of course, insist that \( \lim w_n \neq 0 \).)

容易看出,如果复序列 \( \{z_n\} = \{u_n + iv_n\} \) 收敛到 \( L \),那么两个实序列 \( \{u_n\} \) 和 \( \{v_n\} \) 各自有极限:\( \{u_n\} \) 收敛到 \( \operatorname{Re} L \),\( \{v_n\} \) 收敛到 \( \operatorname{Im} L \)。反之,如果两个实序列 \( \{u_n\} \) 和 \( \{v_n\} \) 各自有极限,那么复序列 \( \{u_n + iv_n\} \) 也有极限。序列极限的所有常见性质都成立: \[ \lim(z_n + w_n) = \lim z_n + \lim w_n, \] \[ \lim(z_n w_n) = (\lim z_n)(\lim w_n), \] \[ \lim \frac{z_n}{w_n} = \frac{\lim z_n}{\lim w_n}, \] 前提是 \( \lim z_n \) 和 \( \lim w_n \) 存在。(在最后一个等式中,当然还要求 \( \lim w_n \neq 0 \)。)

A necessary and sufficient condition for the convergence of a sequence \( \{a_n\} \) is the celebrated Cauchy criterion: given \( \varepsilon > 0 \), there is an integer \( N \) so that \( |a_n - a_m| < \varepsilon \) whenever \( n, m > N_\varepsilon \).

序列 \( \{a_n\} \) 收敛的充要条件是著名的柯西准则:对于给定的 \( \varepsilon > 0 \),存在整数 \( N \) 使得当 \( n, m > N_\varepsilon \) 时 \( |a_n - a_m| < \varepsilon \)。

Sequences of Functions

函数序列

A sequence \( \{f_n\} \) of functions on a domain \( D \) is the obvious thing: a function from the positive integers into the set of complex functions on \( D \). Thus, for each \( z \in D \), we have an ordinary sequence \( \{f_n(z)\} \). If each of the sequences \( \{f_n(z)\} \) converges, then we say the sequence of functions \( \{f_n\} \) converges to the function \( f \) defined by \( f(z) = \lim f_n(z) \). This is pretty obvious stuff.

定义域 \( D \) 上的函数序列 \( \{f_n\} \) 是显而易见的:从正整数到 \( D \) 上复函数集合的函数。因此,对于每个 \( z \in D \),我们有一个普通序列 \( \{f_n(z)\} \)。如果每个序列 \( \{f_n(z)\} \) 都收敛,那么我们说函数序列 \( \{f_n\} \) 收敛到由 \( f(z) = \lim f_n(z) \) 定义的函数 \( f \)。

The sequence \( \{f_n\} \) is said to converge to \( f \) uniformly on a set \( S \) if given an \( \varepsilon > 0 \), there is an integer \( N \) so that \( |f_n(z) - f(z)| < \varepsilon \) for all \( n > N_\varepsilon \) and all \( z \in S \).

如果对于给定的 \( \varepsilon > 0 \),存在整数 \( N \) 使得对所有 \( n > N_\varepsilon \) 和所有 \( z \in S \) 都有 \( |f_n(z) - f(z)| < \varepsilon \),则称序列 \( \{f_n\} \) 在集合 \( S \) 上一致收敛到 \( f \)。

Note that it is possible for a sequence of continuous functions to have a limit function that is not continuous. This cannot happen if the convergence is uniform. To see this, suppose the sequence \( \{f_n\} \) of continuous functions converges uniformly to \( f \) on a domain \( D \), let \( z_0 \in D \), and let \( \varepsilon > 0 \). We need to show there is a \( \delta \) so that \( |f(z_0) - f(z)| < \varepsilon \) whenever \( |z - z_0| < \delta \). Let's do it.

注意,连续函数的序列可能有一个不连续的极限函数。但如果收敛是一致的,这就不会发生。为了证明这一点,假设连续函数序列 \( \{f_n\} \) 在定义域 \( D \) 上一致收敛到 \( f \),设 \( z_0 \in D \),且 \( \varepsilon > 0 \)。我们需要证明存在 \( \delta \) 使得当 \( |z - z_0| < \delta \) 时 \( |f(z_0) - f(z)| < \varepsilon \)。

First, choose \( N \) so that \( |f_N(z) - f(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{3} \). We can do this because of the uniform convergence of the sequence \( \{f_n\} \). Next, choose \( \delta \) so that \( |f_N(z_0) - f_N(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{3} \) whenever \( |z - z_0| < \delta \). This is possible because \( f_N \) is continuous. Now then, when \( |z - z_0| < \delta \), we have

\[ |f(z_0) - f(z)| = |f(z_0) - f_N(z_0) + f_N(z_0) - f_N(z) + f_N(z) - f(z)| \]

\[ \leq |f(z_0) - f_N(z_0)| + |f_N(z_0) - f_N(z)| + |f_N(z) - f(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{3} + \frac{\varepsilon}{3} + \frac{\varepsilon}{3} = \varepsilon, \]

and we have done it!

首先,选择 \( N \) 使得 \( |f_N(z) - f(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{3} \)。这可以由序列 \( \{f_n\} \) 的一致收敛性保证。然后,选择 \( \delta \) 使得当 \( |z - z_0| < \delta \) 时 \( |f_N(z_0) - f_N(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{3} \)。这是可能的,因为 \( f_N \) 是连续的。于是当 \( |z - z_0| < \delta \) 时, \[ |f(z_0) - f(z)| \leq |f(z_0) - f_N(z_0)| + |f_N(z_0) - f_N(z)| + |f_N(z) - f(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{3} + \frac{\varepsilon}{3} + \frac{\varepsilon}{3} = \varepsilon. \]

Integration of Uniformly Convergent Sequences

一致收敛序列的积分

Now suppose we have a sequence \( \{f_n\} \) of continuous functions which converges uniformly on a contour \( C \) to the function \( f \). Then the sequence \( \left\{\int_C f_n(z)\,dz\right\} \) converges to \( \int_C f(z)\,dz \). This is easy to see. Let \( \varepsilon > 0 \). Now let \( N \) be so that \( |f_n(z) - f(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{A} \) for \( n > N \), where \( A \) is the length of \( C \). Then,

\[ \left|\int_C f_n(z)\,dz - \int_C f(z)\,dz\right| = \left|\int_C (f_n(z) - f(z))\,dz\right| \leq \frac{\varepsilon}{A} \cdot A = \varepsilon \]

whenever \( n > N \).

现在假设我们有一个连续函数序列 \( \{f_n\} \),它在曲线 \( C \) 上一致收敛到函数 \( f \)。那么序列 \( \left\{\int_C f_n(z)\,dz\right\} \) 收敛到 \( \int_C f(z)\,dz \)。设 \( \varepsilon > 0 \),选择 \( N \) 使得当 \( n > N \) 时 \( |f_n(z) - f(z)| < \frac{\varepsilon}{A} \),其中 \( A \) 是 \( C \) 的长度。则 \[ \left|\int_C f_n(z)\,dz - \int_C f(z)\,dz\right| \leq \frac{\varepsilon}{A} \cdot A = \varepsilon. \]

Uniform Limits of Analytic Functions

解析函数的一致极限

Now suppose \( \{f_n\} \) is a sequence of functions each analytic on some region \( D \), and suppose the sequence converges uniformly on \( D \) to the function \( f \). Then \( f \) is analytic. This result is in marked contrast to what happens with real functions -- examples of uniformly convergent sequences of differentiable functions with a nondifferentiable limit abound in the real case.

现在假设 \( \{f_n\} \) 是一个函数序列,每个函数在某区域 \( D \) 上解析,并且该序列在 \( D \) 上一致收敛到函数 \( f \)。那么 \( f \) 是解析的。这个结果与实函数的情形形成鲜明对比——在实数情形中,可微函数的一致收敛序列具有不可微极限的例子比比皆是。

To see that this uniform limit is analytic, let \( z_0 \in D \), and let \( S = \{z : |z - z_0| < r\} \subset D \). Now consider any simple closed curve \( C \subset S \). Each \( f_n \) is analytic, and so \( \int_C f_n(z)\,dz = 0 \) for every \( n \). From the uniform convergence of \( \{f_n\} \), we know that \( \int_C f(z)\,dz \) is the limit of the sequence \( \left\{\int_C f_n(z)\,dz\right\} \), and so \( \int_C f(z)\,dz = 0 \). Morera's theorem now tells us that \( f \) is analytic on \( S \), and hence at \( z_0 \). Truly a miracle.

为了证明这个一致极限是解析的,设 \( z_0 \in D \),令 \( S = \{z : |z - z_0| < r\} \subset D \)。考虑 \( S \) 中任何简单闭合曲线 \( C \)。每个 \( f_n \) 都是解析的,所以对每个 \( n \) 有 \( \int_C f_n(z)\,dz = 0 \)。由 \( \{f_n\} \) 的一致收敛性,我们知道 \( \int_C f(z)\,dz \) 是序列 \( \left\{\int_C f_n(z)\,dz\right\} \) 的极限,因此 \( \int_C f(z)\,dz = 0 \)。由莫雷拉定理可知 \( f \) 在 \( S \) 上解析,从而在 \( z_0 \) 处解析。真是奇迹。

8.2 Series

8.2 级数

A series is simply a sequence \( \{s_n\} \) in which \( s_n = a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n \). In other words, there is a sequence \( \{a_n\} \) so that \( s_n = s_{n-1} + a_n \). The \( s_n \) are usually called the partial sums. Recall from Mrs. Turner's class that if the series \( \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_j \) has a limit, then it must be true that \( \lim_{n \to \infty} a_n = 0 \).

级数就是一个序列 \( \{s_n\} \),其中 \( s_n = a_1 + a_2 + \cdots + a_n \)。换言之,存在序列 \( \{a_n\} \) 使得 \( s_n = s_{n-1} + a_n \)。\( s_n \) 通常称为部分和。回忆一下,如果级数 \( \sum_{j=1}^{n} a_j \) 有极限,那么必有 \( \lim_{n \to \infty} a_n = 0 \)。

Weierstrass M-test

魏尔斯特拉斯 M-判别法

Consider a series \( \sum_{j=1}^{n} f_j(z) \) of functions. Chances are this series will converge for some values of \( z \) and not converge for others. A useful result is the celebrated Weierstrass M-test:

考虑函数级数 \( \sum_{j=1}^{n} f_j(z) \)。这个级数可能对某些 \( z \) 值收敛,对其他值不收敛。一个有用的结果是著名的魏尔斯特拉斯 M-判别法:

Suppose \( \{M_j\} \) is a sequence of real numbers such that \( M_j \geq 0 \) for all \( j \geq J \), where \( J \) is some number, and suppose also that the series \( \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} M_j \) converges. If for all \( z \in D \), we have \( |f_j(z)| \leq M_j \) for all \( j \geq J \), then the series \( \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} f_j(z) \) converges uniformly on \( D \).

假设 \( \{M_j\} \) 是一个实数序列,对所有 \( j \geq J \)(其中 \( J \) 是某个数)有 \( M_j \geq 0 \),并且级数 \( \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} M_j \) 收敛。如果对所有 \( z \in D \),当 \( j \geq J \) 时有 \( |f_j(z)| \leq M_j \),那么级数 \( \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} f_j(z) \) 在 \( D \) 上一致收敛。

Proof: Begin by letting \( \varepsilon > 0 \) and choosing \( N \geq J \) so that

\[ \sum_{j=m}^{n} M_j < \varepsilon \]

for all \( n, m > N \). (We can do this because of the famous Cauchy criterion.) Next, observe that

\[ \left|\sum_{j=m}^{n} f_j(z)\right| \leq \sum_{j=m}^{n} |f_j(z)| \leq \sum_{j=m}^{n} M_j < \varepsilon. \]

This shows that \( \sum_{j=1}^{n} f_j(z) \) converges. To see the uniform convergence, observe that

\[ \left|\sum_{j=m}^{n} f_j(z)\right| = \left|\sum_{j=0}^{n} f_j(z) - \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} f_j(z)\right| < \varepsilon \]

for all \( z \in D \) and \( n > m > N \). Thus,

\[ \left|\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} f_j(z) - \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} f_j(z)\right| \leq \varepsilon \]

for \( m > N \). (The limit of a series \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} a_j \) is almost always written as \( \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} a_j \).)

证明:设 \( \varepsilon > 0 \),选择 \( N \geq J \) 使得当 \( n, m > N \) 时 \( \sum_{j=m}^{n} M_j < \varepsilon \)。(由柯西准则可以做到。)然后注意 \[ \left|\sum_{j=m}^{n} f_j(z)\right| \leq \sum_{j=m}^{n} |f_j(z)| \leq \sum_{j=m}^{n} M_j < \varepsilon. \] 这表明 \( \sum_{j=1}^{n} f_j(z) \) 收敛。对于一致收敛性,注意对所有 \( z \in D \) 和 \( n > m > N \),有 \[ \left|\sum_{j=0}^{\infty} f_j(z) - \sum_{j=0}^{m-1} f_j(z)\right| \leq \varepsilon. \]

8.3 Power Series

8.3 幂级数

We are particularly interested in series of functions in which the partial sums are polynomials of increasing degree:

\[ s_n(z) = c_0 + c_1(z - z_0) + c_2(z - z_0)^2 + \cdots + c_n(z - z_0)^n. \]

(We start with \( n = 0 \) for esthetic reasons.) These are the so-called power series. Thus, a power series is a series of functions of the form \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \).

我们特别感兴趣的是部分和为次数递增的多项式的函数级数: \[ s_n(z) = c_0 + c_1(z - z_0) + c_2(z - z_0)^2 + \cdots + c_n(z - z_0)^n. \] (出于美学原因,我们从 \( n = 0 \) 开始。)这些就是所谓的幂级数。因此,幂级数是形如 \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \) 的函数级数。

Geometric Series

几何级数

Let's look first at a very special power series, the so-called Geometric series:

\[ \sum_{j=0}^{n} z^j. \]

Here

\[ s_n = 1 + z + z^2 + \cdots + z^n, \quad \text{and} \]

\[ zs_n = z + z^2 + z^3 + \cdots + z^{n+1}. \]

Subtracting the second of these from the first gives us

\[ (1 - z)s_n = 1 - z^{n+1}. \]

If \( z = 1 \), then we can't go any further with this, but I hope it's clear that the series does not have a limit in case \( z = 1 \). Suppose now \( z \neq 1 \). Then we have

\[ s_n = \frac{1}{1 - z} - \frac{z^{n+1}}{1 - z}. \]

Now if \( |z| < 1 \), it should be clear that \( \lim z^{n+1} = 0 \), and so

\[ \lim \sum_{j=0}^{n} z^j = \lim s_n = \frac{1}{1 - z}. \]

Or,

\[ \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} z^j = \frac{1}{1 - z}, \quad \text{for } |z| < 1. \]

让我们先看一个非常特殊的幂级数,即所谓的几何级数: \[ \sum_{j=0}^{n} z^j. \] 其中 \( s_n = 1 + z + z^2 + \cdots + z^n \),而 \( zs_n = z + z^2 + \cdots + z^{n+1} \)。从第一个减去第二个得到 \( (1 - z)s_n = 1 - z^{n+1} \)。当 \( z = 1 \) 时,级数没有极限。当 \( z \neq 1 \) 且 \( |z| < 1 \) 时, \[ \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} z^j = \frac{1}{1 - z}. \]

There is a bit more to the story. First, note that if \( |z| \geq 1 \), then the Geometric series does not have a limit (why?). Next, note that if \( |z| \leq \rho < 1 \), then the Geometric series converges uniformly to \( \frac{1}{1-z} \). To see this, note that \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} \rho^j \) has a limit and appeal to the Weierstrass M-test.

还有更多内容。首先,注意如果 \( |z| \geq 1 \),则几何级数没有极限(为什么?)。其次,注意如果 \( |z| \leq \rho < 1 \),则几何级数一致收敛到 \( \frac{1}{1-z} \)。这可以通过注意 \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} \rho^j \) 有极限并运用魏尔斯特拉斯 M-判别法来看出。

Radius of Convergence

收敛半径

Clearly a power series will have a limit for some values of \( z \) and perhaps not for others. First, note that any power series has a limit when \( z = z_0 \). Let's see what else we can say.

显然,幂级数对某些 \( z \) 值有极限,而对其他值可能没有。首先,注意任何幂级数在 \( z = z_0 \) 时都有极限。让我们看看还能说些什么。

Consider a power series \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \). Let

\[ \alpha = \limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|}. \]

(Recall from 6th grade that \( \limsup\{a_k\} = \lim_{n \to \infty}\{\sup\{a_k : k \geq n\}\} \).) Now let \( R = \frac{1}{\alpha} \). (We shall say \( R = 0 \) if \( \alpha = \infty \), and \( R = \infty \) if \( \alpha = 0 \).) We are going to show that the series converges uniformly for all \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho < R \) and diverges for all \( |z - z_0| > R \).

考虑幂级数 \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \)。令 \[ \alpha = \limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|}. \] (回忆 \( \limsup\{a_k\} = \lim_{n \to \infty}\{\sup\{a_k : k \geq n\}\} \)。)令 \( R = \frac{1}{\alpha} \)。(如果 \( \alpha = \infty \) 则 \( R = 0 \),如果 \( \alpha = 0 \) 则 \( R = \infty \)。)我们将证明级数在所有 \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho < R \) 上一致收敛,在所有 \( |z - z_0| > R \) 上发散。

Divergence for \( |z - z_0| > R \): To begin, let \( k \) be so that

\[ \frac{1}{|z - z_0|} < k < \frac{1}{R} = \alpha. \]

There are an infinite number of \( c_j \) for which \( \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} > k \), otherwise \( \limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} \leq k \). For each of these \( c_j \) we have

\[ |c_j(z - z_0)^j| = \left(\sqrt[j]{|c_j|}\right)^j |z - z_0|^j > (k|z - z_0|)^j > 1. \]

It is thus not possible for \( \lim_{n \to \infty} |c_n(z - z_0)^n| = 0 \), and so the series does not converge.

当 \( |z - z_0| > R \) 时发散:选取 \( k \) 使得 \( \frac{1}{|z - z_0|} < k < \frac{1}{R} = \alpha \)。存在无穷多个 \( c_j \) 满足 \( \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} > k \),否则 \( \limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} \leq k \)。对每个这样的 \( c_j \),有 \[ |c_j(z - z_0)^j| > (k|z - z_0|)^j > 1. \] 因此 \( \lim_{n \to \infty} |c_n(z - z_0)^n| = 0 \) 不可能成立,所以级数不收敛。

Convergence for \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho < R \): Let \( k \) be so that

\[ \frac{1}{R} = \alpha < k < \frac{1}{\rho}. \]

Now, for \( j \) large enough, we have \( \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} < k \). Thus for \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho \), we have

\[ |c_j(z - z_0)^j| \leq \left(\sqrt[j]{|c_j|}\right)^j |z - z_0|^j < (k|z - z_0|)^j \leq (k\rho)^j. \]

The geometric series \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} (k\rho)^j \) converges because \( k\rho < 1 \), and the uniform convergence of \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \) follows from the M-test.

当 \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho < R \) 时收敛:选取 \( k \) 使得 \( \alpha < k < \frac{1}{\rho} \)。对足够大的 \( j \),有 \( \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} < k \)。因此当 \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho \) 时, \[ |c_j(z - z_0)^j| < (k\rho)^j. \] 几何级数 \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} (k\rho)^j \) 收敛(因为 \( k\rho < 1 \)),由 M-判别法可得 \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \) 的一致收敛性。

Example

Consider the series \( \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!} z^j \). Let's compute \( R = 1/\limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} \). Here \( c_j = 1/j! \), so we need \( \limsup \sqrt[j]{1/j!} = 1/\limsup \sqrt[j]{j!} \). Thus \( R = \limsup \sqrt[j]{j!} \).

Let \( K \) be any positive integer and choose an integer \( m \) large enough to ensure that \( 2^m \geq \frac{K^{2K}}{(2K)!} \). Now consider \( \frac{n!}{K^n} \), where \( n = 2K + m \):

\[ \frac{n!}{K^n} = \frac{(2K + m)!}{K^{2K+m}} \geq \frac{(2K+m)(2K+m-1)\cdots(2K+1)(2K)!}{K^m \cdot K^{2K}} \geq 2^m \cdot 1. \]

Thus \( \sqrt[n]{n!} \geq K \). Reflect on what we have just shown: given any number \( K \), there is a number \( n \) such that \( \sqrt[n]{n!} \) is bigger than it. In other words, \( R = \limsup \sqrt[j]{j!} = \infty \), and so the series \( \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!} z^j \) converges for all \( z \).

例子

考虑级数 \( \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!} z^j \)。我们来计算 \( R = 1/\limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|} = \limsup \sqrt[j]{j!} \)。设 \( K \) 为任意正整数。通过仔细估计 \( \frac{n!}{K^n} \)(取 \( n = 2K + m \)),可以证明对任何数 \( K \),存在 \( n \) 使得 \( \sqrt[n]{n!} \geq K \)。换言之,\( R = \limsup \sqrt[j]{j!} = \infty \),因此级数 \( \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!} z^j \) 对所有 \( z \) 收敛。

Let's summarize what we have. For any power series \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \), there is a number

\[ R = \frac{1}{\limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|}} \]

such that the series converges uniformly for \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho < R \) and does not converge for \( |z - z_0| > R \). (Note that we may have \( R = 0 \) or \( R = \infty \).) The number \( R \) is called the radius of convergence of the series, and the set \( |z - z_0| < R \) is called the circle of convergence. Observe also that the limit of a power series is a function analytic inside the circle of convergence (why?).

总结一下我们的结论。对于任何幂级数 \( \sum_{j=0}^{n} c_j(z - z_0)^j \),存在一个数 \[ R = \frac{1}{\limsup \sqrt[j]{|c_j|}} \] 使得级数在 \( |z - z_0| \leq \rho < R \) 上一致收敛,在 \( |z - z_0| > R \) 上不收敛。(注意可能 \( R = 0 \) 或 \( R = \infty \)。)数 \( R \) 称为级数的收敛半径,集合 \( |z - z_0| < R \) 称为收敛圆。还请注意,幂级数的极限是在收敛圆内部解析的函数(为什么?)。

8.4 Integration of Power Series

8.4 幂级数的积分

Inside the circle of convergence, the limit

\[ S(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j \]

is an analytic function. We shall show that this series may be integrated "term-by-term" -- that is, the integral of the limit is the limit of the integrals. Specifically, if \( C \) is any contour inside the circle of convergence, and the function \( g \) is continuous on \( C \), then

\[ \int_C g(z) S(z)\,dz = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j \int_C g(z)(z - z_0)^j\,dz. \]

在收敛圆内,极限 \[ S(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j \] 是一个解析函数。我们将证明这个级数可以"逐项积分"——即极限的积分等于积分的极限。具体地,如果 \( C \) 是收敛圆内的任何曲线,函数 \( g \) 在 \( C \) 上连续,则 \[ \int_C g(z) S(z)\,dz = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j \int_C g(z)(z - z_0)^j\,dz. \]

Proof: Let \( \varepsilon > 0 \). Let \( M \) be the maximum of \( |g(z)| \) on \( C \) and let \( L \) be the length of \( C \). Then there is an integer \( N \) so that

\[ \left|\sum_{j=n}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j\right| < \frac{\varepsilon}{ML} \]

for all \( n > N \). Thus,

\[ \left|\int_C g(z) \sum_{j=n}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j\,dz\right| \leq ML \cdot \frac{\varepsilon}{ML} = \varepsilon. \]

Hence,

\[ \int_C g(z) S(z)\,dz - \sum_{j=0}^{n-1} c_j \int_C g(z)(z - z_0)^j\,dz = \int_C g(z) \sum_{j=n}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j\,dz, \]

which has absolute value less than \( \varepsilon \), and we have shown what we promised.

证明:设 \( \varepsilon > 0 \)。设 \( M \) 为 \( |g(z)| \) 在 \( C \) 上的最大值,\( L \) 为 \( C \) 的长度。则存在整数 \( N \) 使得当 \( n > N \) 时 \[ \left|\sum_{j=n}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j\right| < \frac{\varepsilon}{ML}. \] 因此 \[ \left|\int_C g(z) \sum_{j=n}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j\,dz\right| \leq ML \cdot \frac{\varepsilon}{ML} = \varepsilon. \] 于是 \[ \left|\int_C g(z) S(z)\,dz - \sum_{j=0}^{n-1} c_j \int_C g(z)(z - z_0)^j\,dz\right| < \varepsilon, \] 这就证明了我们所承诺的。

8.5 Differentiation of Power Series

8.5 幂级数的微分

Again, let

\[ S(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j. \]

Now we are ready to show that inside the circle of convergence,

\[ S'(z) = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} jc_j(z - z_0)^{j-1}. \]

再次,令 \[ S(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j(z - z_0)^j. \] 现在我们准备证明在收敛圆内, \[ S'(z) = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} jc_j(z - z_0)^{j-1}. \]

Let \( z \) be a point inside the circle of convergence and let \( C \) be a positively oriented circle centered at \( z \) and inside the circle of convergence. Define

\[ g(s) = \frac{1}{2\pi i(s - z)^2}, \]

and apply the result of the previous section to conclude that

\[ \int_C g(s) S(s)\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j \int_C g(s)(s - z_0)^j\,ds, \]

or

\[ \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{S(s)}{(s - z)^2}\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{(s - z_0)^j}{(s - z)^2}\,ds. \]

Thus

\[ S'(z) = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} jc_j(z - z_0)^{j-1}, \]

as promised!

设 \( z \) 是收敛圆内的一个点,\( C \) 是以 \( z \) 为圆心的正向圆,且在收敛圆内。定义 \[ g(s) = \frac{1}{2\pi i(s - z)^2}, \] 应用上一节的结果可得 \[ \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{S(s)}{(s - z)^2}\,ds = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} c_j \frac{1}{2\pi i}\int_C \frac{(s - z_0)^j}{(s - z)^2}\,ds. \] 因此 \[ S'(z) = \sum_{j=1}^{\infty} jc_j(z - z_0)^{j-1}, \] 正如所承诺的!
