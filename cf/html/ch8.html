8.1 Laurent Series

8.1 洛朗级数

A Laurent series is a series of the form \( \sum_{n=-\infty}^\infty a_n(z-z_0)^n \). It represents functions in an annular region.

洛朗级数是形式为 \( \sum_{n=-\infty}^\infty a_n(z-z_0)^n \) 的级数。它表示环形区域中的函数。

8.2 Residues

8.2 留数

The residue of \( f \) at \( z_0 \) is the coefficient \( a_{-1} \) in the Laurent expansion.

\( f \) 在 \( z_0 \) 处的留数是洛朗展开中的系数 \( a_{-1} \)。

8.3 The Residue Theorem

8.3 留数定理

If \( C \) is a simple closed curve and \( f \) is analytic inside and on \( C \) except for isolated singularities \( z_1,\ldots,z_n \) inside \( C \), then \( \int_C f(z)\,dz = 2\pi i\sum_{k=1}^n \text{Res}(f,z_k) \).

如果 \( C \) 是简单闭合曲线,\( f \) 在 \( C \) 上及内部解析,除了 \( C \) 内的孤立奇点 \( z_1,\ldots,z_n \),那么 \( \int_C f(z)\,dz = 2\pi i\sum_{k=1}^n \text{Res}(f,z_k) \)。
