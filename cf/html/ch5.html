5.1 Homotopy（同伦）

同伦的定义与直观理解

Suppose \( D \) is a connected subset of the plane such that every point of \( D \) is an interior point—we call such a set a region—and let \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \) be oriented closed curves in \( D \). We say \( C_1 \) is homotopic to \( C_2 \) in \( D \) if there is a continuous function \( H: S \to D \), where \( S \) is the square \( S = \{(t,s) : 0 \leq s,t \leq 1\} \), such that \( H(t,0) \) describes \( C_1 \) and \( H(t,1) \) describes \( C_2 \), and for each fixed \( s \), the function \( H(t,s) \) describes a closed curve \( C_s \) in \( D \). The function \( H \) is called a homotopy between \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \). Note that if \( C_1 \) is homotopic to \( C_2 \) in \( D \), then \( C_2 \) is homotopic to \( C_1 \) in \( D \). Just observe that the function \( K(t,s) = H(t,1-s) \) is a homotopy.

假设 \( D \) 是平面的连通子集,使得 \( D \) 的每个点都是内点——我们称这样的集合为区域——设 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 是 \( D \) 中的定向闭合曲线。如果存在连续函数 \( H: S \to D \),其中 \( S \) 是正方形 \( S = \{(t,s) : 0 \leq s,t \leq 1\} \),使得 \( H(t,0) \) 描述 \( C_1 \),\( H(t,1) \) 描述 \( C_2 \),并且对于每个固定的 \( s \),函数 \( H(t,s) \) 描述 \( D \) 中的闭合曲线 \( C_s \),我们就说 \( C_1 \) 在 \( D \) 中同伦于 \( C_2 \)。函数 \( H \) 称为 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 之间的同伦。注意如果 \( C_1 \) 在 \( D \) 中同伦于 \( C_2 \),那么 \( C_2 \) 在 \( D \) 中同伦于 \( C_1 \)。只需观察函数 \( K(t,s) = H(t,1-s) \) 是一个同伦。

直观理解同伦

形象比喻：想象 \( C_1 \) 是一根橡皮筋的形状，\( C_2 \) 是另一个形状。如果你能在区域 \( D \) 内连续地变形这根橡皮筋从 \( C_1 \) 到 \( C_2 \)，而不离开 \( D \) 也不撕断它，那么 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 就是同伦的。

参数理解：

  • \( t \)：描述曲线上的位置（\( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \) 走一圈）
  • \( s \)：描述变形的进度（\( s=0 \) 是起始曲线，\( s=1 \) 是终止曲线）
  • \( H(t, s) \)：在变形进度 \( s \) 时，曲线上位置 \( t \) 的点的坐标

关键条件：

  1. 连续性：\( H \) 必须连续，不能突然"跳跃"
  2. 闭合性：对每个固定的 \( s \)，\( H(0, s) = H(1, s) \)（曲线是闭合的）
  3. 留在 \( D \) 内：整个变形过程中，\( H(t, s) \in D \) 对所有 \( t, s \)

It is convenient to consider a point to be a closed curve. The point \( c \) is described by a constant function \( \phi(t) = c \). We thus speak of a closed curve \( C \) being homotopic to a constant—we sometimes say \( C \) is contractible to a point.

Emotionally, the fact that two closed curves are homotopic in \( D \) means that one can be continuously deformed into the other in \( D \).

方便起见,我们认为一个点是一条闭合曲线。点 \( c \) 由常函数 \( \phi(t) = c \) 描述。因此我们说闭合曲线 \( C \) 同伦于常数——有时我们说 \( C \) 可收缩到一个点。

从直观上讲,两条闭合曲线在 \( D \) 中同伦意味着一条可以在 \( D \) 中连续变形为另一条。

Example 1: Annular Region（环形区域中的同伦）

Let \( D \) be the annular region \( D = \{z : 1 < |z| < 5\} \). Suppose \( C_1 \) is the circle described by \( \phi_1(t) = 2e^{i2\pi t} \), \( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \); and \( C_2 \) is the circle described by \( \phi_2(t) = 4e^{i2\pi t} \), \( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \). Then \( H(t,s) = (2+2s)e^{i2\pi t} \) is a homotopy in \( D \) between \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \).

验证这是一个同伦：

  1. 起点和终点：
    • \( H(t, 0) = 2e^{i2\pi t} = \phi_1(t) \)（描述 \( C_1 \)）
    • \( H(t, 1) = 4e^{i2\pi t} = \phi_2(t) \)（描述 \( C_2 \)）
  2. 中间状态：对于 \( 0 < s < 1 \)，\( H(t, s) = (2+2s)e^{i2\pi t} \) 描述半径为 \( 2+2s \) 的圆（在 \( 2 \) 到 \( 4 \) 之间）
  3. 留在 \( D \) 内：对所有 \( 0 \leq s \leq 1 \)，半径 \( 2+2s \in [2, 4] \subset (1, 5) \)，所以 \( H(t,s) \in D \) ✓
  4. 闭合性：\( H(0, s) = (2+2s)e^0 = 2+2s = (2+2s)e^{i2\pi} = H(1, s) \) ✓

几何图像：这个同伦就像一个气球在慢慢充气——从半径 2 的圆逐渐扩大到半径 4 的圆，整个过程中都保持在环形区域内。

方向的重要性：Suppose \( C_3 \) is the same circle as \( C_2 \) but with the opposite orientation; that is, a description is given by \( \phi_3(t) = 4e^{-i2\pi t} \), \( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \). A homotopy between \( C_1 \) and \( C_3 \) is not too easy to construct—in fact, it is not possible! The moral: orientation counts. From now on, the term "closed curve" will mean an oriented closed curve.

为什么 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_3 \) 不同伦？

  • \( C_1 \)：逆时针绕原点一圈（正方向）
  • \( C_3 \)：顺时针绕原点一圈（负方向）
  • 在环形区域 \( D \)（中心有"洞"）中，无法在不穿过洞的情况下将逆时针的圈变成顺时针的圈
  • 直观理解：像莫比乌斯带一样，改变方向需要"翻转"，而在环形区域中这是不可能的

关键洞察：同伦不仅关心曲线的形状，也关心方向。两条相同形状但方向相反的曲线在有洞的区域中一般不同伦。

Figure: Homotopy between circles in an annular region
例子

设 \( D \) 是环形区域 \( D = \{z : 1 < |z| < 5\} \)。假设 \( C_1 \) 是由 \( \phi_1(t) = 2e^{i2\pi t} \),\( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \) 描述的圆;\( C_2 \) 是由 \( \phi_2(t) = 4e^{i2\pi t} \),\( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \) 描述的圆。那么 \( H(t,s) = (2+2s)e^{i2\pi t} \) 是 \( D \) 中 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 之间的同伦。假设 \( C_3 \) 是与 \( C_2 \) 相同的圆但方向相反;也就是说,由 \( \phi_3(t) = 4e^{-i2\pi t} \),\( 0 \leq t \leq 1 \) 给出描述。\( C_1 \) 和 \( C_3 \) 之间的同伦不太容易构造——事实上,这是不可能的!寓意:方向很重要。从现在开始,术语"闭合曲线"将意味着定向闭合曲线。

5.2 Cauchy's Theorem

Suppose \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \) are closed curves in a region \( D \) that are homotopic in \( D \), and suppose \( f \) is a function analytic on \( D \). Let \( H(t,s) \) be a homotopy between \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \). For each \( s \), the function \( \phi_s(t) \) describes a closed curve \( C_s \) in \( D \). Let \( I(s) \) be given by

\[ I(s) = \int_{C_s} f(z)\,dz. \]

Then,

\[ I(s) = \int_0^1 f(H(t,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial t}(t,s)\,dt. \]

Now let's look at the derivative of \( I(s) \). We assume everything is nice enough to allow us to differentiate under the integral:

\[ I'(s) = \int_0^1 \frac{d}{ds}\left[f(H(t,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial t}(t,s)\right]\,dt \]

\[ = \int_0^1 \left[f'(H(t,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(t,s)\frac{\partial H}{\partial t}(t,s) + f(H(t,s))\frac{\partial^2 H}{\partial s\partial t}(t,s)\right]\,dt \]

\[ = \int_0^1 \left[f'(H(t,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(t,s)\frac{\partial H}{\partial t}(t,s) + f(H(t,s))\frac{\partial^2 H}{\partial t\partial s}(t,s)\right]\,dt \]

\[ = \int_0^1 \frac{\partial}{\partial t}\left[f(H(t,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(t,s)\right]\,dt \]

\[ = f(H(1,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(1,s) - f(H(0,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(0,s). \]

But we know each \( H(t,s) \) describes a closed curve, and so \( H(0,s) = H(1,s) \) for all \( s \). Thus,

\[ I'(s) = f(H(1,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(1,s) - f(H(0,s))\frac{\partial H}{\partial s}(0,s) = 0, \]

which means \( I(s) \) is constant! In particular, \( I(0) = I(1) \), or

\[ \int_{C_1} f(z)\,dz = \int_{C_2} f(z)\,dz. \]

This is a big deal. We have shown that if \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \) are closed curves in a region \( D \) that are homotopic in \( D \), and \( f \) is analytic on \( D \), then \( \int_{C_1} f(z)\,dz = \int_{C_2} f(z)\,dz \).

假设 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 是区域 \( D \) 中在 \( D \) 中同伦的闭合曲线,并且假设 \( f \) 是 \( D \) 上的解析函数。设 \( H(t,s) \) 是 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 之间的同伦。对于每个 \( s \),函数 \( \phi_s(t) \) 描述 \( D \) 中的闭合曲线 \( C_s \)。经过推导,我们证明了 \( I(s) \) 是常数!特别地,\( I(0) = I(1) \),或 \[ \int_{C_1} f(z)\,dz = \int_{C_2} f(z)\,dz. \] 这是一件大事。我们已经证明,如果 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 是区域 \( D \) 中在 \( D \) 中同伦的闭合曲线,并且 \( f \) 在 \( D \) 上解析,那么 \( \int_{C_1} f(z)\,dz = \int_{C_2} f(z)\,dz \)。

An easy corollary of this result is the celebrated Cauchy's Theorem, which says that if \( f \) is analytic on a simply connected region \( D \), then for any closed curve \( C \) in \( D \),

\[ \int_C f(z)\,dz = 0. \]

In court testimony, one is admonished to tell the truth, the whole truth, and nothing but the truth. Well, so far in this chapter, we have told the truth and nothing but the truth, but we have not quite told the whole truth. We assumed all sorts of continuous derivatives in the preceding discussion. These are not always necessary—specifically, the results can be proved true without all our smoothness assumptions—think about approximation.

这个结果的一个简单推论是著名的柯西定理,它说如果 \( f \) 在单连通区域 \( D \) 上解析,那么对于 \( D \) 中的任何闭合曲线 \( C \),\[ \int_C f(z)\,dz = 0. \] 在法庭证词中,人们被告诫要说真话,全部真话,只说真话。嗯,到目前为止在本章中,我们说了真话,只说了真话,但我们还没有完全说出全部真话。我们在前面的讨论中假设了各种连续导数。这些并不总是必要的——具体来说,结果可以在没有我们所有平滑性假设的情况下被证明为真——想想逼近。

Example

Look at the picture below and convince yourself that the path \( C \) is homotopic to the closed path consisting of the two curves \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \) together with the line \( L \). We traverse the line twice, once from \( C_1 \) to \( C_2 \) and once from \( C_2 \) to \( C_1 \).

Observe then that an integral over this closed path is simply the sum of the integrals over \( C_1 \) and \( C_2 \), since the two integrals along \( L \), being in opposite directions, would sum to zero. Thus, if \( f \) is analytic in the region bounded by these curves (the region with two holes in it), then we know that

\[ \int_C f(z)\,dz = \int_{C_1} f(z)\,dz + \int_{C_2} f(z)\,dz. \]

Figure: Path deformation in a region with holes
例子

看下面的图片并说服自己,路径 \( C \) 同伦于由两条曲线 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 以及直线 \( L \) 组成的闭合路径。我们沿直线走两次,一次从 \( C_1 \) 到 \( C_2 \),一次从 \( C_2 \) 到 \( C_1 \)。然后观察到这条闭合路径上的积分只是 \( C_1 \) 和 \( C_2 \) 上积分的和,因为沿 \( L \) 的两个积分方向相反,会相加为零。因此,如果 \( f \) 在这些曲线包围的区域(有两个洞的区域)中解析,那么我们知道 \[ \int_C f(z)\,dz = \int_{C_1} f(z)\,dz + \int_{C_2} f(z)\,dz. \]
