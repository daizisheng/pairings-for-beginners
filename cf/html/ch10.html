10.1 Conformal Mappings

10.1 保角映射

An analytic function \( f \) with \( f'(z_0) \neq 0 \) preserves angles at \( z_0 \). Such mappings are called conformal.

解析函数 \( f \) 若 \( f'(z_0) \neq 0 \) 则在 \( z_0 \) 处保持角度。这样的映射称为保角映射。

10.2 Linear Fractional Transformations

10.2 线性分式变换

Linear fractional transformations have the form \( f(z) = \frac{az+b}{cz+d} \) where \( ad-bc \neq 0 \). They map circles and lines to circles and lines.

线性分式变换的形式为 \( f(z) = \frac{az+b}{cz+d} \),其中 \( ad-bc \neq 0 \)。它们将圆和直线映射到圆和直线。
