10.1 Residues

10.1 留数

A point \( z_0 \) is a singular point of a function \( f \) if \( f \) is not analytic at \( z_0 \), but is analytic at some point of each neighborhood of \( z_0 \). A singular point \( z_0 \) of \( f \) is said to be isolated if there is a neighborhood of \( z_0 \) which contains no singular points of \( f \) save \( z_0 \) itself. In other words, \( f \) is analytic on some region \( 0 < |z - z_0| < \delta \).

如果函数 \( f \) 在点 \( z_0 \) 不解析,但在 \( z_0 \) 的每个邻域中的某个点处解析,则称 \( z_0 \) 是 \( f \) 的奇点。如果 \( z_0 \) 存在一个邻域,其中除 \( z_0 \) 本身外不包含 \( f \) 的其他奇点,则称奇点 \( z_0 \) 是孤立的。换句话说,\( f \) 在某个区域 \( 0 < |z - z_0| < \delta \) 上解析。

Examples

例子

The function \( f \) given by \[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z(z^2 + 4)} \] has isolated singular points at \( z = 0 \), \( z = 2i \), and \( z = -2i \).

函数 \( f(z) = \frac{1}{z(z^2 + 4)} \) 有孤立奇点 \( z = 0 \)、\( z = 2i \) 和 \( z = -2i \)。

Every point on the negative real axis and the origin is a singular point of \( \operatorname{Log} z \), but there are no isolated singular points.

\( \operatorname{Log}\, z \) 的每个负实轴上的点和原点都是奇点,但没有孤立奇点。

Suppose now that \( z_0 \) is an isolated singular point of \( f \). Then there is a Laurent series \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=-\infty}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j \] valid for \( 0 < |z - z_0| < R \), for some positive \( R \). The coefficient \( c_{-1} \) of \( (z - z_0)^{-1} \) is called the residue of \( f \) at \( z_0 \), and is frequently written \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_0} f. \]

现在假设 \( z_0 \) 是 \( f \) 的一个孤立奇点。那么存在洛朗级数 \[ f(z) = \sum_{j=-\infty}^{\infty} c_j (z - z_0)^j \] 在 \( 0 < |z - z_0| < R \) 上有效,其中 \( R \) 为某个正数。\( (z - z_0)^{-1} \) 的系数 \( c_{-1} \) 称为 \( f \) 在 \( z_0 \) 处的留数,通常记作 \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_0} f. \]

Now, why do we care enough about \( c_{-1} \) to give it a special name? Well, observe that if \( C \) is any positively oriented simple closed curve in \( 0 < |z - z_0| < R \) which contains \( z_0 \) inside, then \[ c_{-1} = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \oint_C f(z)\,dz. \]

那么,为什么我们要特别关注 \( c_{-1} \) 并给它一个专门的名字呢?请注意,如果 \( C \) 是 \( 0 < |z - z_0| < R \) 中任何包含 \( z_0 \) 在内的正向简单闭合曲线,那么 \[ c_{-1} = \frac{1}{2\pi i} \oint_C f(z)\,dz. \]

This provides the key to evaluating many complex integrals.

这为计算许多复积分提供了关键。

Example

We shall evaluate the integral \[ \oint_C e^{1/z}\,dz \] where \( C \) is the circle \( |z| = 1 \) with the usual positive orientation. Observe that the integrand has an isolated singularity at \( z = 0 \). We know then that the value of the integral is simply \( 2\pi i \) times the residue of \( e^{1/z} \) at 0. Let's find the Laurent series about 0. We already know that \[ e^z = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!} z^j \] for all \( z \). Thus, \[ e^{1/z} = \sum_{j=0}^{\infty} \frac{1}{j!} z^{-j} = 1 + \frac{1}{z} + \frac{1}{2!}\frac{1}{z^2} + \cdots \] The residue \( c_{-1} = 1 \), and so the value of the integral is simply \( 2\pi i \).

例子

我们来计算积分 \( \oint_C e^{1/z}\,dz \),其中 \( C \) 是正向圆 \( |z| = 1 \)。被积函数在 \( z = 0 \) 处有孤立奇点。积分的值就是 \( 2\pi i \) 乘以 \( e^{1/z} \) 在 0 处的留数。利用 \( e^z \) 的级数展开, \[ e^{1/z} = 1 + \frac{1}{z} + \frac{1}{2!z^2} + \cdots \] 留数 \( c_{-1} = 1 \),因此积分值为 \( 2\pi i \)。

Now suppose we have a function \( f \) which is analytic everywhere except for isolated singularities, and let \( C \) be a simple closed curve (positively oriented) on which \( f \) is analytic. Then there will be only a finite number of singularities of \( f \) inside \( C \) (why?). Call them \( z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_n \). For each \( k = 1, 2, \ldots, n \), let \( C_k \) be a positively oriented circle centered at \( z_k \) and with radius small enough to ensure that it is inside \( C \) and has no other singular points inside it.

现在假设函数 \( f \) 除孤立奇点外处处解析,设 \( C \) 是一条正向简单闭合曲线,\( f \) 在其上解析。那么 \( C \) 内只有有限个 \( f \) 的奇点(为什么?)。记它们为 \( z_1, z_2, \ldots, z_n \)。对每个 \( k = 1, 2, \ldots, n \),设 \( C_k \) 是以 \( z_k \) 为圆心的正向圆,半径足够小,使其在 \( C \) 内且内部没有其他奇点。

Figure: Contour enclosing singularities for the Residue Theorem

图:留数定理中包围奇点的轮廓

Then, \[ \oint_C f(z)\,dz = \oint_{C_1} f(z)\,dz + \oint_{C_2} f(z)\,dz + \cdots + \oint_{C_n} f(z)\,dz \] \[ = 2\pi i \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_1} f + 2\pi i \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_2} f + \cdots + 2\pi i \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_n} f = 2\pi i \sum_{k=1}^{n} \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_k} f. \]

那么, \[ \oint_C f(z)\,dz = 2\pi i \sum_{k=1}^{n} \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_k} f. \]

This is the celebrated Residue Theorem. It says that the integral of \( f \) is simply \( 2\pi i \) times the sum of the residues at the singular points enclosed by the contour \( C \).

这就是著名的留数定理。它说 \( f \) 的积分就是 \( 2\pi i \) 乘以轮廓 \( C \) 所围奇点处留数之和。

10.2 Poles and Other Singularities

10.2 极点和其他奇点

In order for the Residue Theorem to be of much help in evaluating integrals, there needs to be some better way of computing the residue -- finding the Laurent expansion about each isolated singular point is a chore. We shall now see that in the case of a special but commonly occurring type of singularity the residue is easy to find.

为了使留数定理在计算积分时真正有用,需要有更好的方法来计算留数——求每个孤立奇点处的洛朗展开是一件麻烦事。我们现在将看到,对于一种特殊但常见的奇点类型,留数很容易求得。

Suppose \( z_0 \) is an isolated singularity of \( f \) and suppose that the Laurent series of \( f \) at \( z_0 \) contains only a finite number of terms involving negative powers of \( (z - z_0) \). Thus, \[ f(z) = \frac{c_{-n}}{(z-z_0)^n} + \frac{c_{-n+1}}{(z-z_0)^{n-1}} + \cdots + \frac{c_{-1}}{z-z_0} + c_0 + c_1(z-z_0) + \cdots \] Multiply this expression by \( (z - z_0)^n \): \[ (z-z_0)^n f(z) = c_{-n} + c_{-n+1}(z-z_0) + \cdots + c_{-1}(z-z_0)^{n-1} + \cdots \] What we see is the Taylor series at \( z_0 \) for the function \( \varphi(z) = (z-z_0)^n f(z) \). The coefficient of \( (z-z_0)^{n-1} \) is what we seek, and we know that this is \[ \frac{\varphi^{(n-1)}(z_0)}{(n-1)!}. \]

假设 \( z_0 \) 是 \( f \) 的一个孤立奇点,并且 \( f \) 在 \( z_0 \) 处的洛朗级数只包含有限个关于 \( (z - z_0) \) 的负幂项。即 \[ f(z) = \frac{c_{-n}}{(z-z_0)^n} + \frac{c_{-n+1}}{(z-z_0)^{n-1}} + \cdots + \frac{c_{-1}}{z-z_0} + c_0 + c_1(z-z_0) + \cdots \] 将此表达式乘以 \( (z - z_0)^n \),得到的就是函数 \( \varphi(z) = (z-z_0)^n f(z) \) 在 \( z_0 \) 处的泰勒级数。\( (z-z_0)^{n-1} \) 的系数正是我们要求的,即 \( \frac{\varphi^{(n-1)}(z_0)}{(n-1)!} \)。

The sought-after residue \( c_{-1} \) is thus \[ c_{-1} = \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_0} f = \frac{\varphi^{(n-1)}(z_0)}{(n-1)!}, \] where \( \varphi(z) = (z - z_0)^n f(z) \).

因此,所求的留数 \( c_{-1} \) 为 \[ c_{-1} = \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_0} f = \frac{\varphi^{(n-1)}(z_0)}{(n-1)!}, \] 其中 \( \varphi(z) = (z - z_0)^n f(z) \)。

Example

We shall find all the residues of the function \[ f(z) = \frac{e^z}{z^2(z^2+1)}. \] First, observe that \( f \) has isolated singularities at \( 0 \), \( i \), and \( -i \).

Let's see about the residue at 0. Here we have \[ \varphi(z) = z^2 f(z) = \frac{e^z}{z^2+1}. \] The residue is simply \( \varphi'(0) \): \[ \varphi'(z) = \frac{(z^2+1)e^z - 2z e^z}{(z^2+1)^2}. \] Hence, \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=0} f = \varphi'(0) = 1. \]

Next, let's see what we have at \( z = i \): \[ \varphi(z) = (z-i)f(z) = \frac{e^z}{z^2(z+i)}, \] and so \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=i} f = \varphi(i) = \frac{e^i}{i^2 \cdot 2i} = \frac{e^i}{-2i}. \] In the same way, we see that \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=-i} f = \frac{e^{-i}}{2i}. \]

例子

我们来求函数 \( f(z) = \frac{e^z}{z^2(z^2+1)} \) 的所有留数。\( f \) 在 \( 0 \)、\( i \) 和 \( -i \) 处有孤立奇点。在 \( z = 0 \) 处,令 \( \varphi(z) = z^2 f(z) = \frac{e^z}{z^2+1} \),留数为 \( \varphi'(0) = 1 \)。在 \( z = i \) 处,\( \operatorname*{Res}_{z=i} f = \frac{e^i}{-2i} \)。类似地,\( \operatorname*{Res}_{z=-i} f = \frac{e^{-i}}{2i} \)。

Figure: Contour for the integral of \( e^z / [z^2(z^2+1)] \)

图:\( e^z / [z^2(z^2+1)] \) 积分的轮廓

Example (continued)

Let's find the integral \( \displaystyle\oint_C \frac{e^z}{z^2(z^2+1)}\,dz \), where \( C \) is the contour pictured above.

This is now easy. The contour is positively oriented and encloses two singularities of \( f \); viz., \( i \) and \( -i \). Hence, \[ \oint_C \frac{e^z}{z^2(z^2+1)}\,dz = 2\pi i\left(\operatorname*{Res}_{z=i} f + \operatorname*{Res}_{z=-i} f\right) = 2\pi i\left(\frac{e^i}{-2i} + \frac{e^{-i}}{2i}\right) = -2\pi \sin 1. \] Miraculously easy!

例子(续)

我们来求积分 \( \oint_C \frac{e^z}{z^2(z^2+1)}\,dz \),其中 \( C \) 是上图所示的轮廓。轮廓正向并围住了 \( f \) 的两个奇点 \( i \) 和 \( -i \)。因此, \[ \oint_C \frac{e^z}{z^2(z^2+1)}\,dz = 2\pi i\left(\frac{e^i}{-2i} + \frac{e^{-i}}{2i}\right) = -2\pi \sin 1. \] 奇迹般地简单!

There is some jargon that goes with all this. An isolated singular point \( z_0 \) of \( f \) such that the Laurent series at \( z_0 \) includes only a finite number of terms involving negative powers of \( (z - z_0) \) is called a pole. Thus, if \( z_0 \) is a pole, there is an integer \( n \) so that \( \varphi(z) = (z - z_0)^n f(z) \) is analytic at \( z_0 \), and \( \varphi(z_0) \neq 0 \). The number \( n \) is called the order of the pole. Thus, in the preceding example, 0 is a pole of order 2, while \( i \) and \( -i \) are poles of order 1. (A pole of order 1 is frequently called a simple pole.)

这里有一些相关的术语。如果 \( f \) 的孤立奇点 \( z_0 \) 处的洛朗级数只包含有限个关于 \( (z - z_0) \) 的负幂项,则称 \( z_0 \) 为极点。即存在整数 \( n \) 使得 \( \varphi(z) = (z - z_0)^n f(z) \) 在 \( z_0 \) 处解析且 \( \varphi(z_0) \neq 0 \)。数 \( n \) 称为极点的阶。在上例中,0 是二阶极点,而 \( i \) 和 \( -i \) 是一阶极点(即简单极点)。

We must hedge just a bit here. If \( z_0 \) is an isolated singularity of \( f \) and there are no Laurent series terms involving negative powers of \( (z - z_0) \), then we say \( z_0 \) is a removable singularity.

这里要稍加说明。如果 \( z_0 \) 是 \( f \) 的孤立奇点,并且洛朗级数中没有关于 \( (z - z_0) \) 的负幂项,那么我们说 \( z_0 \) 是可去奇点。

Example

Let \[ f(z) = \frac{\sin z}{z}; \] then the singularity \( z = 0 \) is a removable singularity: \[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z}\sin z = \frac{1}{z}\left(z - \frac{z^3}{3!} + \frac{z^5}{5!} - \cdots\right) = 1 - \frac{z^2}{3!} + \frac{z^4}{5!} - \cdots \] and we see that in some sense \( f \) is "really" analytic at \( z = 0 \) if we would just define it to be the right thing there.

例子

设 \( f(z) = \frac{\sin z}{z} \),则奇点 \( z = 0 \) 是可去奇点: \[ f(z) = \frac{1}{z}\left(z - \frac{z^3}{3!} + \frac{z^5}{5!} - \cdots\right) = 1 - \frac{z^2}{3!} + \frac{z^4}{5!} - \cdots \] 可以看出,如果在 \( z = 0 \) 处适当定义函数值,\( f \) 在该点实际上是解析的。

A singularity that is neither a pole nor removable is called an essential singularity.

既不是极点也不是可去奇点的奇点称为本性奇点。

Let's look at one more labor-saving trick -- or technique, if you prefer. Suppose \( f \) is a function: \[ f(z) = \frac{p(z)}{q(z)}, \] where \( p \) and \( q \) are analytic at \( z_0 \), and we have \( q(z_0) = 0 \), while \( q'(z_0) \neq 0 \), and \( p(z_0) \neq 0 \). Then \[ f(z) = \frac{p(z)}{q(z)} = \frac{p(z_0) + p'(z_0)(z - z_0) + \cdots}{\frac{q'(z_0)}{1!}(z-z_0) + \frac{q''(z_0)}{2!}(z-z_0)^2 + \cdots}, \] and so \[ (z-z_0)f(z) = \frac{p(z_0) + p'(z_0)(z-z_0) + \cdots}{q'(z_0) + \frac{q''(z_0)}{2}(z-z_0) + \cdots}. \] Thus \( z_0 \) is a simple pole and \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_0} f = \frac{p(z_0)}{q'(z_0)}. \]

我们来看一个省力的技巧。设 \( f(z) = \frac{p(z)}{q(z)} \),其中 \( p \) 和 \( q \) 在 \( z_0 \) 处解析,且 \( q(z_0) = 0 \)、\( q'(z_0) \neq 0 \)、\( p(z_0) \neq 0 \)。那么 \( z_0 \) 是简单极点,且 \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=z_0} f = \frac{p(z_0)}{q'(z_0)}. \]

Example

Find the integral \[ \oint_C \frac{\cos z}{e^z - 1}\,dz, \] where \( C \) is the rectangle with sides \( x = -1 \), \( x = 1 \), \( y = -\pi \), and \( y = 3\pi \).

The singularities of the integrand are all the places at which \( e^z = 1 \), or in other words, the points \( z = 0, \pm 2\pi i, \pm 4\pi i, \ldots \). The singularities enclosed by \( C \) are \( 0 \) and \( 2\pi i \). Thus, \[ \oint_C \frac{\cos z}{e^z - 1}\,dz = 2\pi i\left(\operatorname*{Res}_{z=0} f + \operatorname*{Res}_{z=2\pi i} f\right), \] where \( f(z) = \frac{\cos z}{e^z - 1} \).

Observe this is precisely the situation just discussed: \( f(z) = \frac{p(z)}{q(z)} \), where \( p \) and \( q \) are analytic, etc. Now, \[ \frac{p(z)}{q'(z)} = \frac{\cos z}{e^z}. \] Thus, \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=0} f = \frac{\cos 0}{e^0} = 1, \] and \[ \operatorname*{Res}_{z=2\pi i} f = \frac{\cos(2\pi i)}{e^{2\pi i}} = \frac{e^{2\pi} + e^{-2\pi}}{2} = \cosh(2\pi). \] Finally, \[ \oint_C \frac{\cos z}{e^z - 1}\,dz = 2\pi i\left(1 + \cosh(2\pi)\right). \]

例子

求积分 \( \oint_C \frac{\cos z}{e^z - 1}\,dz \),其中 \( C \) 是边为 \( x = -1 \)、\( x = 1 \)、\( y = -\pi \)、\( y = 3\pi \) 的矩形。被积函数的奇点是 \( e^z = 1 \) 的所有点,即 \( z = 0, \pm 2\pi i, \pm 4\pi i, \ldots \)。\( C \) 围住的奇点为 \( 0 \) 和 \( 2\pi i \)。利用 \( p(z)/q'(z) = \cos z / e^z \) 的公式,得 \( \operatorname*{Res}_{z=0} f = 1 \),\( \operatorname*{Res}_{z=2\pi i} f = \cosh(2\pi) \)。最终, \[ \oint_C \frac{\cos z}{e^z - 1}\,dz = 2\pi i\left(1 + \cosh(2\pi)\right). \]
